**Q7:** According to cognitive dissonance theory, people experience discomfort when:

A. Their attitudes match their behaviors  
B. They receive positive feedback  
C. Two or more attitudes are inconsistent, or attitude and behavior are inconsistent  
D. They work in a supportive environment  

<details>
<summary>查看答案 / Answer</summary>

**Answer:** C

**解析:** 认知失调理论（Festinger, 1957）指出，当两种或多种态度不一致，或态度与行为不一致时，人们会感到不适，并试图减少这种失调。

</details>
