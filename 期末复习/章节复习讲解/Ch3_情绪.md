## 第一部分：理论基础 / Theory Foundations

### 一、情绪与心境的定义与区别 / Emotions vs. Moods

#### 1.1 情绪（Emotions）

Emotions are intense feelings that are directed at someone or something. They are short-lived and have a clear cause.

情绪是针对特定对象或事件的强烈感受，持续时间较短（通常几秒到几分钟），且有明确的触发原因。

**特征 / Characteristics:**
- Caused by specific events (有明确原因)
- Short-lived (持续时间短)
- Accompanied by facial expressions and behavioral reactions (伴随面部表情和行为反应)
- Include action tendencies (包含行动倾向)

#### 1.2 心境（Moods）

Moods are feelings that tend to be less intense than emotions and often lack a contextual stimulus. They last longer, for hours or days.

心境是较微弱、持续时间较长的情感状态，通常没有明确的触发事件，可持续数小时到数天。

**特征 / Characteristics:**
- No clear triggering event (没有明确的触发事件)
- Long-lasting (持续时间长，数小时到数天)
- Lower intensity (强度较低)
- More like a "background feeling" (更像是一种"背景感受")

#### 1.3 情感（Affect）

Affect is a broad range of feelings that includes both emotions and moods.

情感是一个广义术语，涵盖情绪和心境两个概念。

> **Key Distinction**: Emotions have a clear target, moods do not; emotions are intense but brief, moods are mild but lasting.  
> **关键区别**：情绪有明确对象，心境没有；情绪强烈但短暂，心境微弱但持久。

***

### 二、影响情绪与心境的因素 / What Influences Emotions and Moods

#### 2.1 时间 / Time

- **Time of day**: Most people are in a better mood by mid-morning and later in the day.
  一天中的时间：多数人在上午中段情绪最好。
- **Day of the week**: People are generally in a better mood toward the weekend.
  一周中的时间：多数人在接近周末时情绪最好。

#### 2.2 事件 / Events

Negative events at work can trigger negative emotions, but positive events can also boost mood.

工作中的负面事件会引发消极情绪，但正面事件也能提升情绪。

#### 2.3 睡眠 / Sleep

Sleep quality affects emotional well-being more than sleep quantity. Poor sleep increases negative affect.

睡眠质量比睡眠时长更能影响情绪健康。睡眠不足会增加负面情绪。

#### 2.4 运动 / Exercise

Regular exercise can improve mood and reduce negative emotions.

规律运动可以改善情绪并减少负面情绪。

#### 2.5 天气 / Weather

The impact of weather on mood is often overestimated. People tend to think sunny days make them happier, but research shows minimal effect.

天气对情绪的影响常被高估。人们认为晴天更快乐，但研究显示影响很小。

#### 2.6 年龄 / Age

As people age, they tend to experience fewer negative emotions.

随着年龄增长，人们倾向于体验更少的负面情绪。

#### 2.7 性别 / Gender

Women show more emotional expression than men, but there are few differences in actual emotional experience.

女性比男性表现出更多情绪表达，但在实际情绪体验上差异不大。

#### 2.8 人格 / Personality

Personality traits influence emotional baseline. People high in neuroticism experience more negative emotions.

人格特质影响情绪基线。高神经质者更容易体验负面情绪。

***

### 三、情绪劳动 / Emotional Labor

#### 3.1 定义 / Definition

Emotional labor is an employee's expression of organizationally desired emotions during interpersonal transactions at work.

情绪劳动是员工在人际互动中表现出组织期望的情绪。

#### 3.2 感受情绪 vs 表达情绪 / Felt vs. Displayed Emotions

| 类型 | 英文 | 定义 / Definition |
|------|------|------------------|
| 感受情绪 | Felt Emotions | An individual's actual emotions (个体的真实情绪) |
| 表达情绪 | Displayed Emotions | Emotions that are organizationally required and considered appropriate (组织要求的、被认为合适的情绪) |

#### 3.3 表层扮演 vs 深层扮演 / Surface Acting vs. Deep Acting

| 类型 | 英文 | 定义 / Definition | 心理成本 |
|------|------|------------------|---------|
| 表层扮演 | Surface Acting | Hiding one's inner feelings and forgoing emotional expressions in response to display rules (隐藏真实感受，仅改变外在表达) | 高 |
| 深层扮演 | Deep Acting | Trying to modify one's true inner feelings based on display rules (试图改变内在真实感受) | 低 |

> **Key Finding**: Deep acting is less psychologically costly than surface acting because you are actually trying to experience the emotion.  
> **关键结论**：深层扮演比表层扮演心理成本更低，因为你在真正尝试体验那种情绪。

#### 3.4 情绪失调 / Emotional Dissonance

Emotional dissonance occurs when employees have to project one emotion while feeling another. This disparity can lead to emotional exhaustion and burnout.

情绪失调是指员工必须表达一种情绪但同时感受到另一种情绪时的不一致。这种冲突会导致情绪耗竭和工作倦怠。

**后果 / Consequences:**
- Emotional exhaustion (情绪耗竭)
- Job burnout (工作倦怠)
- Decreased job satisfaction (工作满意度下降)
- Reduced performance (绩效下降)

#### 3.5 正念 / Mindfulness

Mindfulness means objectively and deliberately evaluating our emotional situation in the moment. It can reduce emotional exhaustion and increase job satisfaction.

正念是客观且有意识地评估当下的情绪状况。它可以减轻情绪耗竭，提高工作满意度。

***

### 四、情感事件理论 / Affective Events Theory (AET)

**Proposed by:** Weiss & Cropanzano (1996)

Affective Events Theory (AET) proposes that workplace events cause emotional reactions, which then influence job attitudes and performance. Personality and mood moderate these emotional reactions.

情感事件理论（AET）提出工作事件引发情绪反应，进而影响工作态度和绩效。人格和心境会调节情绪反应的强度。

**Theory Flow / 理论流程:**
```
Work Events → Emotional Reactions → Job Attitudes & Performance
工作事件 → 情绪反应 → 工作态度与行为
         (Moderated by Personality & Mood / 受人格和心境调节)
```

> **Managerial Implication**: Managers should not ignore employee emotions and the events that cause them, even if they seem trivial, because they accumulate.  
> **管理启示**: 管理者不应忽视工作中的情绪和引发情绪的事件，即使它们看起来很微小，因为它们会累积。

***

### 五、情绪智力 / Emotional Intelligence (EI)

Emotional intelligence (EI) refers to an individual's ability to perceive, understand, and regulate their own emotions and the emotions of others.

情绪智力（EI）是指个人感知、理解和调节自己及他人情绪的能力。

**Four Dimensions of EI / 情绪智力的四个维度:**

| 维度 / Dimension | 英文 | 描述 / Description |
|-----------------|------|-------------------|
| 自我意识 | Self-Awareness | Recognizing one's own emotions (识别自身情绪) |
| 社会意识 | Social Awareness | Recognizing others' emotions (识别他人情绪) |
| 自我管理 | Self-Management | Managing one's own emotions (管理自身情绪) |
| 关系管理 | Relationship Management | Managing emotions in relationships (管理人际关系中的情绪) |

**Controversies / 争议:**
- EI is related to job performance, but the correlation is not strong
  EI与工作绩效相关，但相关性不高
- EI tests have reliability issues (mostly self-report questionnaires)
  EI测试的可靠性存在问题（多为自评问卷）
- EI can largely be explained by personality traits like emotional stability
  EI在很大程度上可由情绪稳定性等人格特质解释

***

### 六、情绪调节 / Emotion Regulation

Emotion regulation is the process of identifying and modifying the emotions you feel.

情绪调节是识别和修改所感受到的情绪的过程。

#### 6.1 影响因素 / Influencing Factors

- **Personality**: People high in neuroticism have more difficulty regulating emotions
  人格：高神经质者更难调节情绪
- **Self-esteem**: People with low self-esteem are less likely to try to improve sad moods
  自尊：低自尊者不太可能尝试改善悲伤情绪
- **Work context**: Diverse teams promote emotion regulation
  工作环境：多元化程度高的团队促进情绪调节

#### 6.2 情绪压抑 / Emotional Suppression

Emotional suppression means ignoring or inhibiting your initial emotional response.

情绪压抑是指忽略或抑制你的初始情绪反应。

- Helps with rational thinking in the short term
  短期内有助于理性思考
- Only helpful in crisis situations
  仅在危机情况下有帮助
- Daily use has negative psychological, emotional, and interpersonal consequences
  日常使用会对心理、情感和人际关系造成负面影响

***

### 七、情绪与心境的应用 / OB Applications of Emotions and Moods

| 应用领域 / Application | 关键发现 / Key Finding |
|----------------------|----------------------|
| **人员甄选 / Selection** | High EI performers do better in jobs requiring social interaction (高EI者在需要社交互动的工作中表现更好) |
| **决策制定 / Decision Making** | Positive emotions help make better decisions and solve problems (积极情绪有助于做出更好的决策和解决问题) |
| **创造力 / Creativity** | Positive emotions increase creativity; activating moods help more than deactivating moods (积极情绪提高创造力；激活型心境更有助于创造力) |
| **动机 / Motivation** | Performance feedback affects emotions, which in turn affect motivation (绩效反馈影响情绪，进而影响动机) |
| **领导力 / Leadership** | Leaders who share positive emotions are perceived as more effective (分享积极情绪的领导者被认为更有效) |
| **客户服务 / Customer Service** | Emotional contagion affects customer satisfaction (情绪传染影响客户满意度) |
| **工作态度 / Work Attitudes** | Positive emotions at work spill over to home life (工作中的积极情绪会溢出到家庭) |
| **偏差行为 / Deviant Behavior** | Negative emotions are related to CWB (负面情绪与偏差行为相关) |
| **工作安全 / Workplace Safety** | Negative emotions increase accident risk (负面情绪增加工作事故风险) |

#### 论述题 / Essay Question

**Q11:** Explain the difference between surface acting and deep acting. Which is more effective and why? How can organizations help employees manage emotional labor?

<details>
<summary>查看答案 / Answer Template</summary>

**Standard Answer Template / 标准答案模板:**

1. **Define Both Concepts / 定义两个概念**

   Surface acting is hiding one's inner feelings and forgoing emotional expressions in response to display rules — the employee puts on a false face. Deep acting is trying to modify one's true inner feelings based on display rules — the employee actually tries to experience the emotion required.

   表层扮演是隐藏真实感受并抑制情绪表达以响应展示规则——员工戴上假面具。深层扮演是根据展示规则试图改变内在真实感受——员工真正尝试体验所需的情绪。

2. **Effectiveness Comparison / 有效性比较**

   Deep acting is more effective and less psychologically costly than surface acting. Surface acting creates emotional dissonance (inconsistency between felt and displayed emotions), which leads to emotional exhaustion, burnout, and reduced job satisfaction. Deep acting reduces this dissonance because the employee actually tries to feel the emotion they need to display.

   深层扮演比表层扮演更有效且心理成本更低。表层扮演产生情绪失调（感受与表达情绪的不一致），导致情绪耗竭、倦怠和工作满意度降低。深层扮演减少这种失调，因为员工真正尝试感受他们需要表达的情绪。

3. **Why Deep Acting Works Better / 为什么深层扮演更有效**

   Deep acting reduces the psychological burden because employees are not suppressing genuine emotions. It creates more authentic emotional displays, which customers and coworkers perceive as genuine. Surface acting, in contrast, can be detected as inauthentic and may damage interpersonal relationships.

   深层扮演减轻心理负担，因为员工没有压抑真实情绪。它创造更真实的情绪展示，客户和同事能感知到其真诚性。相比之下，表层扮演可能被察觉为不真诚，可能损害人际关系。

4. **Organizational Interventions / 组织干预措施**

   (a) Select employees with high emotional intelligence for jobs requiring high emotional labor. (b) Provide training in deep acting techniques and emotion regulation. (c) Create a supportive work environment that allows employees to express genuine emotions during breaks. (d) Use mindfulness programs to help employees evaluate their emotional situations objectively. (e) Design jobs that minimize the gap between felt and displayed emotions. (f) Monitor for signs of emotional exhaustion and burnout.

### 论述题答题模板 / Essay Answer Template

**Sample Question / 题目示例:** How can managers use their understanding of emotions and moods to improve employee performance?

**Standard Answer / 标准答案:**

1. **Acknowledge Emotions as Natural / 承认情绪的自然性**

   Managers should recognize that emotions are a natural part of workplace life and cannot be eliminated. Employees bring their emotions to work every day, and ignoring them is counterproductive.

   管理者应认识到情绪是职场生活的自然组成部分，无法消除。员工每天都带着情绪上班，忽视它们会适得其反。

2. **Model Positive Emotions / 示范积极情绪**

   Leaders who display positive emotions foster effective decision making, creativity, and cooperation among team members. Emotional contagion means the leader's mood spreads to the team.

   展示积极情绪的领导者促进团队的有效决策、创造力和合作。情绪传染意味着领导者的情绪会传染给团队。

3. **Provide Positive Feedback / 提供积极反馈**

   Performance feedback affects employees' emotional reactions, which in turn affect motivation and future performance. Positive feedback increases positivity and commitment.

   绩效反馈影响员工的情绪反应，进而影响动机和未来绩效。积极反馈增加积极性和承诺。

4. **Understand Affective Events / 理解情感事件**

   Managers should not ignore workplace events that trigger emotional reactions, even if they seem trivial, because they accumulate over time and affect job attitudes and performance.

   管理者不应忽视引发情绪反应的工作事件，即使它们看起来微不足道，因为它们会随时间累积并影响工作态度和绩效。

5. **Address Emotional Labor / 处理情绪劳动**

   In service sector jobs, encourage deep acting rather than surface acting. Provide training, select high-EI employees, and create supportive environments to reduce emotional dissonance.

   在服务行业工作中，鼓励深层扮演而非表层扮演。提供培训、选择高EI员工、创造支持性环境以减少情绪失调。
