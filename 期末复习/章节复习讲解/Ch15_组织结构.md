## 第一部分：理论基础 / Theory Foundations

### 一、组织结构定义与七要素 / Definition and Seven Elements

Organizational structure defines how job tasks are formally divided, grouped, and coordinated.

组织结构定义工作如何正式被划分、分组和协调。

1. **Work Specialization (工作专业化)**: Degree to which activities are divided into separate jobs. Also called division of labor. Human diseconomies appear when specialization is too high (boredom, fatigue, stress, low productivity, high turnover).
   活动被划分为独立工作的程度。也称劳动分工。当专业化过高时出现人类不经济（无聊、疲劳、压力、低生产力、高离职）。

2. **Departmentalization (部门化)**: Basis by which jobs are grouped together.
   工作被分组的基础。
   - **Functional (职能部门化)**: Grouped by function (engineering, HR, accounting). Most popular. Efficiencies from specialists together.
     按功能分组。最受欢迎。专家在一起产生效率。
   - **Product/Service (产品/服务部门化)**: Grouped by product line. Increased accountability.
     按产品线分组。增加问责制。
   - **Geographical (地理部门化)**: Grouped by territory/region. Valuable when customers scattered.
     按地区分组。当客户分散时有价值。
   - **Process (过程部门化)**: Grouped by steps in a process.
     按过程步骤分组。
   - **Customer (客户部门化)**: Grouped by customer type.
     按客户类型分组。

3. **Chain of Command (指挥链)**: Unbroken line of authority from top to lowest echelon. Clarifies who reports to whom.
   从最高到最低层级的不间断权威线。明确谁向谁报告。
   - **Authority (权威)**: Rights inherent in managerial position to give orders and expect obedience.
     管理职位下达命令并期望服从的权利。
   - **Unity of Command (统一指挥)**: Each subordinate should have only one superior. Broken = conflicting demands.
     每个下属应该只有一个上级。被破坏=冲突需求。

4. **Span of Control (管理跨度)**: Number of subordinates a manager can efficiently and effectively direct. Wider span = more efficient (fewer managers, lower costs). But at some point effectiveness declines.
   管理者能有效指导的下属数量。更宽的管理跨度=更高效（更少管理者、更低成本）。但在某一点上效果下降。

5. **Centralization vs. Decentralization (集权与分权)**: Degree to which decision making is concentrated at a single point. Centralized = top managers make all decisions. Decentralized = pushed down to managers closest to action.
   决策集中在单一位置的程度。集权=高层管理者做所有决策。分权=下推到最接近行动的管理者。

6. **Formalization (正规化)**: Degree to which jobs are standardized. High formalization = explicit job descriptions, lots of rules, consistent output. Low formalization = great discretion.
   工作被标准化的程度。高正规化=明确的工作描述、大量规则、一致的产出。低正规化=很大的自由裁量权。

7. **Boundary Spanning (边界跨越)**: When individuals form relationships outside their formally assigned groups. Enhances creativity and prevents rigidity.
   个人在正式分配群体之外形成关系时。增强创造力并防止僵化。

### 二、常见组织框架 / Common Organizational Frameworks

**Simple Structure (简单结构)**: Low departmentalization, wide spans of control, centralized authority, little formalization. Fast, flexible, inexpensive. Becomes inadequate as organization grows (information overload).
低部门化、宽管理跨度、集权、低正规化。快速、灵活、廉价。随着组织成长变得不足（信息超载）。

**Bureaucracy (官僚制)**: Highly routine tasks through specialization, strictly formalized rules, functional departments, centralized authority, narrow spans, chain of command. Efficient for standardized activities but inflexible when cases don't fit rules.
通过专业化的常规任务、严格规则、职能部门、集权、窄跨度、指挥链。标准化活动高效，但当案例不符合规则时不灵活。

**Matrix Structure (矩阵结构)**: Combines functional and product departmentalization. Dual lines of command (functional + product managers). Breaks unity-of-command. Facilitates coordination for complex activities. Creates confusion, power struggles, stress.
结合职能部门化和产品部门化。双重指挥线（功能+产品经理）。打破统一指挥。促进复杂活动的协调。造成混乱、权力斗争、压力。

### 三、替代设计方案 / Alternate Design Options

**Virtual Structure (虚拟结构)**: Small core organization that outsources major functions. Highly centralized, little departmentalization. Major advantage: flexibility. Drawbacks: perpetual flux, unclear roles, limited innovation.
外包主要功能的小型核心组织。高度集中、少部门化。主要优势：灵活性。缺点：持续变动、角色不清、创新有限。

**Team Structure (团队结构)**: Eliminates chain of command, replaces departments with empowered teams. Removes vertical and horizontal boundaries. Flattens hierarchy.
消除指挥链，用授权团队取代部门。消除垂直和水平边界。扁平化层级。

**Circular Structure (圆形结构)**: Executives at center, radiating outward in rings grouped by function. Still a hierarchy, but vision spreads outward. Intuitive appeal for creative entrepreneurs.
高管在中心，按功能分组向外辐射。仍是层级，但愿景向外传播。对创造性企业家具有直观吸引力。

### 四、精简组织：裁员 / The Leaner Organization: Downsizing

Downsizing: systematic effort to make organization leaner by closing locations, reducing staff, or selling off non-value-adding units. Immediate positive: lower wage costs. Negative: remaining employees feel worried, less committed, stress reactions, more voluntary turnover, vital human capital lost.
裁员：通过关闭地点、减少员工或出售无价值增值单位使组织更精简的系统努力。即时正面：更低工资成本。负面：留下的员工感到担心、承诺降低、压力反应、更多自愿离职、流失重要人力资本。

### 五、为什么结构不同？ / Why Do Structures Differ?

**Mechanistic Model (机械模型)**: Extensive departmentalization, high formalization, limited information network, centralization, clear chain of command, narrow spans. Like bureaucracy.
广泛部门化、高正规化、有限信息网络、集权、明确指挥链、窄跨度。像官僚制。

**Organic Model (有机模型)**: Cross-functional teams, cross-hierarchical teams, free flow of information, wide spans, decentralization, low formalization. Flat structure.
跨职能团队、跨层级团队、信息自由流动、宽跨度、分权、低正规化。扁平结构。

**Strategy-Structure Match (战略-结构匹配)**:
- **Innovation strategy** → Organic structure (flexibility)
  创新战略→有机结构（灵活性）
- **Cost-minimization strategy** → Mechanistic structure (efficiency, stability)
  成本最小化战略→机械结构（效率、稳定性）
- **Imitation strategy** → Mix of mechanistic and organic
  模仿战略→机械和有机的混合

**Other Factors (其他因素)**:
- **Organization Size**: Larger organizations have more specialization, departmentalization, vertical levels, rules. Size matters less beyond ~2000 employees.
  更大组织有更多专业化、部门化、垂直层级、规则。超过~2000员工后规模影响减小。
- **Technology**: How organization transfers inputs into outputs. Structures adapt to technology.
  组织如何将输入转化为输出。结构适应技术。
- **Environment**: Three dimensions — capacity (abundant vs. scarce), volatility (stable vs. dynamic), complexity (simple vs. complex). More uncertain environment → more organic.
  三个维度——容量（充足vs.稀缺）、波动性（稳定vs.动态）、复杂性（简单vs.复杂）。更不确定环境→更有机。

### 六、结构对员工行为的影响 / Structures and Employee Behavior

- Work specialization contributes to productivity but at price of satisfaction.
- No evidence supports relationship between span of control and satisfaction/performance.
- Less centralized organizations → greater autonomy → positively related to job satisfaction (but some find ambiguity frustrating).
- People select employers matching their characteristics (predictability → mechanistic; autonomy → organic).
- National culture influences preference (high power-distance cultures more accepting of mechanistic structures).

- 工作专业化促进生产力但以满意度为代价。
- 没有证据支持管理跨度与满意度/绩效之间的关系。
- 较少集中的组织→更大自主权→与工作满意度正相关（但有些人发现模糊性令人沮丧）。
- 人们选择与其特征匹配的雇主（可预测性→机械；自主权→有机）。
- 民族文化影响偏好（高权力距离文化更易接受机械结构）。

## 第二部分：核心概念速查 / Core Concepts Quick Reference

### 机械vs有机模型 / Mechanistic vs. Organic Models

| 维度 / Dimension | Mechanistic 机械 | Organic 有机 |
|-----------------|-----------------|-------------|
| Departmentalization 部门化 | Rigid 刚性 | Cross-functional 跨职能 |
| Chain of command 指挥链 | Clear 明确 | Flexible 灵活 |
| Span of control 管理跨度 | Narrow 窄 | Wide 宽 |
| Centralization 集权 | Centralized 集权 | Decentralized 分权 |
| Formalization 正规化 | High 高 | Low 低 |

### 战略-结构匹配 / Strategy-Structure Match

| 战略 / Strategy | 结构 / Structure | 理由 / Reason |
|---------------|-----------------|-------------|
| Innovation 创新 | Organic 有机 | Flexibility 灵活性 |
| Cost-minimization 成本最小化 | Mechanistic 机械 | Efficiency, stability 效率、稳定性 |
| Imitation 模仿 | Both 两者 | Tight controls current, loose for new 当前紧、新业务松 |

2. **机械vs有机模型 / Mechanistic vs. Organic Models**: Characteristics of each
3. **战略-结构匹配 / Strategy-Structure Match**: Innovation→organic, cost-minimization→mechanistic, imitation→both

1. **矩阵结构的优缺点 / Advantages and Disadvantages of Matrix Structure**
2. **虚拟结构 / Virtual Structure**: Flexibility vs. perpetual flux
3. **裁员的影响 / Effects of Downsizing**: Cost savings vs. employee attitudes
4. **环境三维度 / Three Dimensions of Environment**: Capacity, volatility, complexity

### 🟢 低频考点
1. **边界跨越 / Boundary Spanning**: Within and between organizations
2. **圆形结构 / Circular Structure**: Executives at center
3. **管理跨度与满意度的关系 / Span of Control and Satisfaction**: No evidence
