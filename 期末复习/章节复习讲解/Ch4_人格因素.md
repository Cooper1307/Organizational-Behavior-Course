## 第一部分：理论基础 / Theory Foundations

### 一、什么是人格 / What is Personality?

Personality is the sum total of ways in which an individual reacts to and interacts with others. It is the dynamic organization within an individual of those psychophysical systems that determine unique adjustments to their environment.

人格是个体对环境的反应和与他人互动的方式的总和。它是个体内部决定其独特环境适应的心理生理系统的动态组织。

> **Key Point**: Personality is largely genetically determined and is shaped by both heredity and environment.  
> **关键点**：人格主要由遗传决定，同时受遗传和环境的共同塑造。

---

### 二、人格的决定因素 / Determinants of Personality

#### 1. 遗传因素 / Heredity

Heredity refers to factors determined at conception, including physical stature, facial attractiveness, gender, temperament, and muscle composition and reflexes. Research suggests that heredity is more important than environment in determining personality.

遗传因素是指在受孕时确定的因素，包括身材、面部吸引力、性别、气质、肌肉组成和反射。研究表明遗传在决定人格方面比环境更重要。

#### 2. 环境因素 / Environment

Environment includes the culture in which we are raised, our early conditioning, the norms among our family, friends, and social groups, and other influences we experience.

环境包括我们成长的文化、早期教育、家庭和朋友规范，以及其他经历的影响。

#### 3. 情境因素 / Situation

Situation influences the effects of heredity and environment on personality. Different situations call forth different aspects of one's personality.

情境因素影响遗传和环境对人格的作用。不同的情境会激发人格的不同方面。

> **Key Finding**: Research studies show heredity is more important than environment, but situation also plays a role in how personality manifests.  
> **关键发现**：研究表明遗传比环境更重要，但情境也会影响人格的表现方式。

---

### 三、MBTI人格类型指标 / Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI)

The MBTI is one of the most widely used personality-assessment instruments. It classifies individuals into 16 personality types based on four dimensions.

MBTI是最广泛使用的人格评估工具之一。它基于四个维度将个体分为16种人格类型。

#### 四个维度 / Four Dimensions

| 维度 / Dimension | 两端 / Poles | 描述 / Description |
|-----------------|-------------|-------------------|
| **外向/内向** | Extraverted (E) vs. Introverted (I) | Where you get energy: from the outer world or inner world (能量来源：外部世界或内在世界) |
| **感觉/直觉** | Sensing (S) vs. iNtuitive (N) | How you gather information: through senses or patterns (获取信息方式：通过感官或模式) |
| **思维/情感** | Thinking (T) vs. Feeling (F) | How you make decisions: logic and consistency or personal values (决策方式：逻辑和一致性或个人价值观) |
| **判断/感知** | Judging (J) vs. Perceiving (P) | How you approach life: structured and orderly or flexible and spontaneous (生活方式：结构化有条理或灵活自发) |

#### 常见MBTI类型 / Common MBTI Types

| 类型 / Type | 昵称 / Nickname | 特征 / Characteristics |
|------------|----------------|----------------------|
| **INTJ** | 远见者 / Visionary | Original minds, great drive, independent, critical, sometimes stubborn (原创思维、强烈驱动力、独立、批判性、有时固执) |
| **ENTP** | 概念者 / Conceptualizer | Innovative, individualistic, versatile, attracted to entrepreneurial ideas, may be too independent to follow routine rules (创新、个人主义、多样化、喜欢创业想法、可能太独立而不遵循常规) |
| **ESTJ** | 管理者 / Administrator | Practical, realistic, matter-of-fact, good at organizing (务实、实际、结果导向、善于组织) |
| **ESFP** | 表演者 / Performer | Outgoing, sociable, fun-loving, spontaneous (外向、善于社交、有趣、自发) |
| **INFP** | 理想主义者 / Idealist | Idealistic, loyal to values, creative, adaptable (理想主义、忠于价值观、创造力、适应性强) |

> **Memory Tip**: MBTI dimensions = E-I, S-N, T-F, J-P  
> **记忆口诀**：MBTI维度 = E-I, S-N, T-F, J-P

> **Important**: MBTI does NOT include Conscientiousness or Openness—those are Big Five dimensions!  
> **重要**：MBTI不包括尽责性或开放性——这些是大五人格的维度！

### 四、大五人格模型 / Big Five Model

The Big Five Model is the most widely accepted personality framework in OB. It identifies five fundamental dimensions of personality.

大五人格模型是OB中最广泛接受的人格框架。它确定了人格的五个基本维度。

#### 五个维度 / Five Dimensions (OCEAN)

| 维度 / Dimension | 高分特征 / High Score | 低分特征 / Low Score | 工作表现 / Work Performance |
|-----------------|---------------------|---------------------|---------------------------|
| **开放性 / Openness to Experience** | Creative, curious, artistic, open to new ideas (创造力、好奇心、开放新想法) | Conventional, conservative, prefers routine (传统、保守、喜欢常规) | Good for leadership and innovation (适合领导力和创新) |
| **尽责性 / Conscientiousness** | Responsible, organized, dependable, persistent (负责任、有组织、可靠、坚持) | Careless, unreliable, disorganized (粗心、不可靠、无组织) | Strongest predictor of job performance across all jobs (所有工作中绩效的最强预测因子) |
| **外向性 / Extraversion** | Sociable, assertive, energetic, talkative (善于社交、果断、精力充沛、健谈) | Reserved, quiet, independent (内向、安静、独立) | Good for sales and management (适合销售和管理) |
| **宜人性 / Agreeableness** | Warm, cooperative, trusting, helpful (温暖、合作、信任、乐于助人) | Competitive, suspicious, uncooperative (竞争、怀疑、不合作) | Good for teamwork (适合团队合作) |
| **情绪稳定性 / Emotional Stability** | Calm, confident, secure, less anxious (冷静、自信、安全、较少焦虑) | Anxious, insecure, nervous, emotional (焦虑、不安全、紧张、情绪化) | Important for high-stress jobs (高压工作很重要) |

> **Memory Tip**: **OCEAN** = Openness, Conscientiousness, Extraversion, Agreeableness, Neuroticism (Emotional Stability)  
> **记忆口诀**：OCEAN = 开放性、尽责性、外向性、宜人性、神经质（情绪稳定性）

#### 关键发现 / Key Findings

**Conscientiousness is the strongest predictor of job performance.**

尽责性是工作绩效的最强预测因子。

**Personality-Job Fit:**

In individualistic countries, matching personality to job increases satisfaction. In collectivistic countries, this relationship is weaker because people don't expect jobs to be tailored to them.

在个人主义国家，将人格与工作匹配可提高满意度。在集体主义国家，这种关系较弱，因为人们不期望工作为他们量身定制。

### 五、其他重要人格特质 / Other Personality Traits

#### 1. 核心自我评价 / Core Self-Evaluation (CSE)

Core self-evaluation is the degree to which people like themselves and believe in their capabilities. People with positive CSE believe in their inner worth and basic competence.

核心自我评价是人们喜欢自己并相信自己能力的程度。具有积极CSE的人相信自己的内在价值和基本能力。

**Positive CSE = More satisfied with their jobs, perform better, lower turnover**

积极CSE = 对工作更满意、表现更好、离职率更低

#### 2. 黑暗三联征 / Dark Triad

| 特质 / Trait | 描述 / Description |
|-------------|-------------------|
| **马基雅维利主义 / Machiavellianism** | Degree to which an individual is pragmatic, maintains emotional distance, and believes ends can justify means (实用主义、保持情感距离、相信目的可以证明手段的合理性) |
| **自恋 / Narcissism** | Tendency to be arrogant, have grandiose sense of self-importance, need excessive admiration (傲慢、自视甚高、需要过度崇拜) |
| **心理病态 / Psychopathy** | Lack of concern for others, impulsivity, lack of remorse (对他人漠不关心、冲动、无悔意) |

#### 3. 控制点 / Locus of Control

| 类型 / Type | 描述 / Description |
|------------|-------------------|
| **内控者 / Internal Locus** | Believe they control their own destiny (相信自己控制自己的命运) |
| **外控者 / External Locus** | Believe outside forces control their fate (相信外部力量控制自己的命运) |

### 六、人格-工作匹配理论 / Personality-Job Fit Theory

**Proposed by:** John Holland

Holland's theory identifies six personality types and proposes that the fit between personality type and occupational environment determines satisfaction and turnover.

霍兰德的理论确定了六种人格类型，并提出人格类型与职业环境之间的匹配决定满意度和离职率。

#### 霍兰德的六种人格类型 / Holland's Six Types

| 类型 / Type | 人格特征 / Characteristics | 匹配职业 / Congruent Occupations |
|------------|---------------------------|-------------------------------|
| **现实型 / Realistic** | Prefers physical activities, practical (偏好身体活动、实际) | Mechanic, farmer, assembly-line worker |
| **研究型 / Investigative** | Prefers thinking, organizing, understanding (偏好思考、组织、理解) | Biologist, economist, mathematician |
| **社会型 / Social** | Prefers helping and developing others (偏好帮助和培养他人) | Social worker, teacher, counselor |
| **常规型 / Conventional** | Prefers rule-regulated, orderly activities (偏好规则化、有序的活动) | Accountant, bank teller, file clerk |
| **企业型 / Enterprising** | Prefers verbal activities, influencing others (偏好语言活动、影响他人) | Lawyer, real estate agent, manager |
| **艺术型 / Artistic** | Prefers creative expression, ambiguous situations (偏好创造性表达、模糊情境) | Painter, musician, writer |

### 七、文化价值观 / Cultural Values

#### 霍斯特的五维度框架 / Hofstede's Five Dimensions

| 维度 / Dimension | 描述 / Description | 高分 / High Score | 低分 / Low Score |
|-----------------|-------------------|-----------------|-----------------|
| **权力距离 / Power Distance** | Acceptance of unequal power distribution (接受不平等的权力分配) | Accepts inequality, class system (接受不平等、阶级制度) | Stresses equality and opportunity (强调平等和机会) |
| **个人主义/集体主义 / Individualism vs. Collectivism** | Preference for acting as individual vs. group member (作为个体还是群体成员行事) | Individual rights above all (个人权利高于一切) | Tight social framework, group loyalty (紧密社会框架、群体忠诚) |
| **男性化/女性化 / Masculinity vs. Femininity** | Favoring traditional masculine roles vs. gender equality (偏好传统男性角色还是性别平等) | Achievement, power, control (成就、权力、控制) | Little differentiation between genders (性别差异小) |
| **不确定性规避 / Uncertainty Avoidance** | Preference for structured over unstructured situations (偏好结构化还是非结构化情境) | High anxiety, use laws to reduce uncertainty (高焦虑、使用法律减少不确定性) | Accept ambiguity, take risks (接受模糊性、承担风险) |
| **长期/短期导向 / Long-term vs. Short-term Orientation** | Devotion to traditional values vs. present orientation (忠于传统价值观还是面向现在) | Value future, thrift, persistence (重视未来、节俭、坚持) | Value present, accept change (重视现在、接受变化) |

#### GLOBE框架 / GLOBE Framework

The GLOBE framework adds two additional dimensions to Hofstede's framework:

GLOBE框架在霍斯特框架基础上增加了两个维度：

- **Humane Orientation / 人文导向**: Degree to which society rewards altruism, generosity, and kindness (社会奖励利他主义、慷慨和善良的程度)
- **Performance Orientation / 绩效导向**: Degree to which society encourages and rewards performance improvement and excellence (社会鼓励和奖励绩效改进和卓越的程度)
