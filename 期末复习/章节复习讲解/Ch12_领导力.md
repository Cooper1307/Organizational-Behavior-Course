## 第一部分：理论基础 / Theory Foundations

### 一、领导特质理论 / Trait Theories of Leadership

Leadership is the ability to influence a group toward the achievement of a vision or set of goals. Not all leaders are managers, nor are all managers leaders. Nonsanctioned leadership — the ability to influence that arises outside the formal structure of the organization — is sometimes more important than formal influence.

领导是影响群体实现愿景或一组目标的能力。并非所有领导者都是管理者，也并非所有管理者都是领导者。非正式领导——在组织正式结构之外产生的影响能力——有时比正式影响更重要。

Trait theories of leadership focus on personal qualities, including personality traits and characteristics that predict two distinct outcomes: leadership emergence and leadership effectiveness. Traits can predict leadership, but they do a better job predicting the emergence and appearance of leadership than distinguishing between effective and ineffective leaders. The fact that an individual exhibits the right traits and others consider that person a leader does not necessarily mean he or she will be effective.

领导特质理论关注个人品质，包括人格特质和预测两种不同结果的特征：领导涌现和领导有效性。特质可以预测领导，但它们在预测领导的涌现和表现方面比区分有效和无效领导者做得更好。个人展现出正确的特质且他人认为该人是领导者并不一定意味着他/她会有效。

> **Key Point**: Traits predict leadership emergence better than leadership effectiveness.  
> **记忆要点**：特质预测领导涌现比预测领导有效性更好。

### 二、大五人格特质与领导 / Big Five Traits and Leadership

**Extraversion**: The most predictive trait of effective leadership. However, it sometimes relates more to how leaders emerge than to their effectiveness. Sociable and dominant people are more likely to assert themselves in group situations. But effective leaders are not domineering — leaders who scored very high in assertiveness were less effective than those who were moderately high.

**外向性**：预测有效领导的最具预测性的特质。然而，它有时更多地与领导者如何涌现相关，而非他们的有效性。善于社交和支配的人更可能在群体情境中坚持自己。但有效领导者不是专制的——在果断性上得分非常高的领导者不如中等高分的领导者有效。

**Conscientiousness and Openness**: May predict leadership, especially leader effectiveness. Top management teams high in conscientiousness positively influenced organizational performance. Both are positively related to leaders' self-efficacy, and since people are more likely to follow someone confident, these leaders tend to emerge.

**尽责性和开放性**：可能预测领导，特别是领导有效性。尽责性高的高管团队积极影响组织绩效。两者都与领导者的自我效能正相关，由于人们更可能跟随自信的人，这些领导者倾向于涌现。

**Agreeableness and Emotional Stability**: Do not seem to predict leadership.

**宜人性和情绪稳定性**：似乎不能预测领导。

**Dark-Side Traits (Machiavellianism, Narcissism, Psychopathy)**: Normative (mid-range) scores are optimal; low and high scores are associated with ineffective leadership. High scores and high emotional stability can contribute to leadership emergence. Building self-awareness and self-regulation skills may help control Dark-Side trait effects.

**黑暗面特质（马基雅维利主义、自恋、心理变态）**：规范性（中等范围）得分最优；低分和高分与无效领导相关。高分和高情绪稳定性可能促进领导涌现。建立自我意识和自我调节技能可能有助于控制黑暗面特质效应。

### 三、情绪智力与领导 / Emotional Intelligence and Leadership

A core component of EI is empathy. Empathetic leaders can sense others' needs, listen to what followers say (and don't say), and read the reactions of others. A leader who effectively displays and manages emotions will find it easier to influence the feelings of followers. People high in EI are more likely to emerge as leaders, even after taking cognitive ability and personality into account.

情绪智力的核心成分是同理心。有同理心的领导者能感知他人的需求，倾听追随者说的话（和没说的话），并解读他人的反应。有效展示和管理情绪的领导者会发现更容易影响追随者的感受。即使考虑了认知能力和人格，情绪智力高的人也更可能成为领导者。

### 四、行为理论 / Behavioral Theories

Behavioral theories help define the parameters of leadership. Trait research provides a basis for selecting the right people; behavioral theories imply we can train people to be leaders.

行为理论帮助定义领导的参数。特质研究为选择合适的人提供基础；行为理论意味着我们可以培训人们成为领导者。

The Ohio State Studies identified two independent dimensions of leader behavior:

俄亥俄州立大学研究确定了领导者行为的两个独立维度：

**Initiating Structure**: The extent to which a leader is likely to define and structure his or her role and those of employees in the search for goal attainment. Includes assigning followers particular tasks, setting definite performance standards, and emphasizing deadlines. More strongly related to higher levels of group and organization productivity and more positive performance evaluations.

**定规**：领导者在追求目标实现过程中定义和构建自己角色及员工角色的程度。包括为追随者分配特定任务、设定明确的绩效标准并强调截止日期。与更高水平的群体和组织生产力以及更积极的绩效评估更密切相关。

**Consideration**: The extent to which a person's job relationships are characterized by mutual trust, respect for employees' ideas, and regard for their feelings. A leader high in consideration helps employees with personal problems, is friendly and approachable, treats all employees as equals, and expresses appreciation and support (people-oriented). 66% of U.S. employees mentioned appreciation as what most motivated them at work. Followers of leaders high in consideration were more satisfied, motivated, and respectful.

**关怀**：一个人的工作关系以相互信任、尊重员工想法和关注他们感受为特征的程度。高关怀的领导者帮助员工解决个人问题，友好且平易近人，将所有员工视为平等，并表达欣赏和支持（以人为本）。66%的美国员工提到欣赏是工作中最能激励他们的因素。高关怀领导者的追随者更满意、更有动力和更尊重。

**Cultural Differences**: Leaders high in consideration succeed best in countries where cultural values do not favor unilateral decision making (e.g., Brazil). A leader high in initiating structure will do best in France (more bureaucratic view of leaders). In China, both consideration and initiating structure may be important.

**文化差异**：高关怀的领导者在不重视单方面决策的国家（如巴西）表现最好。高定规的领导者在法国（对领导者更官僚的看法）表现最好。在中国，关怀和定规都可能很重要。

### 五、权变理论 / Contingency Theories

**Fiedler Contingency Model**: Proposes that group performance depends on the proper match between the leader's style and the degree to which the situation gives the leader control. The leader's style is assumed to be permanent. The Least Preferred Coworker (LPC) questionnaire identifies whether a person is task-oriented (low LPC score) or relationship-oriented (high LPC score).

**费德勒权变模型**：提出群体绩效取决于领导者风格与情境赋予领导者控制程度之间的适当匹配。假设领导者的风格是固定的。最不喜欢的同事（LPC）问卷识别一个人是任务导向（低LPC分数）还是关系导向（高LPC分数）。

Three contingency dimensions:
1. **Leader-member relations**: Degree of confidence, trust, and respect members have in their leader.
2. **Task structure**: Degree to which job assignments are procedurized.
3. **Position power**: Degree of influence a leader has over power variables (hiring, firing, discipline, promotions, salary increases).

三个权变维度：
1. **领导者-成员关系**：成员对领导者的信任、信赖和尊重程度。
2. **任务结构**：工作分配的程序化程度。
3. **职位权力**：领导者对权力变量（招聘、解雇、纪律、晋升、加薪）的影响程度。

Task-oriented leaders perform better in very favorable (categories I, II, III) and very unfavorable (category VIII) situations. Relationship-oriented leaders perform better in moderately favorable situations (categories IV, V, VI).

任务导向领导者在非常有利（I、II、III类）和非常不利（VIII类）情境下表现更好。关系导向领导者在中等有利情境下（IV、V、VI类）表现更好。

**Situational Leadership Theory (SLT)**: Focuses on followers' readiness — the extent to which followers are willing and able to accomplish a specific task. Four behaviors based on follower readiness:
- Unable and unwilling → clear and specific directions
- Unable but willing → high task orientation + high relationship orientation
- Able but unwilling → supportive and participative style
- Able and willing → leader doesn't need to do much

**情境领导理论（SLT）**：关注追随者的准备度——追随者愿意和能够完成特定任务的程度。基于追随者准备度的四种行为：
- 不能且不愿→清晰具体的指示
- 不能但愿→高任务导向+高关系导向
- 能但不愿→支持和参与式风格
- 能且愿→领导者不需要做太多

**Path-Goal Theory**: The leader's job is to provide followers with information, support, or other resources necessary to achieve goals. Directive leadership yields greater satisfaction when tasks are ambiguous or stressful. Supportive leadership results in high performance and satisfaction when employees perform structured tasks. Directive leadership is redundant among employees with high ability or experience.

**路径-目标理论**：领导者的工作是为追随者提供实现目标所需的信息、支持或其他资源。当任务模糊或压力大时，指令型领导产生更高的满意度。当员工执行结构化任务时，支持型领导产生高绩效和满意度。对于高能力或经验的员工，指令型领导是多余的。

**Leader-Participation Model**: Relates leadership behavior to subordinate participation in decision making. Leader behavior must adjust to reflect the task structure. Provides rules for what types of decisions might be best made with subordinate participation.

**领导者参与模型**：将领导者行为与下属参与决策联系起来。领导者行为必须调整以反映任务结构。提供哪些类型的决策最好由下属参与的规则。

### 六、当代领导理论 / Contemporary Theories of Leadership

**Leader-Member Exchange (LMX) Theory**: Because of time pressures, leaders establish a special relationship with a small group of followers — the ingroup. They are trusted, get disproportionate attention, and are more likely to receive special privileges. Other followers fall into the outgroup. The leader implicitly categorizes the follower as "in" or "out" early in the interaction; the relationship becomes stable over time.

**领导者-成员交换（LMX）理论**：由于时间压力，领导者与一小部分追随者建立特殊关系——内群体。他们被信任，获得不成比例的关注，更可能获得特殊特权。其他追随者落入外群体。领导者在互动早期隐式地将追随者分类为"内"或"外"；关系随时间变得稳定。

Ingroup members have demographic, attitude, and personality characteristics similar to their leaders or higher competence. Leaders and followers of the same gender tend to have closer LMX relationships. Ingroup members receive higher performance ratings, engage in more citizenship behaviors, and report greater satisfaction. However, both ingroup and outgroup realize negative effects when leaders differentiate strongly — differential treatment hurts team trust and overall team performance suffers.

内群体成员具有与领导者相似的人口、态度和人格特征或更高的能力。同性别的领导者和追随者往往有更亲密的LMX关系。内群体成员获得更高的绩效评级，参与更多的公民行为，并报告更高的满意度。然而，当领导者强烈区分时，内群体和外群体都实现负面效应——差异化对待损害团队信任，整体团队绩效受损。

**Charismatic Leadership Theory**: Followers attribute heroic or extraordinary leadership abilities when they observe certain behaviors. Key characteristics: (1) Vision and articulation — expressed as an idealized goal; (2) Personal risk — willing to take high personal costs; (3) Sensitivity to follower needs — perceptive and responsive; (4) Unconventional behavior — novel and counter to norms.

**魅力型领导理论**：追随者在观察到某些行为时将英雄或非凡领导能力归因于领导者。关键特征：（1）愿景和表达——表达为理想化的目标；（2）个人风险——愿意承受高昂的个人代价；（3）对追随者需求的敏感度——感知敏锐且响应迅速；（4）非常规行为——新颖且违背规范。

Are charismatic leaders born or made? Both. Individuals are born with traits (identical twins scored similarly). Personality (extraverted, self-confident, achievement-oriented) is related. But all of us can develop a more charismatic leadership style within our limitations — use passion as a catalyst, speak animatedly, use eye contact and gestures.

魅力型领导者是天生的还是培养的？两者都是。个人天生具有特质（同卵双胞胎得分相似）。人格（外向、自信、成就导向）与之相关。但我们所有人都可以在自己的局限内培养更具魅力的领导风格——以激情为催化剂、生动地说话、使用眼神交流和手势。

Charismatic leadership has positive effects across many contexts, especially under stress or crisis. However, the dark-side exists — narcissistic charismatic leaders may allow personal goals to override organizational goals (e.g., Enron, Tyco, WorldCom).

魅力型领导在许多情境下都有积极影响，特别是在压力或危机下。然而，存在黑暗面——自恋的魅力型领导者可能让个人目标超越组织目标（例如，安然、泰科、世通）。

**Transactional vs. Transformational Leadership**:

**交易型领导与变革型领导**：

**Transactional Leaders**: Guide followers toward established goals by clarifying role and task requirements. Behaviors: contingent reward (exchange rewards for effort), management by exception active (watch for deviations and correct), management by exception passive (intervene only if standards not met), laissez-faire (abdicate responsibilities).

**交易型领导者**：通过澄清角色和任务要求来引导追随者实现既定目标。行为：权变奖励（用奖励交换努力）、积极例外管理（观察偏差并纠正）、消极例外管理（仅在标准未达到时干预）、放任型（放弃责任）。

**Transformational Leaders**: Inspire followers to transcend their self-interests for the good of the organization. They have an extraordinary effect on followers, who respond with increased commitment. Behaviors (the four I's): (1) Idealized influence — provides vision and sense of mission; (2) Inspirational motivation — communicates high expectations; (3) Intellectual stimulation — promotes rationality and careful problem solving; (4) Individualized consideration — gives personal attention, coaches, advises.

**变革型领导者**：激励追随者为了组织利益超越自身利益。他们对追随者产生非凡影响，追随者以增加的承诺作为回应。行为（四个I）：（1）理想化影响——提供愿景和使命感；（2）鼓舞性激励——传达高期望；（3）智力刺激——促进理性和仔细解决问题；（4）个性化关怀——给予个人关注、辅导、建议。

Transformational leadership builds on transactional leadership and produces levels of effort beyond what transactional leadership alone can do. The best leaders are both transactional and transformational. Transformational leadership is more strongly correlated with lower turnover, higher productivity, lower stress and burnout, and higher satisfaction.

变革型领导建立在交易型领导之上，产生超越仅靠交易型领导所能达到的努力水平。最好的领导者既是交易型也是变革型的。变革型领导与更低离职率、更高生产力、更低压力和倦怠、更高满意度更密切相关。

### 七、负责任的领导 / Responsible Leadership

**Authentic Leadership**: Focuses on moral aspects. Authentic leaders know who they are, know what they believe in, and act on those values openly and candidly. The primary quality produced is trust. They share information, encourage open communication, and stick to their ideals. They may promote corporate social responsibility (CSR).

**真诚领导**：关注道德方面。真诚的领导者知道自己是谁，知道自己信仰什么，并公开坦诚地按照这些价值观行事。产生的主要品质是信任。他们分享信息，鼓励开放沟通，坚持自己的理想。他们可能促进企业社会责任（CSR）。

**Ethical Leadership**: Addresses the means a leader uses to achieve goals as well as the content of those goals. Ethical top leadership influences all the way down the command structure. Leaders rated as highly ethical tend to have followers who engage in more OCBs and are more willing to bring problems to leaders' attention. Unethical leaders use charisma to enhance power over followers for self-serving ends. Socialized charismatic leadership conveys other-centered values through leaders who model ethical conduct.

**伦理领导**：解决领导者实现目标的手段以及目标的内容。伦理高层领导一直影响到整个指挥结构。被评为高度伦理的领导者往往有参与更多OCB的追随者，他们更愿意将问题引起领导者注意。不道德的领导者利用魅力增强对追随者的权力以谋取私利。社会化魅力型领导通过以身作则伦理行为的领导者传递以他人为中心的价值观。

**Servant Leadership**: Go beyond self-interest and focus on opportunities to help followers grow and develop. Behaviors include listening, empathizing, persuading, accepting stewardship, and actively developing followers' potential. Effects: higher commitment to supervisor, self-efficacy, perceptions of justice, OCB, team potency, creative performance, job performance. Negatively correlated with narcissism. More prevalent and effective in East Asian cultures.

**服务型领导**：超越自身利益，专注于帮助追随者成长和发展的机会。行为包括倾听、同理心、说服、接受管家职责和积极发展追随者的潜力。效果：对主管的更高承诺、自我效能、公平感知、OCB、团队潜力、创造性绩效、工作绩效。与自恋负相关。在东亚文化中更普遍和有效。

### 八、信任 / Trust

Trust is a psychological state that exists when you agree to make yourself vulnerable to another person because you have positive expectations about how things are going to turn out. Transformational leaders generate a higher level of trust from followers, which relates to higher team confidence and performance.

信任是一种心理状态，当你同意使自己易受他人影响，因为你对事情如何发展有积极期望时存在。变革型领导者从追随者那里产生更高水平的信任，这与更高的团队信心和绩效相关。

**Outcomes of Trust**: Encourages risk taking, facilitates information sharing, trusting groups are more effective, enhances productivity.

**信任的结果**：鼓励冒险、促进信息共享、信任的群体更有效、提高生产力。

**Trust Development**: Three key characteristics:
1. **Integrity**: Honesty and truthfulness; consistency between what you do and say.
2. **Benevolence**: The trusted person has your interests at heart.
3. **Ability**: Technical and interpersonal knowledge and skills.

**信任发展**：三个关键特征：
1. **正直**：诚实和真实；言行一致。
2. **善意**：被信任的人把你的利益放在心上。
3. **能力**：技术和人际知识和技能。

## 第三部分：考点总结 / Key Exam Points

### 🔴 高频考点（必考）

1. **特质vs行为理论 / Trait vs. Behavioral Theories**: Traits predict emergence better; behaviors can be trained.
2. **定规vs关怀 / Initiating Structure vs. Consideration**: Definitions, outcomes, cultural differences.
3. **费德勒权变模型 / Fiedler Contingency Model**: LPC questionnaire, three dimensions, when task vs. relationship oriented performs better.

#### 论述题 / Essay Question

**Q11:** Compare transformational leadership with transactional leadership. How do they complement each other?

<details>
<summary>查看答案 / Answer Template</summary>

**Standard Answer Template / 标准答案模板:**

1. **Define Transactional Leadership / 定义交易型领导**

   Transactional leaders guide followers toward established goals by clarifying role and task requirements. Behaviors include contingent reward (exchange rewards for effort), management by exception active/passive, and laissez-faire. They focus on compliance and maintaining the status quo.

   交易型领导者通过澄清角色和任务要求来引导追随者实现既定目标。行为包括权变奖励（用奖励交换努力）、积极/消极例外管理和放任型。他们关注遵从和维持现状。

2. **Define Transformational Leadership / 定义变革型领导**

   Transformational leaders inspire followers to transcend their self-interests for the good of the organization. The four I's: idealized influence (vision and mission), inspirational motivation (high expectations), intellectual stimulation (rationality and problem solving), individualized consideration (personal attention and coaching).

   变革型领导者激励追随者为了组织利益超越自身利益。四个I：理想化影响（愿景和使命）、鼓舞性激励（高期望）、智力刺激（理性和解决问题）、个性化关怀（个人关注和辅导）。

3. **Complementary Nature / 互补性质**

   Transformational leadership builds on transactional leadership. The best leaders are both. Transformational leadership produces effort and performance beyond what transactional leadership alone can do. However, if you are only transactional without transformational qualities, you'll be a mediocre leader. The full range of leadership model shows that the four I's result in extra effort, higher productivity, higher morale and satisfaction, lower turnover and absenteeism, and greater organizational adaptability.

   变革型领导建立在交易型领导之上。最好的领导者两者兼备。变革型领导产生超越仅靠交易型领导所能达到的努力和绩效。然而，如果你只是交易型而没有变革型品质，你将是平庸的领导者。全方位领导模型表明，四个I产生额外努力、更高生产力、更高士气和满意度、更低离职和缺勤率、更大的组织适应性。

4. **Evaluation / 评估**

   Research indicates transformational leadership is more strongly correlated with lower turnover, higher productivity, lower stress and burnout, and higher satisfaction. However, contingent reward (a transactional behavior) sometimes works as well as transformational leadership. The general conclusion is that transformational leadership is desirable and effective, given the right application.

   研究表明变革型领导与更低离职率、更高生产力、更低压力和倦怠、更高满意度更密切相关。然而，权变奖励（一种交易型行为）有时与变革型领导一样有效。一般结论是变革型领导是理想且有效的，前提是正确应用。

</details>

### 论述题答题模板 / Essay Answer Template

**Sample Question / 题目示例:** Discuss the effectiveness of charismatic leadership. What are its characteristics and potential dark sides?

**Standard Answer / 标准答案:**

1. **Define Charismatic Leadership / 定义魅力型领导**

   Charismatic leadership theory states that followers attribute heroic or extraordinary leadership abilities when they observe certain behaviors. Max Weber defined charisma as a quality that sets individuals apart from ordinary people, treated as endowed with exceptional powers.

   魅力型领导理论指出，追随者在观察到某些行为时将英雄或非凡领导能力归因于领导者。马克斯·韦伯将魅力定义为一种使个人与普通人区分开来的品质，被视为具有非凡能力。

2. **Four Key Characteristics / 四个关键特征**

   (1) Vision and articulation — expressed as idealized goal proposing a better future. (2) Personal risk — willing to take high costs and self-sacrifice. (3) Sensitivity to follower needs — perceptive and responsive. (4) Unconventional behavior — novel and counter to norms.

   （1）愿景和表达——表达为提议更美好未来的理想化目标。（2）个人风险——愿意承受高代价和自我牺牲。（3）对追随者需求的敏感度——感知敏锐且响应迅速。（4）非常规行为——新颖且违背规范。

3. **Born or Made / 天生的还是培养的**

   Both. Identical twins scored similarly on charismatic measures. Personality (extraverted, self-confident, achievement-oriented) is related. But all of us can develop charismatic style within limitations — use passion, speak animatedly, use eye contact and gestures.

   两者都是。同卵双胞胎在魅力测量上得分相似。人格（外向、自信、成就导向）与之相关。但我们所有人都可以在局限内培养魅力型风格——使用激情、生动地说话、使用眼神交流和手势。

4. **Situational Factors / 情境因素**

   Enhanced by stress and crisis — people are more receptive under stress. Some personalities (low self-esteem) are especially susceptible. Works across many contexts.

   被压力和危机增强——人们在压力下更容易接受。某些人格（低自尊）特别容易受影响。在许多情境下都有效。

5. **The Dark Side / 黑暗面**

   Charismatic leaders who are narcissistic may allow personal goals to override organizational goals. Examples: Enron, Tyco, WorldCom, HealthSouth leaders used organizational resources for personal benefit. Hitler convinced followers to pursue a disastrous vision. Charisma can be used for good and for ill. Success depends on the situation, the leader's vision, and organizational checks and balances.

   自恋的魅力型领导者可能让个人目标超越组织目标。示例：安然、泰科、世通、HealthSouth的领导者将组织资源用于个人利益。希特勒说服追随者追求灾难性的愿景。魅力可以用于善也可以用于恶。成功取决于情境、领导者的愿景以及组织制衡。
