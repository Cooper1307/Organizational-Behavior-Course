# 第六章：重视多元化 / Chapter 6: Valuing Diversity

## 第一部分：理论基础 / Part 1: Theoretical Foundations

### 一、多元化概述 / Overview of Diversity

We are, each of us, unique. This is obvious enough, but managers sometimes forget they need to recognize and capitalize on individual differences to get the most from their employees.

我们每一个人都是独特的。这显而易见，但管理者有时会忘记他们需要认识并利用个体差异，以从员工身上获得最大价值。

---

#### 1. 多元化的层次 / Levels of Diversity

Although much has been said about diversity in age, race, gender, ethnicity, religion, and disability status, experts now recognize that these demographic characteristics are just the tip of the diversity iceberg.

虽然关于年龄、种族、性别、民族、宗教和残疾状况的多元化已被广泛讨论，但专家们现在认识到这些人口统计学特征只是多元化冰山的一角。

**Surface-Level Diversity / 表面层次多元化**:

Differences in easily perceived characteristics such as gender, race, ethnicity, age, or disability, that do not necessarily reflect the ways people think or feel but that may activate certain stereotypes.

在容易察觉的特征（如性别、种族、民族、年龄或残疾）方面的差异，这些差异不一定反映人们的思维方式或感受方式，但可能激活某些刻板印象。

> **Examples**: Gender, race, ethnicity, age, disability
> **示例**：性别、种族、民族、年龄、残疾

**Deep-Level Diversity / 深层多元化**:

Differences in values, personality, and work preferences that become progressively more important for determining similarity as people get to know one another better.

在价值观、人格和工作偏好方面的差异，随着人们彼此更好地了解，这些差异在确定相似性方面变得越来越重要。

> **Examples**: Values, personality, work preferences, communication styles
> **示例**：价值观、人格、工作偏好、沟通风格

---

#### 2. 表面与深层多元化的关系 / Relationship Between Surface and Deep-Level Diversity

People are less concerned about demographic differences if they see themselves as sharing more important characteristics, such as personality and values, that represent deep-level diversity.

如果人们发现自己在更重要的特征（如人格和价值观）上相似，这些特征代表深层多元化，那么他们对人口统计学差异的关注就会减少。

> **Key Insight**: Deep-level similarities can overshadow superficial differences.
> **关键洞察**：深层相似性可以掩盖表面差异。

**Example / 示例**:

Philip (young male from East European neighborhood) and Sharon (older woman from rural Manchester) notice surface-level differences in education, ethnicity, regional background, and gender. However, as they get to know each other, they may find they share deep-level similarities: commitment to families, common thinking about work problems, collaborative work style, interest in international assignments.

菲利普（来自东欧社区的年轻男性）和莎伦（来自曼彻斯特农村的年长女性）注意到在教育、民族、地域背景和性别方面的表面差异。然而，随着他们彼此了解，他们可能会发现深层相似性：对家庭的承诺、对工作问题的共同思维方式、协作工作风格、对国际任务的兴趣。

---

### 二、歧视 / Discrimination

To discriminate is to note a difference between things, which in itself isn't necessarily bad. Usually when we talk about discrimination, though, we mean allowing our behavior to be influenced by stereotypes about groups of people.

歧视是注意到事物之间的差异，这本身并不一定是坏事。然而，通常当我们谈论歧视时，我们的意思是让我们的行为受到关于人群的刻板印象的影响。

---

#### 1. 刻板印象威胁 / Stereotype Threat

Stereotype threat describes the degree to which we internally agree with the generally negative stereotyped perceptions of our groups. Along with that comes a fear of being judged when we are identified with the negative connotations of that group.

刻板印象威胁描述了我们内心对群体的普遍负面刻板印象认同的程度。随之而来的是，当我们与该群体的负面含义联系在一起时，害怕被评判的恐惧。

> **Example**: An older worker applying for a job in a predominately millennial-age workforce may assume the interviewer thinks he is out of touch with current trends. What creates stereotype threat is not whether this worker is up to date, but whether he internally agrees that older workers are out of date.
> **示例**：一位年长员工申请以千禧一代为主的 workforce 的工作时，可能假设面试官认为他与当前趋势脱节。造成刻板印象威胁的不是这位员工是否与时俱进，而是他内心是否认同年长员工过时的刻板印象。

**Consequences of Stereotype Threat / 刻板印象威胁的后果**:

- Lower performance / 表现降低
- Lower satisfaction / 满意度降低
- Negative job attitudes / 负面工作态度
- Decreased engagement / 参与度降低
- Decreased motivation / 动机降低
- Higher absenteeism / 缺勤率增加
- More health issues / 更多健康问题
- Higher turnover intentions / 离职意向增加

---

#### 2. 职场歧视的形式 / Forms of Workplace Discrimination

| Type / 类型 | Definition / 定义 | Example / 示例 |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **Discriminatory policies or practices / 歧视性政策或实践** | Actions that deny equal opportunity or unequal rewards for performance | Older workers targeted for layoffs because they are highly paid |
| **Sexual harassment / 性骚扰** | Unwanted sexual advances creating hostile environment | Salespeople visiting strip clubs, bringing strippers to office |
| **Intimidation / 恐吓** | Overt threats or bullying directed at specific groups | Nooses hanging over workstations |
| **Mockery and insults / 嘲笑和侮辱** | Jokes or negative stereotypes | Asking Arab Americans if carrying bombs |
| **Exclusion / 排斥** | Exclusion from opportunities, events, discussions; can be unintentional | Women assigned to marginal job roles |
| **Incivility / 不文明行为** | Disrespectful treatment, interrupting, ignoring opinions | Male attorneys cutting off female colleagues |

**Key Point / 关键点**: Since discrimination has come under legal scrutiny and social disapproval, most overt forms have faded, resulting in an increase in more covert forms like incivility or exclusion.

由于歧视受到法律审查和社会不赞成，大多数公开形式已经消退，导致更隐蔽的形式（如不文明行为或排斥）增加。

---

### 三、传记特征 / Biographical Characteristics

Biographical characteristics such as age, gender, race, ethnicity, and disability are some of the most obvious ways employees differ. These characteristics are representative of surface-level diversity.

年龄、性别、种族、民族和残疾等传记特征是员工差异最明显的方式。这些特征代表表面层次的多元化。

> **General Rule**: Many biographical differences are not important to actual work outcomes, and far more variation occurs within groups sharing biographical characteristics than between them.
> **一般规则**：许多传记差异对实际工作结果不重要，具有相同传记特征的群体内部的差异远大于群体之间的差异。

---

#### 1. 年龄 / Age

The workforce is aging worldwide in most developed countries. In the United States, the proportion of the workforce age 55 and older is 22 percent and increasing.

在大多数发达国家，劳动力正在老龄化。在美国，55岁及以上劳动力的比例为22%且正在增加。

**Stereotypes vs. Reality / 刻板印象与现实**:

| Stereotype / 刻板印象 | Reality / 现实 |
|:---|:---|
| Behind the times, grumpy, inflexible / 落后时代、脾气暴躁、不灵活 | Experience, judgment, strong work ethic, commitment to quality / 经验、判断力、强烈职业道德、质量承诺 |
| Less adaptable and less motivated to learn technology / 适应性较差，学习技术动机较低 | Study shows older workers know better how to avoid severe errors / 研究表明年长员工更知道如何避免严重错误 |
| Creativity diminishes with age / 创造力随年龄减弱 | People who create through experimentation do greatest work in 40s, 50s, 60s / 通过实验创造的人在40、50、60岁做最伟大工作 |

**Age and Job Performance / 年龄与工作表现**:

The majority of studies show "virtually no relationship between age and job performance." Some studies indicate older adults perform better than younger counterparts.

大多数研究表明"年龄与工作表现之间几乎没有关系"。一些研究表明成年人表现比年轻同行更好。

**Age and Job Satisfaction / 年龄与工作满意度**:

Older workers tend to be more satisfied with their work, report better relationships with coworkers, and are more committed to their organizations.

年长员工往往对工作更满意，报告与同事关系更好，对组织更承诺。

---

#### 2. 性别 / Sex

Few—if any—differences between men and women affect job performance. In general, men may have slightly higher math ability and women slightly higher verbal ability, but differences are fairly small.

男女之间影响工作表现的差异很少（如果有的话）。一般来说，男性可能在数学能力上略高，女性在语言能力上略高，但差异相当小。

**Key Findings / 关键发现**:

- One meta-analysis found women scored slightly higher than men on performance measures
  
  一项元分析发现女性在表现测量上得分略高于男性

- A separate meta-analysis of 95 leadership studies indicated men and women are rated equally effective as leaders
  
  对95项领导力研究的单独元分析表明男女被评为同样有效的领导者

**Persistent Biases / 持续的偏见**:

- Men are preferred in hiring for male-dominated occupations, particularly when men are doing the hiring
  
  在男性主导职业的招聘中男性更受青睐，特别是当男性负责招聘时

- Females are less likely to be assigned challenging positions by men
  
  女性较不可能被男性分配具有挑战性的职位

- Men are more likely to be chosen for leadership roles even though equally effective
  
  男性更可能被选为领导角色，尽管同样有效

> **Global Note**: Many countries have laws against sex discrimination, including Australia, the United Kingdom, and the United States.
> **全球提示**：许多国家有反对性别歧视的法律，包括澳大利亚、英国和美国。

---

#### 3. 种族与民族 / Race and Ethnicity

Race is the heritage people use to identify themselves; ethnicity is the additional set of cultural characteristics that often overlaps with race. Typically, we associate race with biology and ethnicity with culture.

种族是人们用来识别自己的遗产；民族是经常与种族重叠的额外文化特征集。通常，我们将种族与生物学联系起来，将民族与文化联系起来。

**Employment Outcomes / 就业结果**:

- Individuals may slightly favor colleagues of their own race in performance evaluations, promotion decisions, and pay raises
  
  个体可能在绩效评估、晋升决定和加薪中稍微偏爱自己种族的同事

- Members of racial and ethnic minorities report higher levels of discrimination in the workplace
  
  种族和民族少数群体成员报告职场中更高水平的歧视

- African Americans generally fare worse than Whites in employment decisions
  
  非裔美国人在就业决定中通常比白人处境更差

> **Shocking Finding**: One study found African American applicants with no criminal history received fewer job offers than White applicants with criminal records.
> **惊人发现**：一项研究发现，没有犯罪记录的非裔美国申请人比有犯罪记录的白人申请人收到更少的录用通知。

---

#### 4. 残疾 / Disabilities

**Scope of Disabilities / 残疾范围**:

Physical or mental impairment that has a substantial and long-term adverse effect on carrying out normal day-to-day activities.

对正常日常活动产生实质性和长期不利影响的身体或精神损伤。

**Impact on Employment Outcomes / 对就业结果的影响**:

- Disabled individuals rated as having superior personal qualities like dependability
  
  残疾个体被评为具有卓越的可靠性等个人品质

- Workers with disabilities receive higher performance evaluations
  
  残疾员工获得更高的绩效评估

- However, individuals with disabilities tend to encounter lower performance expectations and are less likely to be hired
  
  然而，残疾个体往往遇到更低的绩效期望，且较不可能被雇佣

- Mental disabilities may impair performance more than physical disabilities
  
  精神残疾可能比身体残疾对表现的损害更大

---

#### 5. 隐性残疾 / Hidden Disabilities

Hidden disabilities (or invisible disabilities) generally fall under: sensory disabilities (impaired hearing), autoimmune disorders (rheumatoid arthritis), chronic illness or pain (carpal tunnel syndrome), cognitive or learning impairments (ADHD), sleep disorders (insomnia), and psychological challenges (PTSD).

隐性残疾（或不可见残疾）通常属于：感觉残疾（听力受损）、自身免疫疾病（类风湿关节炎）、慢性疾病或疼痛（腕管综合征）、认知或学习障碍（ADHD）、睡眠障碍（失眠）和心理挑战（PTSD）。

**The Disclosure Challenge / 披露挑战**:

Employees must disclose their conditions to be eligible for workplace accommodations. However, many do not want to disclose because they fear being stigmatized or ostracized, and believe managers will think they are less capable.

员工必须披露自己的状况才有资格获得工作场所便利。然而，许多人不想披露，因为他们害怕被污名化或排斥，并认为管理者会认为他们能力较差。

> **Research Finding**: Disclosure helps all—the individual, others, and organizations. Disclosure may increase job satisfaction and well-being, help others understand and assist, and allow organization to accommodate for top performance.
> **研究发现**：披露对所有人都有帮助——个人、他人和组织。披露可能增加工作满意度和幸福感，帮助他人理解和协助，并允许组织适应以实现最佳表现。

---

### 四、其他差异化特征 / Other Differentiating Characteristics

#### 1. 宗教 / Religion

There are few—if any—countries in which religion is a nonissue in the workplace. Employers are prohibited by law from discriminating against employees based on religion in many countries.

很少有（如果有的话）国家宗教在职场中不是问题。许多国家法律禁止雇主基于宗教歧视员工。

> **Complex Example**: Samantha Elauf was turned down for employment because she wears a hijab. She was not aware of the organization's rule against head coverings and did not mention her reason for the scarf. Should employers be required to deduce why applicants dress as they do?
> **复杂示例**：萨曼莎·埃尔法夫因为戴希贾布而被拒绝录用。她不知道组织反对头饰的规定，也没有提及戴围巾的原因。雇主是否应该被要求推断申请人为什么那样穿着？

---

#### 2. 性取向与性别认同 / Sexual Orientation and Gender Identity

The full acceptance and accommodation of LGBT employees remains a work in progress.

对LGBT员工的完全接纳和适应仍在进行中。

**Discrimination Evidence / 歧视证据**:

A Harvard study sent identical resumés to 1,700 job openings. Half mentioned involvement in gay organizations during college. Applications without the mention received 60 percent more callbacks.

哈佛研究向1700个职位发送相同简历。一半提到大学期间参与同性恋组织。未提到的申请收到60%更多的回电。

**Organizational Progress / 组织进展**:

- More than 90 percent of Fortune 500 have policies covering sexual orientation
  
  超过90%的财富500强有涵盖性取向的政策

- In 2001, only 8 companies had policies on gender identity; now more than 250
  
  2001年，只有8家公司有性别认同政策；现在超过250家

---

#### 3. 文化认同 / Cultural Identity

Many people carry a strong cultural identity—a link with the culture of family ancestry that lasts a lifetime, no matter where the individual may live in the world.

许多人有强烈的文化认同——与家族祖先文化的联系持续一生，无论个人可能住在世界何处。

**Organizational Implication / 组织启示**:

Organizations must understand and respect cultural identities. Workplace practices that coincided with cultural norms were commonplace years ago. Today's organizations do well to understand and respect cultural identities, both as groups and as individuals.

组织必须理解和尊重文化认同。与文化规范一致的工作实践在多年前很常见。今天的组织最好理解和尊重文化认同，无论是群体还是个体。

> **Example**: A U.S. company doing business in Latin America needs to understand employees expect long summer holidays. Requiring work during culturally established breaks will meet strong resistance.
> **示例**：在拉丁美洲开展业务的美国公司需要理解员工期望长暑假。要求在文化既定假期工作将遇到强烈抵制。

---

### 五、能力 / Ability

Ability is an individual's current capacity to perform the various tasks of a job. Overall abilities are essentially made up of two sets of factors: intellectual and physical.

能力是个体当前执行工作各项任务的能力。整体能力本质上由两组因素组成：智力和体力。

---

#### 1. 智力能力 / Intellectual Abilities

Intellectual abilities are abilities needed to perform mental activities—thinking, reasoning, and problem solving.

智力能力是执行智力活动所需的能力——思考、推理和解决问题。

**Seven Dimensions of Intellectual Ability / 智力能力的七个维度**:

| Dimension / 维度 | Description / 描述 | Job Example / 工作示例 |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **Number aptitude / 数字能力** | Speedy and accurate arithmetic | Accountant computing sales tax |
| **Verbal comprehension / 语言理解** | Understand what is read or heard | Plant manager following corporate policies |
| **Perceptual speed / 知觉速度** | Identify visual similarities/differences quickly | Fire investigator identifying clues |
| **Inductive reasoning / 归纳推理** | Identify logical sequence and solve problems | Market researcher forecasting demand |
| **Deductive reasoning / 演绎推理** | Use logic and assess implications | Supervisor choosing between suggestions |
| **Spatial visualization / 空间视觉化** | Imagine object in different positions | Interior decorator redecorating office |
| **Memory / 记忆** | Retain and recall past experiences | Salesperson remembering customer names |

---

#### 2. 一般心智能力（GMA）/ General Mental Ability (GMA)

Intelligence dimensions are positively correlated. If you score high on verbal comprehension, you're more likely to also score high on spatial visualization. Researchers recognize a general factor of intelligence: general mental ability (GMA).

智力维度呈正相关。如果你在语言理解上得分高，你更可能在空间视觉化上也得分高。研究者认识到一个一般智力因素：一般心智能力（GMA）。

> **Cross-Cultural Note**: Structures and measures of intellectual abilities can be generalized across cultures. Someone in Venezuela does not have a different set of mental abilities than an American.
> **跨文化提示**：智力能力的结构和测量可以跨文化推广。委内瑞拉的人与美国人没有不同的智力能力集。

**The Wonderlic Test / 沃德利克测试**:

- Widely used in hiring decisions
- 广泛用于招聘决策
- 50 questions, 12 minutes
- 50道题，12分钟
- Measures both speed and power
- 测量速度和能力
- Average score: about 21 of 50
- 平均分：约21/50
- Used by Publix, Manpower, BP, Dish
- 被Publix、Manpower、BP、Dish使用

**Intellectual Ability and Job Satisfaction / 智力能力与工作满意度**:

Intelligence doesn't make people happier or more satisfied. Although intelligent people perform better and tend to have more interesting jobs, they are also more critical when evaluating job conditions. Smart people have it better, but they also expect more.

智力不会让人更快乐或更满意。虽然智力高的人表现更好且往往有更有趣的工作，他们在评估工作条件时也更具批判性。聪明人条件更好，但他们也期望更多。

---

#### 3. 体力能力 / Physical Abilities

Physical abilities have been and will remain valuable. Research has identified nine basic abilities needed in the performance of physical tasks.

体力能力已经并且将继续有价值。研究已识别出执行体力任务所需的九种基本能力。

**Nine Types of Physical Abilities / 九种体力能力类型**:

**Strength Factors / 力量因素**:
1. Dynamic strength / 动态力量: Exert muscular force repeatedly or continuously over time
2. Trunk strength / 躯干力量: Exert muscular strength using trunk muscles
3. Static strength / 静态力量: Exert force against external objects
4. Explosive strength / 爆发力: Expend maximum energy in explosive acts

**Flexibility Factors / 灵活性因素**:
5. Extent flexibility / 程度灵活性: Move trunk and back muscles as far as possible
6. Dynamic flexibility / 动态灵活性: Make rapid, repeated flexing movements

**Other Factors / 其他因素**:
7. Body coordination / 身体协调: Coordinate simultaneous actions of different body parts
8. Balance / 平衡: Maintain equilibrium despite forces pulling off balance
9. Stamina / 耐力: Continue maximum effort requiring prolonged effort over time

> **Key Principle**: High employee performance is likely when the extent to which a job requires each of the nine abilities matches the abilities of employees in that job.
> **关键原则**：当工作要求九种能力的程度与该岗位员工的能力匹配时，可能实现高员工表现。

---

### 六、实施多元化策略 / Implementing Diversity Management Strategies

Discrimination leads to increased turnover, which is detrimental to organizational performance. An individual of minority status is much less likely to leave if there is a feeling of inclusiveness, known as a positive diversity climate.

歧视导致离职增加，这对组织表现有害。如果有包容感（称为积极的多元化氛围），少数群体个体不太可能离职。

**Positive Diversity Climate / 积极多元化氛围**:

In an organization, an environment of inclusiveness and an acceptance of diversity. A positive climate for diversity can also lead to increased sales.

在组织中，包容和接受多元化的环境。积极的多元化氛围也可能导致销售增加。

---

#### 1. 多元化管理的定义 / Definition of Diversity Management

The process and programs by which managers make everyone more aware of and sensitive to the needs and differences of others.

管理者使每个人更了解并对他人需求和差异更敏感的过程和计划。

> **Key Point**: Diversity programs include and are meant for everyone, regardless of characteristics and varying specific abilities.
> **关键点**：多元化计划包括且旨在为所有人，无论特征和具体能力如何。

---

#### 2. 吸引、选择、发展和保留多元化员工 / Attracting, Selecting, Developing, and Retaining Diverse Employees

**Attracting / 吸引**:

Target recruitment messages to specific demographic groups that are underrepresented. Women and minorities have greater interest in employers that highlight commitment to diversity in recruiting materials.

针对代表性不足的特定人口群体的招聘信息。女性和少数群体对招聘材料中强调多元化承诺的雇主更感兴趣。

**Selecting / 选择**:

Hiring managers need to value fairness and objectivity. When using well-defined protocols for assessing talent, qualifications become far more important than demographic characteristics.

招聘经理需要重视公平和客观。使用明确的协议评估人才时，资历变得远比人口特征重要。

**Developing and Retaining / 发展和保留**:

Individuals demographically different from coworkers may feel lower commitment and leave, but a positive diversity climate can aid retention. Best programs are inclusive in design and implementation.

与同事人口不同的个体可能感到较低承诺并离职，但积极的多元化氛围可以帮助保留。最佳计划在设计和实施上都是包容性的。

---

#### 3. 群体中的多元化 / Diversity in Groups

Whether diverse or homogeneous teams are more effective depends on the characteristic of interest.

多元化团队还是同质团队更有效取决于感兴趣的特征。

**When Diversity Hurts / 多元化有害时**:

- Diversity in intelligence, conscientiousness, teamwork interest is likely bad
  
  智力、尽责性、团队合作兴趣的多元化可能是坏事

- It makes little sense to mix members lower in these variables
  
  混合这些变量较低的成员意义不大

**When Diversity Helps / 多元化有益时**:

- Groups with different types of expertise and education are more effective
  
  具有不同类型专业知识和教育的群体更有效

- Mix of leaders and followers is more effective than all leaders or all followers
  
  领导者和追随者的混合比全是领导者或全是追随者更有效

> **Most Important Factor**: Emphasize the similarities among members. Managers who emphasize higher-order goals and values are more effective in managing diverse teams.
> **最重要因素**：强调成员之间的相似性。强调高阶目标和价值观的管理者在管理多元化团队时更有效。

#### 4. 多元化计划 / Diversity Programs

Effective, comprehensive workforce programs encouraging diversity have three distinct components:

有效的、全面的鼓励多元化的劳动力计划有三个不同的组成部分：

1. **Legal Framework / 法律框架**: Teach managers about legal framework for equal employment opportunity and encourage fair treatment
  
  **法律框架**：教授管理者平等就业机会的法律框架，鼓励公平对待所有人

2. **Business Case / 商业案例**: Teach managers how diverse workforce better serves diverse market of customers and clients
  
  **商业案例**：教授管理者多元化劳动力如何更好地服务多元化的客户市场

3. **Personal Development / 个人发展**: Foster practices that bring out skills and abilities of all workers, acknowledging differences in perspective can improve performance
  
  **个人发展**：培养发挥所有工人技能和能力的实践，承认视角差异可以改善表现

**8. 群体多元化有条件地有效 / Group Diversity Is Conditionally Effective**

Hurts when: diversity in intelligence, conscientiousness

有害时：智力、尽责性的多元化

Helps when: diversity in expertise, education, leader-follower mix

有益时：专业知识、教育、领导-追随者混合的多元化

> Most important: emphasize similarities among members
> 最重要：强调成员之间的相似性

---

**9. 多元化计划有三个组成部分 / Diversity Programs Have Three Components**

1. Legal framework: teach equal employment opportunity
   
   法律框架：教授平等就业机会

2. Business case: diverse workforce serves diverse market
   
   商业案例：多元化劳动力服务多元化市场

3. Personal development: foster all workers' skills
   
   个人发展：培养所有工人技能

---

**10. 积极多元化氛围是关键 / Positive Diversity Climate Is Key**

Environment of inclusiveness and acceptance → increased sales, reduced turnover, better performance

包容和接受的环境→销售增加、离职减少、表现改善

> Diversity programs include and are meant for everyone.
> 多元化计划包括且旨在为所有人。
