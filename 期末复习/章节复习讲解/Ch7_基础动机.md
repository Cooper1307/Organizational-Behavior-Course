# 第七章：基础动机 / Chapter 7: Basic Motivation

## 第一部分：理论基础 / Part 1: Theoretical Foundations

### 一、动机概述 / Overview of Motivation

We define motivation as the processes that account for an individual's intensity, direction, and persistence of effort toward attaining a goal.

我们将动机定义为解释个体为实现目标而付出的努力强度、方向和持久性的过程。

---

#### 动机的三要素 / Three Elements of Motivation

| Element / 要素 | Definition / 定义 | Key Point / 要点 |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **Intensity / 强度** | How hard a person tries | Most focus of motivation discussions, but high intensity alone is insufficient |
| **Direction / 方向** | Effort channeled toward organizational goals | Quality of effort matters; must benefit organization |
| **Persistence / 持久性** | How long a person maintains effort | Motivated individuals stay with tasks long enough to achieve goals |

> **Key Insight**: High intensity is unlikely to lead to favorable job-performance outcomes unless effort is channeled in a direction that benefits the organization.
> **关键洞察**：除非努力被引导到有利于组织的方向，否则高强度不太可能导致有利的工作绩效结果。

---

### 二、早期动机理论 / Early Theories of Motivation

Three theories formulated during the 1950s are probably the best known. Although they are now of questionable validity, they represent a foundation and practicing managers still use their terminology.

20世纪50年代提出的三种理论可能是最著名的。虽然它们现在的有效性受到质疑，但它们代表了基础，实践中的管理者仍在使用它们的术语。

---

#### 1. 需求层次理论 / Hierarchy of Needs Theory

Abraham Maslow's hierarchy hypothesizes that within every human being there is a hierarchy of five needs. As each need becomes substantially satisfied, the next one becomes dominant.

亚伯拉罕·马斯洛的需求层次假设每个人内心都有五种需求的层次。当每种需求被实质性满足后，下一个需求变得主导。

**Five Needs / 五种需求**:

| Level / 层次 | Need / 需求 | Description / 描述 | Example / 示例 |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| 1 | **Physiological / 生理** | Hunger, thirst, shelter, sex, bodily needs | Basic salary for food and housing |
| 2 | **Safety-Security / 安全** | Protection from physical and emotional harm | Job security, health insurance |
| 3 | **Social-Belongingness / 社交** | Affection, belongingness, acceptance, friendship | Team activities, friendly coworkers |
| 4 | **Esteem / 尊重** | Internal (self-respect, autonomy) and external (status, recognition) | Recognition awards, job titles |
| 5 | **Self-Actualization / 自我实现** | Drive to become what we are capable of; growth, potential, self-fulfillment | Challenging work, creative opportunities |

> **Recently proposed**: A sixth need—intrinsic values—has been proposed for the highest level, but has yet to gain widespread acceptance.
> **最近提出**：第六种需求——内在价值观——已被提议为最高层次，但尚未获得广泛接受。

**Research Support / 研究支持**:

Maslow's theory has received long-standing wide recognition, particularly among practicing managers. It is intuitively logical and easy to understand. Unfortunately, however, most research has not validated it, especially when applied to diverse cultures, with the possible exception of physiological needs.

马斯洛的理论获得了长期的广泛认可，特别是在实践管理者中。它直观逻辑且易于理解。然而不幸的是，大多数研究没有验证它，特别是应用于多元文化时，生理需求可能是例外。

---

#### 2. 双因素理论 / Two-Factor Theory

Frederick Herzberg asked people to describe situations in which they felt exceptionally good or bad about their jobs. The responses led him to his two-factor theory (also called motivation-hygiene theory).

弗雷德里克·赫茨伯格要求人们描述他们对工作感觉特别好或特别坏的情况。这些回应导致他提出双因素理论（也称为动机-保健理论）。

**Two Categories of Factors / 两类因素**:

| Factor Type / 因素类型 | Also Called / 也称 | Description / 描述 | Examples / 示例 |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| **Hygiene Factors / 保健因素** | Extrinsic factors / 外在因素 | When adequate, people will NOT be dissatisfied; but neither will they be satisfied | Quality of supervision, pay, company policies, physical work conditions, relationships, job security |
| **Motivators / 激励因素** | Intrinsic factors / 内在因素 | Create satisfaction and motivate people | Promotional opportunities, personal growth, recognition, responsibility, achievement |

**The Dual Continuum / 双重连续体**:

Herzberg proposed that the opposite of "satisfaction" is "no satisfaction," and the opposite of "dissatisfaction" is "no dissatisfaction."

赫茨伯格提出"满意"的对立面是"没有满意"，"不满意"的对立面是"没有不满意"。

| State / 状态 | Description / 描述 |
|:---|:---|
| Satisfaction / 满意 | Motivators present / 激励因素存在 |
| No Satisfaction / 没有满意 | Motivators absent / 激励因素缺失 |
| Dissatisfaction / 不满意 | Hygiene factors absent / 保健因素缺失 |
| No Dissatisfaction / 没有不满意 | Hygiene factors present / 保健因素存在 |

> **Key Insight**: To motivate people, emphasize factors associated with the work itself (motivators), not just hygiene factors.
> **关键洞察**：要激励人们，强调与工作本身相关的因素（激励因素），而不仅仅是保健因素。

**Research Support / 研究支持**:

Two-factor theory has not been well supported in research. Criticisms center on Herzberg's original methodology and assumptions. However, the theory has been quite influential and is currently very much in use in research in Asia.

双因素理论在研究中没有得到很好的支持。批评集中在赫茨伯格的原始方法和假设上。然而，该理论相当有影响力，目前在亚洲研究中广泛使用。

---

#### 3. 麦克利兰的需求理论 / McClelland's Theory of Needs

Developed by David McClelland, these needs are more like motivating factors than strict needs for survival.

由大卫·麦克利兰提出，这些需求更像是激励因素而非严格的生存需求。

**Three Needs / 三种需求**:

| Need / 需求 | Definition / 定义 | Characteristics / 特征 |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **Need for Achievement (nAch) / 成就需求** | The drive to excel, to achieve in relationship to a set of standards, and to strive to succeed | Best at 50-50 chance of success; dislike gambling (high odds) and low odds (no challenge); like goals requiring stretching |
| **Need for Power (nPow) / 权力需求** | The need to make others behave in a way they would not have otherwise | Want to influence and control others |
| **Need for Affiliation (nAff) / 亲和需求** | The desire for friendly and close interpersonal relationships | Want to be liked and accepted |

**High Achievers / 高成就者**:

High achievers perform best when they perceive their probability of success as 0.5—that is, a 50-50 chance. They dislike gambling with high odds because they get no achievement satisfaction from success that comes by pure chance. Similarly, they dislike low odds (high probability of success) because then there is no challenge to their skills.

高成就者在他们感知成功概率为0.5——即50-50机会时表现最好。他们不喜欢高赔率的赌博，因为他们从纯粹运气的成功中得不到成就满足感。同样，他们不喜欢低赔率（高成功概率），因为那对他们的技能没有挑战。

**nAch and Management / 成就需求与管理**:

High nAch does not necessarily make someone a good manager, especially in large organizations. People with high nAch are interested in how well they do personally, not in influencing others to do well.

高成就需求不一定使人成为好管理者，特别是在大组织中。高成就需求者关心个人表现，不关心影响他人表现。

**Most Effective Leaders / 最有效的领导者**:

The most effective leaders are high in nPow and nAff—the "rough edges" of nPow may be tempered by the nAff desire to be included.

最有效的领导者具有高权力需求和高亲和需求——权力需求的"粗糙边缘"可能被亲和需求的包容欲望所缓和。

**Cultural Characteristics / 文化特征**:

High nAch as internal motivator presupposes: (1) willingness to accept moderate degree of risk (excludes strong uncertainty-avoidance countries), and (2) concern with performance (applies to strong achievement countries). This combination is found predominantly in Anglo-American countries (U.S., Canada, Great Britain), much less in collectivistic countries like Chile and Portugal.

高成就需求作为内部激励者假设：（1）愿意接受中等程度的风险（排除强不确定性规避国家），（2）关注绩效（适用于强成就特征国家）。这种组合主要存在于盎格鲁-美洲国家（美国、加拿大、英国），在智利和葡萄牙等集体主义国家较少。

### 三、当代动机理论 / Contemporary Theories of Motivation

Contemporary theories have one thing in common: each has a reasonable degree of valid supporting documentation. We call them "contemporary" because they represent the latest thinking in explaining employee motivation.

当代理论有一个共同点：每种都有合理程度的有效支持文件。我们称它们为"当代"因为它们代表解释员工动机的最新思维。

---

#### 1. 自我决定理论 / Self-Determination Theory

Self-determination theory proposes that people prefer to feel they have control over their actions, so anything that makes a previously enjoyed task feel more like an obligation than a freely chosen activity will undermine motivation.

自我决定理论提出人们喜欢感觉他们对行为有控制，所以任何使以前享受的任务感觉更像义务而非自由选择活动的东西都会削弱动机。

**Example / 示例**:

Marcia started work at the Humane Society as a volunteer, working 15 hours a week and loving it. Then they hired her full-time at $11 an hour, doing the same work. But she's not finding it as much fun. Why? Because the task now feels more like an obligation.

玛西娅开始在动物保护协会做志愿者，每周工作15小时并乐在其中。然后他们以每小时11美元雇佣她全职，做同样的工作。但她不再觉得那么有趣。为什么？因为任务现在感觉更像义务。

---

#### 2. 认知评价理论 / Cognitive Evaluation Theory

A version of self-determination theory hypothesizing that extrinsic rewards will reduce intrinsic interest in a task. When people are paid for work, it feels less like something they want to do and more like something they have to do.

自我决定理论的一个版本假设外在奖励会减少对任务的内在兴趣。当人们因工作获得报酬时，感觉更不像他们想做的事情，而更像他们必须做的事情。

> **Key Finding**: One meta-analysis confirmed that intrinsic motivation contributes to the quality of work, while incentives contribute to the quantity of work.
> **关键发现**：一项元分析证实内在动机有助于工作质量，而激励有助于工作数量。

---

#### 3. 自我一致性 / Self-Concordance

Self-concordance considers how strongly people's reasons for pursuing goals are consistent with their interests and core values. People who pursue work goals for intrinsic reasons are more satisfied with their jobs, feel they fit into their organizations better, and perform better.

自我一致性考虑人们追求目标的原因与他们的兴趣和核心价值观的一致性有多强。因内在原因追求工作目标的人对工作更满意，感觉他们更适合组织，表现更好。

> **Key Insight**: Across cultures, if individuals pursue goals because of intrinsic interest, they are more likely to attain goals, are happier when they do, and are happy even if they do not—because the process of striving toward goals is fun whether or not the goal is achieved.
> **关键洞察**：跨文化地，如果个体因内在兴趣追求目标，他们更可能达成目标，达成时更快乐，即使未达成也快乐——因为为目标奋斗的过程是有趣的，无论目标是否达成。

---

#### 4. 目标设置理论 / Goal-Setting Theory

Proposed by Edwin Locke, goal-setting theory reveals the impressive effects of goal specificity, challenge, and feedback on performance. Under the theory, intentions to work toward a goal are considered a major source of work motivation.

由埃德温·洛克提出的目标设置理论揭示了目标具体性、挑战性和反馈对表现的显著影响。在该理论下，为实现目标工作的意图被认为是工作动机的重大来源。

**Three Key Dimensions / 三个关键维度**:

| Dimension / 维度 | Effect / 效果 | Evidence / 证据 |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **Specific goals / 具体目标** | Increase performance | Specific goals increase performance more than vague "do your best" goals |
| **Difficult goals / 困难目标** | Produce higher performance (when accepted) | Difficult goals, when accepted, produce higher performances than easy goals |
| **Feedback / 反馈** | Leads to higher performance | Feedback leads to higher performance than non-feedback; self-generated feedback is most powerful |

**Self-Generated Feedback / 自我生成反馈**:

Self-generated feedback—with which employees are able to monitor their own progress or receive feedback from the task process itself—is more powerful than externally generated feedback.

自我生成反馈——员工能够监控自己进度或从任务过程本身获得反馈——比外部生成的反馈更强大。

---

**影响目标-表现关系的三个个人因素 / Three Personal Factors Influencing Goals-Performance Relationship**:

**1. Goal Commitment / 目标承诺**:

Goal-setting theory assumes an individual is committed to the goal and determined not to lower or abandon it. The individual (1) believes he or she can achieve the goal and (2) wants to achieve it.

目标设置理论假设个体承诺于目标并决心不降低或放弃它。个体（1）相信他能实现目标，（2）想要实现它。

> **Goal commitment is most likely when**: Goals are made public; individual has internal locus of control; goals are self-set rather than assigned; goals are based at least partially on individual ability.
> **目标承诺最可能当**：目标公开；个体有内部控制点；目标是自我设置而非分配；目标至少部分基于个体能力。

**2. Task Characteristics / 任务特征**:

Goals affect performance more strongly when tasks are simple rather than complex, well learned rather than novel, independent rather than interdependent, and on the high end of achievable. On interdependent tasks, group goals are more effective.

当任务简单而非复杂、熟练掌握而非新颖、独立而非相互依赖、处于可实现的最高端时，目标对表现的影响更强。在相互依赖的任务上，群体目标更有效。

**3. National Culture Factors / 民族文化因素**:

In collectivistic and high power-distance cultures, achievable moderate goals can be more motivating than difficult ones.

在集体主义和高权力距离文化中，可实现的中等目标可能比困难目标更有激励作用。

---

**提升焦点与预防焦点 / Promotion Focus and Prevention Focus**:

| Focus / 焦点 | Strategy / 策略 | Example / 示例 |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **Promotion Focus / 提升焦点** | Striving for goals through advancement and accomplishment | Reading class materials for exam |
| **Prevention Focus / 预防焦点** | Striving for goals by fulfilling duties and obligations, avoiding conditions that pull away from goals | Refraining from playing video games to study |

> **Important Note**: A person's job satisfaction will be more heavily impacted by failure when that person has an avoidance (prevention) outlook.
> **重要提示**：当个体有回避（预防）心态时，他的工作满意度将更严重地受到失败的影响。

---

**目标设置实施 / Goal-Setting Implementation**:

**Management by Objectives (MBO) / 目标管理**:

MBO emphasizes participatively set goals that are tangible, verifiable, and measurable. The organization's overall objectives are translated into specific cascading objectives for each level (divisional, departmental, individual).

MBO强调参与设置的目标，这些目标是具体的、可验证的和可衡量的。组织的总体目标被转化为每个层级（部门、科室、个人）的具体递进目标。

> **When MBO fails**: The culprits tend to be unrealistic expectations, lack of commitment by top management, and inability or unwillingness to allocate rewards based on goal accomplishment.
> **当MBO失败时**：元凶往往是不切实际的期望、高层管理缺乏承诺、以及无法或不愿基于目标达成分配奖励。

**Goal Setting and Ethics / 目标设置与伦理**:

The relationship between goal setting and ethics is complex. If we emphasize attainment of goals, what is the cost? When money is tied to goal attainment, we may focus on getting the money and become willing to compromise ourselves ethically.

目标设置与伦理的关系很复杂。如果我们强调目标达成，代价是什么？当金钱与目标达成绑定时，我们可能专注于获取金钱并愿意在伦理上妥协。

---

#### 5. 自我效能理论 / Self-Efficacy Theory

Self-efficacy theory (also known as social cognitive theory or social learning theory) refers to an individual's belief that he or she is capable of performing a task. The higher your self-efficacy, the more confidence you have in your ability to succeed.

自我效能理论（也称为社会认知理论或社会学习理论）指个体相信自己有能力执行任务。自我效能越高，你对成功能力越有信心。

> **Key Finding**: In difficult situations, people with low self-efficacy are more likely to lessen their effort or give up altogether, while those with high self-efficacy will try harder to master the challenge.
> **关键发现**：在困难情境中，低自我效能的人更可能减少努力或完全放弃，而高自我效能的人会更努力掌握挑战。

**Self-Efficacy Creates a Positive Spiral / 自我效能创造正向螺旋**:

Those with high efficacy become more engaged in their tasks → increase performance → increases efficacy further → higher level of focused attention → increased task performance.

高效能者更投入任务→提高表现→进一步增加效能→更高水平的专注注意力→增加任务表现。

---

**提高自我效能的四种方法 / Four Ways to Increase Self-Efficacy**:

| Method / 方法 | Description / 描述 | Importance / 重要性 |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **1. Enactive Mastery / 实践掌握** | Gaining relevant experience with the task or job | MOST IMPORTANT / 最重要 |
| **2. Vicarious Modeling / 替代建模** | Becoming more confident because you see someone else doing the task; most effective when you see yourself as similar to the person | Second source / 第二来源 |
| **3. Verbal Persuasion / 言语说服** | Becoming more confident when someone convinces you that you have the skills necessary; motivational speakers use this tactic | Third source / 第三来源 |
| **4. Arousal / 唤醒** | Leads to energized state, get "psyched up"; but if task requires steady, lower-key perspective, arousal may hurt performance | Fourth source / 第四来源 |

**皮格马利翁效应 / The Pygmalion Effect**:

A form of self-fulfilling prophecy in which believing something can make it true. What one person expects can come to serve a self-fulfilling prophecy.

一种自我实现预言的形式，相信某事能使其成真。一个人期望的可以成为自我实现预言。

> **Example**: Teachers were told their students had very high IQ scores (when they actually spanned a range). Teachers spent more time with "smart" students, gave challenging assignments, expected more → higher student self-efficacy and better achievement outcomes.
> **示例**：教师被告知他们的学生有很高的智商（实际上范围很广）。教师花更多时间与"聪明"学生在一起，给予挑战性作业，期望更多→更高的学生自我效能和更好的成就结果。

---

#### 6. 强化理论 / Reinforcement Theory

Reinforcement theory takes a behavioristic view, arguing that reinforcement conditions behavior. Reinforcement theorists see behavior as environmentally caused. What controls behavior are reinforcers—any consequences that, when they immediately follow responses, increase the probability that the behavior will be repeated.

强化理论采取行为主义观点，认为强化塑造行为。强化理论家认为行为由环境引起。控制行为的是强化物——任何在反应之后立即出现的结果，增加行为重复的概率。

> **Key Point**: Reinforcement theory ignores the inner state of the individual and concentrates solely on what happens when he or she takes some action. Because it is not concerned with what initiates behavior, it is not, strictly speaking, a theory of motivation.
> **关键点**：强化理论忽略个体的内在状态，专注于当他或她采取某种行动时发生的事情。因为它不关心什么引发行为，严格来说，它不是动机理论。

---

**操作性条件反射/行为主义 / Operant Conditioning/Behaviorism**:

Operant conditioning theory argues that people learn to behave a certain way to either get something they want or to avoid something they don't want. B.F. Skinner demonstrated that:

操作性条件反射理论认为人们学会以某种方式行为以获取想要的或避免不想要的。B.F.斯金纳证明：

1. People will most likely engage in desired behaviors if positively reinforced
   
   如果得到正强化，人们最可能参与期望行为

2. Rewards are most effective if they immediately follow the desired response
   
   如果奖励立即跟随期望反应，奖励最有效

3. Behavior that is not rewarded, or is punished, is less likely to be repeated
   
   不奖励或惩罚的行为不太可能重复

**Behaviorism / 行为主义**:

Argues that behavior follows stimuli in a relatively unthinking manner. Skinner's form of radical behaviorism rejects feelings, thoughts, and other states of mind as causes of behavior.

认为行为以相对不假思索的方式跟随刺激。斯金纳的激进行为主义形式拒绝感受、思想和其他心理状态作为行为的原因。

---

**社会学习理论 / Social-Learning Theory**:

The view that we can learn through both observation and direct experience. Individuals can learn by being told or by observing what happens to other people, as well as through direct experience.

我们可以通过观察和直接经验来学习的观点。个体可以通过被告知或观察他人发生的事情，以及通过直接经验来学习。

> **Key Extension**: Social-learning theory assumes behavior is a function of consequences, but also acknowledges the effects of observational learning and perception. People respond to the way they perceive and define consequences, not to the objective consequences themselves.
> **关键扩展**：社会学习理论假设行为是结果的函数，但也承认观察学习和知觉的效果。人们对他们感知和定义结果的方式做出反应，而不是对客观结果本身。

---

#### 7. 公平理论/组织公正 / Equity Theory/Organizational Justice

According to equity theory, employees compare what they get from their jobs (outcomes such as pay, promotions, recognition, or a bigger office) to what they put into it (inputs such as effort, experience, and education).

根据公平理论，员工比较他们从工作中得到的（结果如薪酬、晋升、认可或更大的办公室）与投入的（投入如努力、经验和教育）。

**The Equity Ratio / 公平比率**:

Employees take the ratio of their Outcomes (O) to their Inputs (I) and compare it to the ratio of others, usually someone similar like a coworker.

员工将他们的结果（O）与投入（I）的比率与他人的比率进行比较，通常是像同事这样的相似人。

| Ratio Comparison / 比率比较 | Perception / 感知 |
|:---|:---|
| My O/I = Other's O/I | Equity / 公平 |
| My O/I < Other's O/I | Inequity due to being underrewarded / 因奖励不足而不公平 |
| My O/I > Other's O/I | Inequity due to being overrewarded / 因奖励过度而不公平 |

---

**员工感知不公平时的六种选择 / Six Choices When Employees Perceive Inequity**:

1. **Change inputs** (exert less effort if underpaid or more if overpaid)
   
   **改变投入**（如果薪酬不足则减少努力，如果薪酬过高则增加努力）

2. **Change outcomes** (individuals paid on piece-rate basis can increase pay by producing higher quantity of lower quality)
   
   **改变结果**（计件工资的员工可以通过生产更多低质量产品来增加收入）

3. **Distort perceptions of self** ("I used to think I worked at moderate pace, but now I realize I work harder than everyone else")
   
   **扭曲自我知觉**（"我以前以为我以中等速度工作，但现在我意识到我比所有人都努力"）

4. **Distort perceptions of others** ("Mike's job isn't as desirable as I thought")
   
   **扭曲他人知觉**（"迈克的工作不如我想象的那么好"）

5. **Choose a different referent** ("I may not make as much as my brother-in-law, but I'm doing better than my Dad did at my age")
   
   **选择不同的参照对象**（"我可能不如我姐夫赚得多，但我比爸爸在我这个年纪时做得好"）

6. **Leave the field** (quit the job)
   
   **离开领域**（辞职）

---

**组织公正的四种类型 / Four Types of Organizational Justice**:

| Type / 类型 | Definition / 定义 | Focus / 焦点 |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **Distributive Justice / 分配公正** | Perceived fairness of the amount and allocation of rewards among individuals | What outcomes are allocated / 分配什么结果 |
| **Procedural Justice / 程序公正** | Perceived fairness of the process used to determine the distribution of rewards | How outcomes are allocated / 如何分配结果 |
| **Informational Justice / 信息公正** | Degree to which employees are provided truthful explanations for decisions | Explanations for decisions / 决策解释 |
| **Interpersonal Justice / 人际公正** | Degree to which employees are treated with dignity and respect | Treatment in interactions / 互动中的对待 |

**Distributive Justice / 分配公正**:

Employees tend to perceive their outcomes are fairest when they are distributed equitably. However, differentiating pay based on inputs (performance) can attract better players, make them stay, and increase team performance.

员工倾向于认为结果在公平分配时最公平。然而，基于投入（表现）区分薪酬可以吸引更好的员工，使他们留下，并增加团队表现。

**Procedural Justice / 程序公正**:

Employees perceive procedures are fairer when they are given a say in the decision-making process. Having direct influence or at least being able to present our opinion creates a sense of control and helps us feel empowered.

当员工在决策过程中有发言权时，他们认为程序更公平。有直接影响或至少能够表达我们的意见创造控制感，帮助我们感到被赋权。

> **Key Insight**: When outcomes are unfavorable, people pay close attention to the process. If the process is judged fair, employees are more accepting of unfavorable outcomes.
> **关键洞察**：当结果不利时，人们密切关注过程。如果过程被判断为公平，员工更接受不利结果。

**Informational Justice / 信息公正**:

The more detailed and candid managers are with employees, the more fairly treated those employees feel. Explanations for bad news are beneficial when they take the form of excuses ("I wanted to give you the office, but it wasn't my decision") rather than justifications ("I decided to give the office to Sam, but having it isn't a big deal").

管理者对员工越详细和坦率，员工感觉越被公平对待。对坏消息的解释在采取借口形式时是有益的（"我想给你办公室，但不是我的决定"）而非理由（"我决定给山姆办公室，但有没有没什么大不了的"）。

**Interpersonal Justice / 人际公正**:

Unique in that it can occur in everyday interactions between managers and employees. Managers can take advantage of (or miss out on) opportunities to make employees feel fairly treated through everyday politeness and respect.

独特之处在于它可以在管理者和员工之间的日常互动中发生。管理者可以通过日常礼貌和尊重来利用（或错失）让员工感觉被公平对待的机会。

---

**公正的结果 / Justice Outcomes**:

When employees feel fairly treated, they respond positively:

当员工感觉被公平对待时，他们积极回应：

- Higher levels of task performance and citizenship behaviors (helping coworkers)
  
  更高水平的任务表现和公民行为（帮助同事）

- Lower levels of counterproductive behaviors (shirking job duties)
  
  更低水平的反生产行为（逃避工作职责）

- Distributive and procedural justice more strongly associated with task performance
  
  分配公正和程序公正与任务表现更强烈相关

- Informational and interpersonal justice more strongly associated with citizenship behavior
  
  信息公正和人际公正与公民行为更强烈相关

- Even physiological outcomes (sleep quality, health) linked to fair treatment
  
  甚至生理结果（睡眠质量、健康）与公平对待相关

---

**文化与公正 / Culture and Justice**:

Across nations, the same basic principles of procedural justice are respected—workers around the world prefer rewards based on performance and skills over rewards based on seniority.

跨国家，相同的程序公正基本原则受到尊重——全球员工更喜欢基于绩效和技能的奖励而非基于资历的奖励。

> **Justice perceptions are most important in countries with**: individualistic, feminine, uncertainty-avoidance, and low power-distance values.
> **公正感知在以下国家最重要**：个人主义、女性化、不确定性规避和低权力距离价值观的国家。

| Culture / 文化 | Justice Approach / 公正方法 |
|:---|:---|
| **High individualism (Australia, U.S.) / 高个人主义** | Competitive pay plans and rewards for superior individual performance |
| **High uncertainty avoidance (France) / 高不确定性规避** | Fixed-pay compensation and employee participation |
| **High femininity (Sweden) / 高女性化** | Work-life balance initiatives and social recognition |
| **Low power-distance (Austria) / 低权力距离** | Ethical concerns foremost; openly justify inequality between leaders and workers |

#### 8. 期望理论 / Expectancy Theory

Victor Vroom's expectancy theory argues that the strength of our tendency to act a certain way depends on the strength of our expectation of a given outcome and its attractiveness.

维克多·弗鲁姆的期望理论认为我们按某种方式行动的倾向强度取决于我们对特定结果的期望强度和其吸引力。

**Three Relationships / 三种关系**:

| Relationship / 关系 | Definition / 定义 | Question / 问题 |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **1. Effort-Performance / 努力-绩效** | Probability perceived by individual that exerting given amount of effort will lead to performance | "If I try hard, will I perform well?" / "如果我努力，我会表现好吗？" |
| **2. Performance-Reward / 绩效-奖励** | Degree to which individual believes performing at particular level will lead to desired outcome | "If I perform well, will I be rewarded?" / "如果我表现好，我会得到奖励吗？" |
| **3. Rewards-Personal Goals / 奖励-个人目标** | Degree to which organizational rewards satisfy individual's personal goals or needs and attractiveness of those rewards | "Will the rewards satisfy my personal goals?" / "奖励会满足我的个人目标吗？" |

> **Key Insight**: Employees are motivated when they believe (1) effort leads to good performance, (2) good performance leads to rewards, and (3) rewards satisfy personal goals.
> **关键洞察**：当员工相信（1）努力带来好表现，（2）好表现带来奖励，（3）奖励满足个人目标时，他们有动力。

**Practical Application / 实际应用**:

Stock analysts place few sell ratings because the expected outcome of buy ratings is higher. Companies love buy ratings but hate sell ratings. Analysts face risks from sell ratings (public rebuke, professional blackballing, exclusion from information) but no such trade-off from buy ratings.

股票分析师很少给出卖出评级，因为买入评级的预期结果更高。公司喜欢买入评级但讨厌卖出评级。分析师面临卖出评级的风险（公开谴责、职业排斥、信息排除），但买入评级没有这样的权衡。

---

### 四、工作投入 / Job Engagement

Job engagement is the investment of an employee's physical, cognitive, and emotional energies into job performance.

工作投入是员工将身体、认知和情感能量投入到工作表现中。

**Research Findings / 研究发现**:

Groups with more engaged employees have:

有更多投入员工的群体有：

- Higher levels of productivity / 更高水平的生产力
- Fewer safety incidents / 更少的安全事故
- Lower turnover / 更低的离职率
- Higher task performance and organizational citizenship behavior (OCB) / 更高的任务表现和组织公民行为

**What Increases Engagement? / 什么增加投入？**:

1. Employee believes it is meaningful to engage in work (determined by job characteristics and access to sufficient resources)
   
   员工相信投入工作是有意义的（由工作特征和获得充足资源决定）

2. Match between individual's values and those of the organization
   
   个体价值观与组织价值观匹配

3. Leadership behaviors that inspire workers to a greater sense of mission
   
   激发员工更大使命感的领导行为

### 五、整合当代动机理论 / Integrating Contemporary Theories

Many theories in this chapter are complementary. The basic foundation is the expectancy model.

本章中许多理论是互补的。基本基础是期望模型。

**How Theories Connect / 理论如何连接**:

- **Goal-setting theory**: Goals direct behavior; clear and difficult goals increase effort
   
  **目标设置理论**：目标指导行为；清晰且困难的目标增加努力

- **Self-efficacy theory**: Setting difficult goals communicates confidence, increasing self-efficacy
   
  **自我效能理论**：设置困难目标传达信心，增加自我效能

- **Reinforcement theory**: Rewards reinforce good performance
   
  **强化理论**：奖励强化良好表现

- **Equity theory/Organizational justice**: Individuals judge favorability of outcomes relative to others and how they are treated
   
  **公平理论/组织公正**：个体判断结果相对于他人和他们如何被对待的有利性

- **McClelland's nAch**: High achievers internally driven; not concerned with effort-performance, performance-reward, or rewards-goal linkages
   
  **麦克利兰的成就需求**：高成就者内部驱动；不关心努力-绩效、绩效-奖励或奖励-目标联系

**Q1.** According to Maslow's hierarchy of needs, what happens when a need becomes substantially satisfied?

根据马斯洛需求层次理论，当一种需求被实质性满足时会发生什么？

A. The same need becomes stronger / 同一种需求变得更强
B. The next need becomes dominant / 下一种需求变得主导
C. Motivation stops / 动机停止
D. The person becomes dissatisfied / 个人变得不满意

<details>
<summary>✅ 答案 / Answer: B</summary>

According to Maslow, as each need becomes substantially satisfied, the next one becomes dominant. So if you want to motivate someone, you need to understand what level they are currently on.

根据马斯洛，当每种需求被实质性满足后，下一种需求变得主导。所以如果你想激励某人，你需要了解他当前的层次。
</details>

C. Company policies / 公司政策
D. Responsibility / 责任

<details>
<summary>✅ 答案 / Answer: C</summary>

保健因素包括：监督质量、薪酬、公司政策、工作条件、人际关系和工作保障。激励因素包括：晋升机会、个人成长、认可、责任和成就。

**Q3.** High achievers perform best when they perceive their probability of success as:

高成就者在他们感知成功概率为以下哪种时表现最好？

A. 0.2 (20% chance) / 0.2（20%机会）
B. 0.5 (50-50 chance) / 0.5（50-50机会）
C. 0.8 (80% chance) / 0.8（80%机会）

<details>
<summary>✅ 答案 / Answer: B</summary>

高成就者在他们感知成功概率为0.5时表现最好。他们不喜欢高赔率（运气的成功没有成就满足感）和低赔率（对技能没有挑战）。

**Q4.** According to cognitive evaluation theory, what happens when extrinsic rewards are provided for intrinsically motivating work?

A. Intrinsic motivation increases / 内在动机增加

<summary>✅ 答案 / Answer: B</summary>

Cognitive evaluation theory hypothesizes that extrinsic rewards will reduce intrinsic interest in a task. When people are paid for work, it feels less like something they want to do and more like something they have to do.

认知评价理论假设外在奖励会减少对任务的内在兴趣。当人们因工作获得报酬时，感觉更不像他们想做的事情，而更像他们必须做的事情。
</details>

提高自我效能最重要的来源是实践掌握——获得与任务或工作相关的经验。替代建模是第二，言语说服第三，唤醒第四。

**Q6.** An employee who perceives inequity due to being underrewarded might NOT:

感知到因奖励不足而不公平的员工可能不会：

A. Change inputs (exert less effort) / 改变投入（减少努力）
B. Distort perceptions of others / 扭曲他人知觉
C. Leave the field (quit) / 离开领域（辞职）
D. Increase their performance / 提高他们的表现

<details>
<summary>✅ 答案 / Answer: D</summary>

According to equity theory, employees who perceive inequity may: (1) change inputs (less effort if underpaid), (2) change outcomes, (3) distort self-perceptions, (4) distort perceptions of others, (5) choose different referent, or (6) leave the field. Increasing performance is NOT one of the choices.

根据公平理论，感知不公平的员工可能：（1）改变投入（薪酬不足时减少努力），（2）改变结果，（3）扭曲自我知觉，（4）扭曲他人知觉，（5）选择不同参照，或（6）离开领域。提高表现不是选项之一。
</details>

信息公正和人际公正与公民行为更强烈相关。分配公正和程序公正与任务表现更强烈相关。

**Q8.** In expectancy theory, the belief that "if I try hard, I will perform well" represents which relationship?

在期望理论中，"如果我努力，我会表现好"的信念代表哪种关系？

A. Performance-reward / 绩效-奖励

The effort-performance relationship is the probability perceived by the individual that exerting a given amount of effort will lead to performance. "If I try hard, will I perform well?"

努力-绩效关系是个体感知付出一定努力会导致绩效的概率。"如果我努力，我会表现好吗？"

### 判断题 / True or False

**Q9.** High need for achievement (nAch) necessarily makes someone a good manager.

高成就需求（nAch）必然使人成为好管理者。

<details>
<summary>✅ 答案 / Answer: False</summary>

High nAch does not necessarily make someone a good manager, especially in large organizations. People with high nAch are interested in how well they do personally, not in influencing others to do well. The most effective leaders are high in nPow and nAff.

高成就需求不一定使人成为好管理者，特别是在大组织中。高成就需求者关心个人表现，不关心影响他人表现。最有效的领导者具有高权力需求和高亲和需求。
</details>

自我生成反馈比外部生成反馈更强大。

Self-generated feedback—with which employees are able to monitor their own progress or receive feedback from the task process itself—is more powerful than externally generated feedback.

自我生成反馈——员工能够监控自己进度或从任务过程本身获得反馈——比外部生成的反馈更强大。

### 论述题模板 / Essay Question Template

**Q13.** Compare and contrast Herzberg's Two-Factor Theory with Maslow's Hierarchy of Needs. How are they similar and how are they different? Which is more useful for managers?

比较和对比赫茨伯格的双因素理论与马斯洛的需求层次理论。它们有什么相似之处和不同之处？哪个对管理者更有用？

<details>
<summary>📝 答题框架 / Answer Framework</summary>

**Part 1: Describe Each Theory / 描述每种理论**
- Maslow: Five needs hierarchy (physiological → safety → social → esteem → self-actualization); as one satisfied, next becomes dominant
- 马斯洛：五种需求层次（生理→安全→社交→尊重→自我实现）；一种满足后下一种变得主导
- Herzberg: Two factors—hygiene (prevent dissatisfaction) and motivators (create satisfaction); dual continuum
- 赫茨伯格：两种因素——保健（防止不满意）和激励（创造满意）；双重连续体

**Part 2: Similarities / 相似之处**
- Both focus on needs / 都关注需求
- Both are early theories (1950s) / 都是早期理论（20世纪50年代）
- Both are intuitively logical but research support is questionable / 都直观逻辑但研究支持可疑
- Both still used by practicing managers / 都被实践管理者使用

**Part 3: Differences / 不同之处**
- Maslow has hierarchy; Herzberg has dual continuum
- 马斯洛有层次；赫茨伯格有双重连续体
- Maslow focuses on needs; Herzberg distinguishes between factors that prevent dissatisfaction vs. create satisfaction
- 马斯洛关注需求；赫茨伯格区分防止不满意与创造满意的因素
- Hygiene factors roughly correspond to Maslow's lower-order needs; motivators to higher-order needs
- 保健因素大致对应马斯洛的低级需求；激励因素对应高级需求

**Part 4: Managerial Usefulness / 管理有用性**
- Herzberg more specific about what managers can do (emphasize motivators, not just hygiene)
- 赫茨伯格更具体说明管理者可以做什么（强调激励因素，而不仅是保健因素）
- Both have limited research support, but Herzberg's dual continuum provides clearer guidance
- 两者都有有限研究支持，但赫茨伯格的双重连续体提供更清晰指导
</details>

### 论述题准备 / Essay Preparation

#### 主题1：如何运用期望理论解释员工动机？ / Topic 1: How to Use Expectancy Theory to Explain Employee Motivation?

**Three-Step Framework / 三步框架**:

1. **Effort-Performance**: Does employee believe effort leads to performance? If not → ability issues, role ambiguity, or lack of resources
   
   **努力-绩效**：员工是否相信努力导致绩效？如果不→能力问题、角色模糊或缺乏资源

2. **Performance-Reward**: Does employee believe performance leads to rewards? If not → unfair appraisal system or rewards based on seniority/favorites
   
   **绩效-奖励**：员工是否相信绩效导致奖励？如果不→不公平的评估系统或基于资历/偏好的奖励

3. **Rewards-Personal Goals**: Do rewards satisfy employee's personal goals? If not → rewards don't match individual needs (using equity theory to understand)
   
   **奖励-个人目标**：奖励是否满足员工的个人目标？如果不→奖励不匹配个人需求（使用公平理论理解）

---

#### 主题2：比较早期理论与当代理论的优缺点 / Topic 2: Compare Strengths and Weaknesses of Early vs. Contemporary Theories

**Early Theories / 早期理论**:
- **Strengths**: Intuitively logical, easy to understand, foundation for contemporary theories, still used by managers
- **优点**：直观逻辑、易于理解、当代理论基础、管理者仍使用
- **Weaknesses**: Questionable research validity, oversimplified, don't apply well across cultures
- **缺点**：研究有效性可疑、过度简化、跨文化应用不佳

**Contemporary Theories / 当代理论**:
- **Strengths**: Valid supporting documentation, complementary to each other, address different aspects of motivation, research-backed
- **优点**：有效支持文件、彼此互补、解决动机不同方面、研究支持
- **Weaknesses**: Complex, idealistic assumptions, may not apply universally
- **缺点**：复杂、理想化假设、可能不普遍适用

## 第六部分：中英文对照总结 / Part 6: Bilingual Summary

### 核心要点总结 / Key Points Summary

**1. 动机三要素 / Three Elements of Motivation**

Intensity (how hard), Direction (toward organizational goals), Persistence (how long)

强度（多努力）、方向（朝向组织目标）、持久性（多久）

---

**2. 马斯洛需求层次 / Maslow's Hierarchy**

Five needs: Physiological → Safety → Social → Esteem → Self-actualization. As one satisfied, next becomes dominant.

五种需求：生理→安全→社交→尊重→自我实现。一种满足后下一种变得主导。

> Research doesn't validate, but still widely used by managers.
> 研究未验证，但管理者仍广泛使用。

---

**3. 赫茨伯格双因素 / Herzberg's Two Factors**

Hygiene factors prevent dissatisfaction; motivators create satisfaction. Dual continuum: satisfaction ↔ no satisfaction; dissatisfaction ↔ no dissatisfaction.

保健因素防止不满意；激励因素创造满意。双重连续体：满意↔没有满意；不满意↔没有不满意。

---

**4. 麦克利兰三需求 / McClelland's Three Needs**

nAch: best at 50-50 chance; doesn't make good manager. nPow + nAff: most effective leaders.

成就需求：50-50机会最佳；不做好管理者。权力需求+亲和需求：最有效领导者。

---

**5. 自我决定理论削弱内在动机 / Self-Determination Theory Undermines Intrinsic**

Extrinsic rewards reduce intrinsic interest. Intrinsic motivation → quality of work. Incentives → quantity of work.

外在奖励减少内在兴趣。内在动机→工作质量。激励→工作数量。

---

**6. 目标设置理论 / Goal-Setting Theory**

Specific + Difficult goals + Feedback → Higher performance. Self-generated feedback most powerful.

具体+困难目标+反馈→更高表现。自我生成反馈最强大。

---

**7. 自我效能四种方法 / Four Ways to Increase Self-Efficacy**

1. Enactive mastery (most important) / 实践掌握（最重要）
2. Vicarious modeling / 替代建模
3. Verbal persuasion / 言语说服
4. Arousal / 唤醒

> Pygmalion effect: expectations become self-fulfilling prophecy.
> 皮格马利翁效应：期望成为自我实现预言。

---

**8. 强化理论 / Reinforcement Theory**

Behavior is function of consequences. Not strictly motivation theory. Behaviorism rejects internal states.

行为是结果的函数。严格来说不是动机理论。行为主义拒绝内在状态。

---

**9. 公平理论六选择 / Equity Theory Six Choices**

When perceiving inequity: (1) change inputs, (2) change outcomes, (3) distort self, (4) distort others, (5) different referent, (6) leave.

感知不公平时：（1）改变投入，（2）改变结果，（3）扭曲自我，（4）扭曲他人，（5）不同参照，（6）离开。

---

**10. 组织公正四种类型 / Four Types of Organizational Justice**

Distributive (what), Procedural (how), Informational (explanations), Interpersonal (respect).

分配（什么）、程序（如何）、信息（解释）、人际（尊重）。

> Distributive/Procedural → Task performance. Informational/Interpersonal → Citizenship behavior.
> 分配/程序→任务表现。信息/人际→公民行为。

---

**11. 期望理论三关系 / Expectancy Theory Three Relationships**

Effort-Performance ("Can I do it?"), Performance-Reward ("Will I be rewarded?"), Rewards-Personal Goals ("Do I want the reward?").

努力-绩效（"我能做到吗？"）、绩效-奖励（"我会得到奖励吗？"）、奖励-个人目标（"我想要奖励吗？"）。

---

**12. 工作投入 / Job Engagement**

Physical, cognitive, emotional investment. Increases productivity, reduces turnover, improves safety.

身体、认知、情感投入。增加生产力、减少离职、改善安全。
