# 第八章：动机应用 / Chapter 8: Applied Motivation

## 第一部分：理论基础 / Part 1: Theoretical Foundations

### 一、工作特征模型 / Job Characteristics Model (JCM)

The way work is structured has a bigger impact on an individual's motivation than how it might first appear. Job design suggests that the way elements in a job are organized can influence employee effort.

工作的结构化方式对个体动机的影响比表面看起来更大。工作设计表明工作中元素的组织方式可以影响员工的努力。

---

#### 1. 五种核心工作维度 / Five Core Job Dimensions

| Dimension / 维度 | Definition / 定义 | High Score Example / 高分示例 | Low Score Example / 低分示例 |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| **Skill Variety / 技能多样性** | Degree to which job requires different activities using specialized skills and talents | Garage owner doing repairs, engine rebuilds, bodywork, customer interaction | Body shop worker spraying paint 8 hours/day |
| **Task Identity / 任务同一性** | Degree to which job requires completion of a whole and identifiable piece of work | Cabinetmaker who designs, selects wood, builds, and finishes furniture | Operating lathe solely to make table legs |
| **Task Significance / 任务重要性** | Degree to which job affects lives or work of other people | Nurse helping patients in ICU | Sweeping floors in hospital |
| **Autonomy / 自主性** | Degree to which job provides freedom, independence, and discretion in scheduling work and determining procedures | Sales manager who schedules own work and tailors approach without supervision | Account rep required to follow standardized sales script |
| **Feedback / 反馈** | Degree to which carrying out work activities generates direct and clear information about own performance | Testing and inspecting iPads | Installing iPad components on assembly line |

---

#### 2. JCM的元素 / Elements of the JCM

The JCM proposes that individuals obtain internal rewards when they learn (knowledge of results) that they personally have performed well (experienced responsibility) on a task they care about (experienced meaningfulness).

JCM提出当个体得知（结果知识）他们在关心的任务上（体验到的意义）个人表现良好（体验到的责任）时，他们获得内在奖励。

**Three Critical Psychological States / 三种关键心理状态**:

| State / 状态 | Created By / 由...创造 |
|:---|:---|
| **Experienced Meaningfulness / 体验到的意义** | Skill variety, task identity, task significance |
| **Experienced Responsibility / 体验到的责任** | Autonomy |
| **Knowledge of Results / 结果知识** | Feedback |

> **Key Prediction**: The more these three psychological states are present, the greater will be employees' motivation, performance, and satisfaction, and the lower their absenteeism and likelihood of leaving.
> **关键预测**：这三种心理状态越存在，员工的动机、表现和满意度就越高，缺勤和离职可能性就越低。

---

#### 3. 激励潜力分数 / Motivating Potential Score (MPS)

We can combine the core dimensions of the JCM into a single predictive index, called the motivating potential score (MPS).

我们可以将JCM的核心维度组合成一个单一预测指数，称为激励潜力分数（MPS）。

> **Formula Note**: To be high on motivating potential, jobs must be high in at least one of the three factors that lead to experienced meaningfulness AND high in both autonomy and feedback.
> **公式注意**：要在激励潜力上得分高，工作必须在导致体验意义的三个因素中至少一个上得分高，并且在自主性和反馈上都得分高。

**Simplified Approach / 简化方法**:

We can better calculate motivating potential by simply adding characteristics rather than using the formula.

我们可以通过简单地相加特征而不是使用公式来更好地计算激励潜力。

---

#### 4. JCM的文化通用性 / Cultural Generalizability of the JCM

The fact that the model is relatively individualistic (it considers the relationship between the employee and his or her work) suggests job enrichment strategies may not have the same effects in collectivistic cultures as in individualistic cultures.

该模型相对个人主义（考虑员工与其工作之间的关系）的事实表明工作丰富化策略在集体主义文化中可能不如在个人主义文化中有效。

> **Mixed Findings / 混合发现**:
> - Study in Nigeria (highly collectivistic): MPS was influenced by job dimensions, but correlations were different than individualistic countries
> - 尼日利亚研究（高度集体主义）：MPS受工作维度影响，但相关性与个人主义国家不同
> - Study in U.S., Japan, Hungary: Intrinsic job motivators predicted job satisfaction equally well
> - 美国、日本、匈牙利研究：内在工作的激励因素同样好地预测工作满意度

---

### 二、工作再设计 / Using Job Redesign to Motivate Employees

#### 1. 工作轮换 / Job Rotation

Job rotation (also called cross-training) is the periodic shifting of an employee from one task to another with similar skill requirements at the same organizational level.

工作轮换（也称为交叉培训）是在同一组织层级上定期将员工从一项任务转移到另一项具有相似技能要求的任务。

**Benefits / 好处**:

- Increases job satisfaction and organizational commitment
  
  增加工作满意度和组织承诺

- Reduces boredom, increases motivation
  
  减少无聊，增加动机

- Helps employees understand how their work contributes to the organization
  
  帮助员工理解他们的工作如何为组织做贡献

- May increase safety and reduce repetitive-based work injuries (mixed findings)
  
  可能增加安全并减少重复性工作伤害（混合发现）

**Drawbacks / 缺点**:

1. Work done repeatedly may become habitual and less thoughtfully considered
   
   重复完成的工作可能变得习惯化且更少深思熟虑

2. Training costs increase when each rotation requires learning new skills
   
   当每次轮换需要学习新技能时培训成本增加

3. Moving worker into new position reduces overall productivity for that role
   
   将员工转移到新职位降低该角色的整体生产力

4. Creates disruptions when work group members adjust to new employees
   
   当工作群体成员适应新员工时产生干扰

5. Supervisors spend more time answering questions and monitoring work
   
   主管花更多时间回答问题并监控工作

---

#### 2. 关系工作设计 / Relational Job Design

Relational job design shifts the spotlight from the employee to those whose lives are affected by the job that employee performs. It focuses on prosocial motivation—motivating employees to promote the well-being of the organization's beneficiaries (customers, clients, patients, employees).

关系工作设计将焦点从员工转移到受该员工工作影响的那些人身上。它关注亲社会动机——激励员工促进组织受益者（客户、顾客、患者、员工）的福祉。

**How to Make Jobs More Prosocially Motivating / 如何使工作更具亲社会激励性**:

1. **Connect employees with beneficiaries** — Relate stories from customers who found products/services helpful
   
   **连接员工与受益者**——讲述发现产品/服务有帮助的客户故事

2. **Meeting beneficiaries firsthand** (or seeing pictures) allows employees to see actions affect real people
   
   **亲自会见受益者**（或看照片）让员工看到行动影响真实的人

3. **Connections allow employees to understand perspective of beneficiaries** → higher commitment
   
   **连接让员工理解受益者的视角**→更高的承诺

> **CSR Connection**: Relational job design with focus on prosocial motivation is salient for organizations with corporate social responsibility (CSR) initiatives.
> **企业社会责任联系**：关注亲社会动机的关系工作设计对有企业社会责任（CSR）倡议的组织很突出。

---

### 三、替代性工作安排 / Alternative Work Arrangements

#### 1. 弹性工作制 / Flextime

Flextime (flexible work time) means employees must work a specific number of hours per week but may vary their hours within limits. Each day consists of a common core (usually six hours) with a flexibility band surrounding it.

弹性工作制（弹性工作时间）意味着员工每周必须工作特定小时数，但可以在限制内变化他们的工作时间。每天由一个共同核心（通常六小时）和围绕它的弹性带组成。

**Benefits / 好处**:

- Increases profitability (when promoted as work-life balance strategy, not organizational gain)
  
  增加盈利能力（当作为工作生活平衡策略推广，而非组织收益时）

- Reduces absenteeism
  
  减少缺勤

- Helps employees balance work and family lives
  
  帮助员工平衡工作和家庭生活

- Popular criterion for judging "family friendly" workplace
  
  评判"家庭友好"工作场所的流行标准

**Limitations / 限制**:

- Not applicable to every job (e.g., service jobs requiring workstation presence at certain hours)
  
  不适用于每个工作（例如，需要在特定时间在工作岗位的服务工作）

- People who want to separate work and family lives less apt to want flextime
  
  想要分离工作和家庭生活的人不太想要弹性工作制

- UK research: employees with flextime do not realize reduction in stress levels
  
  英国研究：有弹性工作制的员工没有实现压力水平降低

---

#### 2. 工作分享 / Job Sharing

Job sharing allows two or more individuals to split a traditional full-time job.

工作分享允许两个或更多个体分割传统的全职工作。

**Benefits / 好处**:

- Allows organization to draw on talents of more than one individual for a given job
  
  允许组织为特定工作利用不止一个人的才能

- Opens opportunity to acquire skilled workers (parents with young children, retirees)
  
  开放获得熟练工人的机会（有小孩的父母、退休人员）

- Increases motivation and satisfaction for employees
  
  增加员工的动机和满意度

**Challenges / 挑战**:

- Difficulty finding compatible partners
  
  找到兼容伙伴的困难

- Negative perceptions as not completely committed
  
  被视为不完全承诺的负面看法

- Training, coordination, and administrative costs can be high
  
  培训、协调和管理成本可能很高

---

#### 3. 远程办公 / Telecommuting

Telecommuting refers to working at home (or anywhere outside the workplace) at least two days a week on a computer linked to the employer's office.

远程办公指每周至少两天在家中（或工作场所以外任何地方）通过连接到雇主办公室的电脑工作。

**Disadvantages for Employees / 对员工的缺点**:

1. Can increase feelings of isolation and reduce job satisfaction
   
   可能增加孤立感并降低工作满意度

2. Does not reduce work-family conflicts (often increases work hours beyond contracted)
   
   不减少工作-家庭冲突（通常增加超过合同的工作时间）

3. "Out of sight, out of mind" effect: disadvantage for raises and promotions due to not putting in "face time"
   
   "眼不见心不烦"效应：由于不出面在晋升和加薪上处于劣势

4. CSR benefit questioned: telecommuters drive over 45 miles more per day due to increased personal trips
   
   企业社会效益受质疑：远程办公者因个人出行增加每天多开45英里

---

### 四、员工参与和参与 / Employee Involvement and Participation (EIP)

Employee involvement and participation (EIP) is a participative process that uses the input of employees to increase employee commitment to organizational success.

员工参与和参与（EIP）是一个参与过程，使用员工的投入来增加员工对组织成功的承诺。

> **Key Benefit**: If workers are engaged in decisions that increase their autonomy and control over their work lives, they will become more motivated, committed, productive, and satisfied.
> **关键好处**：如果员工参与增加他们自主性和对工作生活控制的决策，他们将变得更有动力、更承诺、更高效和更满意。

---

#### 1. 参与式管理 / Participative Management

Common to all participative management programs is joint decision making, in which subordinates share a significant degree of decision-making power with their immediate superiors.

所有参与式管理项目的共同点是联合决策，下属与直接上级共享重大程度的决策权。

> **Requirements for Effectiveness / 有效性的要求**:
> - Followers must have trust and confidence in their leaders
> - 追随者必须对领导者有信任和信心
> - Leaders should avoid coercive techniques
> - 领导者应避免强制技术
> - Stress organizational consequences of decision making
> - 强调决策的组织后果

**Research Findings / 研究发现**:

Studies have yielded mixed findings. Organizations may realize higher stock returns, lower turnover rates, and higher labor productivity, but effects typically not large. At individual level, participation has only modest influence on productivity, motivation, and satisfaction.

研究产生混合发现。组织可能实现更高股票回报、更低离职率和更高劳动生产力，但影响通常不大。在个体层面，参与对生产力、动机和满意度的影响适中。

---

#### 2. 代表参与 / Representative Participation

Representative participation redistributes power within an organization, putting labor's interests on more equal footing with management and stockholders by including a small group of employees as participants in decision making.

代表参与在组织内重新分配权力，通过包括一小部分员工作为决策参与者，将劳工利益与管理层和股东放在更平等的位置。

**Two Common Forms / 两种常见形式**:

| Form / 形式 | Description / 描述 |
|:---|:---|
| **Works Councils / 工作委员会** | Groups of nominated or elected employees who must be consulted when management makes decisions about employees |
| **Board Representatives / 董事会代表** | Employees who sit on company's board of directors and represent employees' interests |

> **Motivational Limitation**: An employee would need to feel interests were well represented and make a difference to the organization for motivation to increase. Thus, representative participation is surpassed by more direct participation methods.
> **动机限制**：员工需要感觉利益被充分代表并对组织产生影响才能增加动机。因此，代表参与被更直接的参与方法超越。

---

### 五、外在奖励激励 / Using Extrinsic Rewards to Motivate Employees

#### 1. 建立薪酬结构 / Establishing a Pay Structure

The process of initially setting pay levels entails balancing:

最初设定薪酬水平的过程需要平衡：

- **Internal Equity / 内部公平**: The worth of the job to the organization (established through job evaluation)
  
  **内部公平**：工作对组织的价值（通过工作评估建立）

- **External Equity / 外部公平**: Competitiveness of organization's pay relative to industry (established through pay surveys)
  
  **外部公平**：组织薪酬相对于行业的竞争力（通过薪酬调查建立）

> **Walmart vs. Costco Example**: Costco paid ~$45,000 vs. Walmart's Sam's Club ~$17,500. Costco's higher wages resulted in increased productivity and reduced turnover. Walmart later increased worker wages.
> **沃尔玛vs好市多示例**：好市多支付约45,000美元vs沃尔玛山姆会员店约17,500美元。好市多的更高工资导致生产力增加和离职率降低。沃尔玛后来提高了工人工资。

---

#### 2. 可变薪酬计划 / Variable-Pay Programs

A variable-pay program (pay-for-performance) bases a portion of an employee's pay on some individual and/or organizational measure of performance.

可变薪酬计划（按绩效付酬）将员工薪酬的一部分基于某些个体和/或组织绩效衡量。

**Global Statistics / 全球统计**:

- 80% of companies globally offer some form of variable-pay plan
  
  全球80%的公司提供某种形式的可变薪酬计划

- 91% in U.S., >90% in Latin America
  
  美国91%，拉丁美洲>90%

- Latin America: highest percentage of payroll allocated to variable pay (~18%)
  
  拉丁美洲：分配给可变薪酬的工资比例最高（~18%）

---

**Types of Variable-Pay Programs / 可变薪酬计划类型**:

**Piece-Rate Pay / 计件工资**:

Workers are paid a fixed sum for each unit of production completed. A pure piece-rate plan provides no base salary.

工人每完成一个生产单位获得固定金额。纯计件工资计划不提供基本工资。

> **Key Findings / 关键发现**:
> - Produce higher productivity and wages
> - 产生更高生产力和工资
> - 68% of risk-averse individuals prefer individual piece-rate; lower performers prefer team piece-rate
> - 68%的风险规避个体偏好个人计件；低表现者偏好团队计件
> - Concerns: workers decrease quality to increase speed; increase probability of workplace injuries
> - 担忧：工人为增加速度降低质量；增加工作场所伤害概率

**Merit-Based Pay / 绩效工资**:

Pays for individual performance based on performance appraisal ratings. High performers can get bigger raises.

基于绩效评估比率为个体绩效付酬。高表现者可以获得更大加薪。

> **Limitations / 限制**:
> 1. Based on annual performance appraisal, which is often subjective
>    
>    基于通常主观的年度绩效评估
> 2. Pay-raise pool fluctuates on economic conditions unrelated to individual performance
>    
>    加薪资金池波动基于与个体绩效无关的经济状况
> 3. Unions typically resist merit-based pay
>    
>    工会通常抵制绩效工资
> 4. Demographic differences in ratings (African Americans receive lower ratings, women's ratings higher than men's)
>    
>    评级中的人口差异（非裔美国人收到更低评级，女性评级高于男性）

**Bonus / 奖金**:

Rewards employees for recent performance rather than historical performance (merit pay is cumulative). When times are bad, firms can cut bonuses.

奖励员工近期绩效而非历史绩效（绩效工资是累积的）。当形势不好时，公司可以削减奖金。

> **Upside / 优点**: More motivating than merit pay; higher percentage of pay for bonuses increases productivity
> 
> **Downside / 缺点**: Leaves employees' pay vulnerable to cuts; employees may take bonuses for granted as expected income
> 
> 使员工薪酬容易受到削减；员工可能将奖金视为预期收入而理所当然

**Profit-Sharing Plan / 利润分享计划**:

Distributes compensation based on established formula designed around company's profitability. Can be direct cash or stock options.

基于围绕公司盈利能力建立的公式分配薪酬。可以是直接现金或股票期权。

> **Findings / 发现**:
> - Organizations with profit-sharing have higher profitability
> - 有利润分享的组织有更高盈利能力
> - Linked to higher employee commitment, especially in small organizations
> - 与更高员工承诺相关，特别是在小型组织中
> - Motivates when used with other pay-for-performance plans
> - 与其他按绩效付酬计划一起使用时激励
> - Does not work in nonprofit organizations or public sector (no reported profit)
> - 在非营利组织或公共部门无效（无报告利润）

**Employee Stock Ownership Plan (ESOP) / 员工持股计划**:

Company-established benefit plan in which employees acquire stock, often at below-market prices, as part of benefits.

公司建立的福利计划，员工以低于市场价格获得股票作为福利的一部分。

> **Findings / 发现**:
> - Increase employee satisfaction and innovation
> - 增加员工满意度和创新
> - Increase satisfaction only when employees psychologically experience ownership
> - 仅当员工心理上体验所有权时增加满意度
> - May not inspire lower absenteeism or greater motivation (benefit comes later when cashing in stock)
> - 可能不激发更低缺勤或更大动机（福利在后来兑现股票时才来）
> - For top management: can reduce unethical behavior (CEOs less likely to manipulate earnings reports)
> - 对高层管理：可以减少不道德行为（CEO不太可能操纵收益报告）

---

#### 3. 薪酬保密的影响 / Impact of Pay Secrecy

Pay secrecy has a detrimental effect on job performance. It adversely affects high performers more than other employees. It increases employees' perception that pay is subjective, which can be demotivating.

薪酬保密对工作绩效有有害影响。它对高表现者的不利影响大于其他员工。它增加员工认为薪酬是主观的感知，这可能会降低动机。

> **Recommendation / 建议**: If general pay categories are made public and employees feel variable pay is linked objectively to their performance, the motivational effects can be retained.
> **建议**：如果公开一般薪酬类别且员工感觉可变薪酬与他们的绩效客观联系，可以保留激励效果。

---

### 六、福利激励 / Using Benefits to Motivate Employees

#### 1. 弹性福利 / Flexible Benefits

Flexible benefits allow each employee to put together a benefits package individually tailored to his or her own needs and situation.

弹性福利允许每个员工组装 individually 定制的福利包以满足他或她自己的需求和情况。

> **Expectancy Theory Connection**: Consistent with expectancy theory's thesis that organizational rewards should be linked to each employee's goals, flexible benefits individualize rewards.
> **期望理论联系**：与期望理论的论点一致，即组织奖励应与每个员工的目标联系，弹性福利个性化奖励。

**Benefits / 好处**:

- Accommodate differences in employee needs based on age, marital status, partner's benefit status, dependents
  
  适应基于年龄、婚姻状况、伴侣福利状况、抚养人的员工需求差异

- Some organizations report increased retention, satisfaction, and productivity
  
  一些组织报告增加保留、满意度和生产力

**Downsides / 缺点**:

- More costly to manage
  
  管理成本更高

- Identifying motivational impact of different provisions is challenging
  
  识别不同条款的动机影响具有挑战性

- May not substitute for higher salaries
  
  可能不替代更高薪水

- Not yet global (limited usage in China and many Asian countries)
  
  尚未全球化（在中国和许多亚洲国家使用有限）

---

### 七、内在奖励激励 / Using Intrinsic Rewards to Motivate Employees

#### 1. 员工认可计划 / Employee Recognition Program

An employee recognition program is a plan to encourage specific behaviors by formally appreciating specific employee contributions.

员工认可计划是通过正式赞赏特定员工贡献来鼓励特定行为的计划。

**Research Findings / 研究发现**:

- 1,500 employees surveyed: most powerful workplace motivator = recognition, recognition, and more recognition
  
  调查1,500名员工：最强大的工作场所激励者=认可、认可和更多认可

- Financial incentives may be more motivating in short term, but nonfinancial incentives work best in long run
  
  财务激励在短期可能更有激励性，但非财务激励在长期效果最好

- Associated with self-esteem, self-efficacy, and job satisfaction
  
  与自尊、自我效能和工作满意度相关

**Advantages / 优点**:

- Inexpensive: praise is free!
  
  便宜：赞美是免费的！

- Can be highly motivating with or without financial rewards
  
  无论有无财务奖励都可以高度激励

**Criticisms / 批评**:

- Highly susceptible to political manipulation by management
  
  极易受管理层政治操纵

- When performance criteria aren't self-evident, managers can manipulate system and recognize favorites
  
  当绩效标准不明显时，管理层可以操纵系统并认可 favorites

> **Recommendation / 建议**: Where formal recognition programs are used, care must be taken to ensure fairness. Where they are not, it is important to consistently recognize performance efforts.
> **建议**：使用正式认可计划时，必须注意确保公平。不使用时，持续认可绩效努力很重要。

| **Task Identity / 任务同一性** | Degree to which job requires completion of whole, identifiable piece of work |
| **Task Significance / 任务重要性** | Degree to which job affects lives or work of other people |
| **Autonomy / 自主性** | Degree to which job provides freedom and discretion |
| **Feedback / 反馈** | Degree to which work activities generate information about performance |
| **Motivating Potential Score (MPS) / 激励潜力分数** | Predictive index of motivating potential in a job |
| **Job Rotation / 工作轮换** | Periodic shifting of employee from one task to another (cross-training) |
| **Relational Job Design / 关系工作设计** | Focuses on prosocial motivation to promote beneficiary well-being |
| **Flextime / 弹性工作制** | Flexible work hours within limits around common core |
| **Job Sharing / 工作分享** | Two or more individuals split traditional full-time job |
| **Telecommuting / 远程办公** | Working from home at least 2 days/week on linked computer |
| **Employee Involvement (EIP) / 员工参与** | Participative process using employee input to increase commitment |
| **Participative Management / 参与式管理** | Subordinates share significant decision-making power with superiors |
| **Representative Participation / 代表参与** | Workers participate through small group of representative employees |
| **Variable-Pay Program / 可变薪酬计划** | Pay plan basing portion of pay on individual/organizational performance |
| **Piece-Rate Pay / 计件工资** | Fixed sum for each unit of production completed |
| **Merit-Based Pay / 绩效工资** | Pay plan based on performance appraisal ratings |
| **Bonus / 奖金** | Rewards recent performance rather than historical |
| **Profit-Sharing / 利润分享** | Compensation based on company's profitability |
| **ESOP / 员工持股计划** | Employees acquire stock as part of benefits |
| **Flexible Benefits / 弹性福利** | Benefits plan tailored to individual needs and situation |
| **Employee Recognition Program / 员工认可计划** | Plan encouraging behaviors by formally appreciating contributions |

## 第三部分：考点总结 / Part 3: Exam Points Summary

### 高频考点 / High-Frequency Exam Points

1. **JCM五种核心维度** / **Five Core Job Dimensions of JCM**
   - Skill variety, task identity, task significance, autonomy, feedback
   - 技能多样性、任务同一性、任务重要性、自主性、反馈
   - 题型：单选、配对、多选

2. **JCM三种关键心理状态** / **Three Critical Psychological States of JCM**
   - Experienced meaningfulness (skill variety + task identity + task significance)
   - 体验到的意义（技能多样性+任务同一性+任务重要性）
   - Experienced responsibility (autonomy)
   - 体验到的责任（自主性）
   - Knowledge of results (feedback)
   - 结果知识（反馈）

3. **可变薪酬计划类型** / **Types of Variable-Pay Programs**
   - Piece-rate, merit-based, bonus, profit-sharing, ESOP
   - 计件、绩效、奖金、利润分享、ESOP
   - 题型：单选、配对、判断

4. **工作轮换优缺点** / **Pros and Cons of Job Rotation**
   - Benefits: reduces boredom, increases motivation
   - 好处：减少无聊，增加动机
   - Drawbacks: training costs, disruptions, reduced productivity
   - 缺点：培训成本、干扰、生产力降低

5. **弹性福利与期望理论的联系** / **Connection Between Flexible Benefits and Expectancy Theory**
   - Flexible benefits individualize rewards to match employee goals
   - 弹性福利个性化奖励以匹配员工目标

---

### 中频考点 / Medium-Frequency Exam Points

6. **替代性工作安排** / **Alternative Work Arrangements**
   - Flextime, job sharing, telecommuting
   - 弹性工作制、工作分享、远程办公
   - Benefits and limitations of each
   - 每种的好处和限制

7. **员工参与两种形式** / **Two Forms of Employee Involvement**
   - Participative management vs. representative participation
   - 参与式管理vs代表参与
   - Representative participation surpassed by direct methods
   - 代表参与被直接方法超越

8. **薪酬保密的负面影响** / **Negative Effects of Pay Secrecy**
   - Detrimental to job performance, especially high performers
   - 对工作绩效有害，特别是高表现者
   - Increases perception that pay is subjective
   - 增加认为薪酬是主观的感知

9. **员工认可计划** / **Employee Recognition Programs**
   - Most powerful motivator according to survey
   - 调查显示最强大的激励者
   - Inexpensive (praise is free)
   - 便宜（赞美免费）
   - Susceptible to political manipulation
   - 易受政治操纵

10. **关系工作设计** / **Relational Job Design**
    - Focus on prosocial motivation
    - 关注亲社会动机
    - Connect employees with beneficiaries
    - 连接员工与受益者

    - 必须在至少一个意义因素上高且在自主性和反馈上高

    - May not have same effects in collectivistic cultures
    - 在集体主义文化中可能没有相同效果

    - 低表现者偏好团队计件

**Q1.** According to the Job Characteristics Model, which dimension refers to the degree to which a job affects the lives or work of other people?

根据工作特征模型，哪个维度指工作影响他人生活或工作的程度？

A. Skill variety / 技能多样性
B. Task identity / 任务同一性
C. Task significance / 任务重要性
D. Autonomy / 自主性

<details>
<summary>✅ 答案 / Answer: C</summary>

Task significance is the degree to which a job affects the lives or work of other people. Example: nurse helping patients in ICU scores high; sweeping floors scores low.

任务重要性是工作影响他人生活或工作的程度。示例：ICU帮助患者的护士得分高；扫地得分低。

哪种可变薪酬计划奖励近期绩效而非纳入基本工资的历史绩效？

A. Merit-based pay / 绩效工资

C. Piece-rate pay / 计件工资

奖金奖励近期绩效，而绩效工资是累积的并纳入基本工资。形势不好时，公司可以削减奖金。

- All tie pay to performance in different ways
- 所有以不同方式将薪酬与绩效联系

- 计件和绩效是个体；利润分享是组织

- Bonus: when flexibility is needed (firms can cut in bad times)

- Profit-sharing: when company is profitable and wants to increase commitment
- 利润分享：当公司盈利且想增加承诺时

- ESOP：当公司想减少不道德行为并增加所有权感觉时
