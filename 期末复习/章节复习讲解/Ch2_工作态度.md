## 第一部分：理论基础 / Theory Foundations

### 一、态度的定义与三成分 / Definition and Three Components of Attitudes

Attitudes are evaluative statements—either favorable or unfavorable—about objects, people, or events. They reflect how we feel about something.

态度是关于对象、人或事件的评价性陈述——无论是正面还是负面的。它们反映了我们对某事的感觉。

---

#### 态度的三成分 / Three Components (ABC Model)

Attitudes have three components: cognitive, affective, and behavioral. These components are closely related and difficult to separate.

态度有三个成分：认知、情感和行为。这三个成分密切相关，难以分离。

| 成分 / Component | 英文术语 | 描述 / Description | 示例 / Example |
|-----------------|---------|-------------------|----------------|
| **认知成分** | Cognitive Component | The opinion or belief segment of an attitude (态度的信念或观点部分) | "My pay is low" (我的薪酬很低) |
| **情感成分** | Affective Component | The emotional or feeling segment of an attitude (态度的情绪或感受部分) | "I am angry over how little I'm paid" (我对薪酬低感到愤怒) |
| **行为成分** | Behavioral Component | An intention to behave in a certain way toward someone or something (对某人或某事以特定方式行为的意向) | "I'm going to look for another job" (我打算找新工作) |

> **Memory Tip**: **ABC** = Affective (情感) + Behavioral (行为) + Cognitive (认知)  
> **记忆口诀**：ABC模型

> **Key Understanding**: Cognition and affect are often intertwined and difficult to separate. Behavior tends to follow from attitude when the attitude is strong and accessible.  
> **关键理解**：认知和情感通常是交织在一起的。当态度强烈且容易获取时，行为往往会跟随态度。

---

### 二、认知失调理论 / Cognitive Dissonance Theory

**Proposed by:** Leon Festinger (1957)

Cognitive dissonance refers to any incompatibility or inconsistency between attitudes or between behavior and attitudes. People experience discomfort when they hold contradictory attitudes or when their behavior conflicts with their attitudes.

认知失调是指态度之间或行为与态度之间的任何不一致。当人们持有矛盾的态度或行为与态度冲突时，会产生不适感。

#### Reducing Dissonance / 减少失调的方法

1. **Change the attitude**: Modify one of the conflicting attitudes
   改变态度：修改其中一个冲突的态度

2. **Change the behavior**: Alter the behavior to be consistent with the attitude
   改变行为：改变行为以与态度一致

3. **Rationalize the difference**: Find a justification for the inconsistency
   合理化差异：为不一致找到理由

#### Factors Influencing Dissonance Reduction / 影响减少失调的因素

| 因素 / Factor | 说明 / Description |
|--------------|-------------------|
| **重要性 / Importance** | The more important the elements that create dissonance, the greater the motivation to reduce it (造成失调的元素越重要，减少失调的动力越大) |
| **控制度 / Influence** | The more influence we believe we have over the situation, the more likely we are to reduce dissonance (我们认为对情境的控制度越高，越可能减少失调) |
| **奖励 / Rewards** | Higher rewards can decrease the tension created by dissonance (高奖励可以降低失调带来的紧张感) |

### 三、主要工作态度 / Major Job Attitudes

#### 1. 工作满意度 / Job Satisfaction

Job satisfaction is a positive feeling about one's job resulting from an evaluation of its characteristics.

工作满意度是对工作产生的积极感受，源于对工作特征的评估。

> **Key Point**: High job satisfaction = positive attitude toward the job; Low job satisfaction = negative attitude  
> **关键点**：高工作满意度 = 对工作的正面态度；低工作满意度 = 对工作的负面态度

---

#### 2. 工作投入度 / Job Involvement

Job involvement is the degree to which a person identifies psychologically with their job and considers their perceived performance level important to self-worth.

工作投入度是个人在心理上认同自己的工作程度，以及认为自己的工作表现对自我价值的重要程度。

**相关概念：心理授权 / Psychological Empowerment:**

Psychological empowerment is employees' belief in:
1. The degree to which they affect their work environment (对工作环境的控制度)
2. Their competence (自己的能力)
3. The meaningfulness of their job (工作的意义)
4. Their perceived autonomy (感知到的自主性)

---

#### 3. 组织承诺 / Organizational Commitment

Organizational commitment is the degree to which an employee identifies with a particular organization and its goals and wishes to maintain membership in the organization.

组织承诺是员工认同特定组织及其目标，并希望保持组织成员身份的程度。

> **Key Finding**: Highly committed employees are less likely to leave even when dissatisfied, because of their emotional attachment to the organization.  
> **关键发现**：高度承诺的员工即使不满意也不太可能离职，因为他们对组织有情感依恋。

---

#### 4. 感知组织支持 / Perceived Organizational Support (POS)

POS is the degree to which employees believe an organization values their contributions and cares about their well-being.

感知组织支持是员工相信组织重视其贡献并关心其福祉的程度。

**Factors that increase POS / 增加POS的因素:**
- Fair rewards (奖励公平)
- Voice in decisions (参与决策)
- Supportive supervisors (主管支持)

**Cultural Differences / 文化差异:**

In low power-distance countries (like the United States), POS is more important because employees expect fairness and support from the organization.

在低权力距离文化中（如美国），POS更重要，因为员工期望组织提供公平和支持。

---

#### 5. 员工敬业度 / Employee Engagement

Employee engagement is an individual's involvement with, satisfaction with, and enthusiasm for the work they do.

员工敬业度是个体对其工作的参与度、满意度和热情。

> **Key Data**: Only 17%-29% of employees are highly engaged!  
> **关键数据**：只有17%-29%的员工高度敬业！

**Two drivers of engagement / 敬业度的两大驱动因素:**
1. Having a good supervisor whom they like working for (有好主管)
2. Feeling appreciated by the supervisor (感到被主管赞赏)

---

### 四、测量工作满意度 / Measuring Job Satisfaction

| 方法 / Method | 描述 / Description | 优点 / Advantage |
|--------------|-------------------|-----------------|
| **单一整体评分** | Single Global Rating: One question like "All things considered, how satisfied are you with your job?" | Simple and fast (简单快捷) |
| **工作要素总和** | Summation of Job Facets: Evaluating multiple facets (pay, promotion, coworkers, nature of work) and adding them up | Helps identify problem areas (帮助定位问题) |

> **Important Finding**: Both methods are equally valid! The simple single global rating is not inferior to the more complex summation method.  
> **重要发现**：两种方法同样有效！简单的单一整体评分并不比复杂的工作要素总和方法差。

---

### 五、工作满意度的原因 / Causes of Job Satisfaction

#### 1. 工作条件 / Job Conditions ⭐ Most Important

Jobs that are interesting, provide training, variety, independence, and control are more satisfying. The intrinsic nature of the work itself is the strongest predictor.

有趣的工作提供培训、多样性、独立性和控制感，更能带来满意度。工作本身的内在性质是最强的预测因素。

#### 2. 人格 / Personality

People with positive Core Self-Evaluations (CSEs) are more satisfied with their jobs. CSE is the degree to which people like themselves and believe in their capabilities.

核心自我评价（CSE）高的人对工作更满意。CSE是人们喜欢自己并相信自己能力的程度。

#### 3. 薪酬 / Pay

Pay is correlated with job satisfaction, but after reaching a comfortable living standard, the relationship weakens. Money does not buy happiness indefinitely.

薪酬与工作满意度相关，但达到舒适生活水平后，关系减弱。金钱不能无限地购买幸福。

#### 4. 企业社会责任 / Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR)

Employees whose personal values align with the organization's CSR mission are more satisfied. This is especially true for Millennials.

个人价值观与组织CSR使命匹配的员工更满意。这对千禧一代尤其重要。

---

### 六、工作满意度的结果 / Outcomes of Job Satisfaction

#### 1. 工作绩效 / Job Performance

Satisfied workers are more productive workers. A review of 300 studies confirms a strong relationship between satisfaction and performance.

满意度的员工生产力更高。对300项研究的回顾确认了满意度与绩效之间的强关系。

#### 2. 组织公民行为 / OCB

Job satisfaction is moderately correlated with OCB. Satisfied employees are more likely to engage in discretionary behavior that helps the organization.

工作满意度与OCB中度相关。满意的员工更可能参与帮助组织的自愿行为。

#### 3. 客户满意度 / Customer Satisfaction

Satisfied frontline employees increase customer satisfaction and loyalty, which increases organizational performance and profitability.

满意的一线员工提高客户满意度和忠诚度，从而提高组织绩效和盈利能力。

#### 4. 缺勤 / Absenteeism

Job satisfaction is negatively correlated with absenteeism, but the relationship is not very strong because absenteeism has other causes.

工作满意度与缺勤负相关，但关系不是很强，因为缺勤还有其他原因。

#### 5. 离职 / Turnover

Job satisfaction is more strongly related to turnover than to absenteeism. The pattern of declining satisfaction is the best predictor of turnover intention.

工作满意度与离职的相关性强于与缺勤的相关性。满意度下降的模式是预测离职意愿的最佳指标。

---

### 七、EVLN模型 / Responses to Job Dissatisfaction (EVLN Model)

When employees are dissatisfied, they respond in one of four ways:

当员工不满意时，他们会以四种方式之一做出反应：

| 反应 / Response | 类型 / Type | 描述 / Description | 示例 / Example |
|----------------|------------|-------------------|----------------|
| **退出 / Exit** | Active-Destructive (主动-破坏性) | Leaving the organization (离开组织) | Looking for a new job, resigning (找新工作、辞职) |
| **建言 / Voice** | Active-Constructive (主动-建设性) | Actively trying to improve conditions (积极尝试改善条件) | Suggesting improvements, filing grievances (提建议、申诉) |
| **忠诚 / Loyalty** | Passive-Constructive (被动-建设性) | Passively waiting for conditions to improve (被动等待条件改善) | Trusting the organization to do the right thing (信任组织会做正确的事) |
| **忽视 / Neglect** | Passive-Destructive (被动-破坏性) | Allowing conditions to worsen (让条件恶化) | Chronic absenteeism, lateness, reduced effort (频繁缺勤、迟到、减少努力) |

> **Memory Tip**: **E-V-L-N** = Exit, Voice, Loyalty, Neglect  
> **记忆口诀**：E-V-L-N

> **Key Distinction**: 
> - Active = Exit, Voice
> - Passive = Loyalty, Neglect
> - Constructive = Voice, Loyalty
> - Destructive = Exit, Neglect
