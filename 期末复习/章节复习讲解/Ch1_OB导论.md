## 第一部分：理论基础 / Theory Foundations

### 一、什么是组织行为学 / What is Organizational Behavior?

Organizational Behavior (OB) is a field of study that investigates the impact that **individuals, groups, and structure** have on behavior within organizations, for the purpose of applying such knowledge toward improving an organization's effectiveness.

组织行为学（OB）是一个研究领域，研究**个体、群体和结构**对组织内部行为的影响，目的是将这些知识应用于提高组织效能。

> **Key Point**: OB = what influences behavior + purpose is to improve organizational effectiveness  
> **记忆要点**：OB = 研究什么影响行为 + 目的是提高组织效能

---

### 二、为什么OB很重要 / Why OB Matters

**1. Improve Interpersonal Skills**

Managers need people skills to attract and keep high-performing employees.

管理者需要人际技能来吸引和留住高绩效员工。

**2. Transform Workplace Performance**

Companies with good OB practices generate superior financial performance.

OB实践良好的公司产生更优越的财务表现。

**3. Improve Workplace Relationships**

The quality of employee relationships directly affects job satisfaction, stress levels, and turnover.

员工关系的质量直接影响工作满意度、压力和离职率。

**4. Foster Social Responsibility**

Understanding OB helps managers address Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) awareness.

理解OB帮助管理者应对企业社会责任意识。

---

### 三、成功管理者 vs 有效管理者 / Successful vs Effective Managers

Successful managers are measured by the speed of their promotion within the organization. They spend the most time on **networking** (socializing, politicking, and interacting with outsiders).

成功管理者以他们在组织内的晋升速度来衡量。他们花最多时间在**社交网络**（社交、政治活动、与外部人员互动）上。

Effective managers are measured by the quality and quantity of their unit's performance. They spend the most time on **communication** (exchanging routine information and processing paperwork).

有效管理者以其部门绩效的质量和数量来衡量。他们花最多时间在**沟通**（交换日常信息、处理文书工作）上。

> **Luthans Research Finding**: Successful managers and effective managers are NOT the same people!  
> **Luthans研究发现**：成功管理者和有效管理者不是同一批人！

#### 管理者活动对比 / Managerial Activities Comparison

| 活动 / Activity | 成功管理者 / Successful Managers | 有效管理者 / Effective Managers |
|-----------------|----------------------------------|--------------------------------|
| Traditional Management | 13% (第3) | 19% (第3) |
| Communication | 28% (第2) | **44% (第1)** |
| Human Resource Management | 11% (第4) | 26% (第2) |
| **Networking** | **48% (第1)** | 11% (第4) |

### 四、OB模型的三大层次 / Three Levels of OB Analysis

The OB model has three levels of analysis: individual, group, and organizational. Each level builds on the lower level.

OB模型有三个分析层次：个体、群体和组织。每个层次都建立在较低层次之上。

```
INPUTS → PROCESSES → OUTCOMES

┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  Individual Level (Ch4-8)   │ 个体层面            │
│  Group Level (Ch10-14)      │ 群体层面            │
│  Organizational Level       │ 组织层面            │
│  (Ch15-17)                  │                     │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

#### 1. Inputs（输入）

Inputs are variables like personality, group structure, and organizational culture that lead to processes. Think of them as "nouns" — pre-existing conditions.

输入是导致过程的变量，如人格、群体结构、组织文化。可以理解为"名词"——预先存在的条件。

#### 2. Processes（过程）

Processes are actions that individuals, groups, and organizations engage in as a result of inputs and that lead to certain outcomes. Think of them as "verbs" — actions that occur.

过程是个体、群体和组织因输入而采取的行动，导致特定结果。可以理解为"动词"——实际发生的行为。

| 层次 / Level | 过程 / Processes |
|--------------|-----------------|
| Individual / 个体 | Emotions, Motivation, Perception, Decision Making |
| Group / 群体 | Communication, Leadership, Power & Politics, Conflict |
| Organizational / 组织 | HR Management, Change Practices |

#### 3. Outcomes（结果）

Outcomes are key factors that are affected by some other variables. They are the results we want to explain and predict.

结果是受其他变量影响的关键因素。它们是我们想要解释和预测的结果。

---

### 五、OB的核心结果变量 / Key OB Outcomes

#### 个体层面 / Individual Level

**Task Performance**

Task performance is the effectiveness and efficiency with which an employee performs core job tasks. It is the most important outcome in OB.

任务绩效是员工执行核心工作任务的有效性和效率。它是OB中最重要的结果。

**Organizational Citizenship Behavior (OCB)**

OCB is discretionary behavior that is not part of an employee's formal job requirements but contributes to the psychological and social environment of the workplace.

组织公民行为是超出员工正式职责的自愿行为，但有助于工作场所的心理和社会环境。

**Withdrawal Behavior**

Withdrawal behavior refers to actions employees take to separate themselves from the organization, including absenteeism and turnover.

退缩行为是指员工为与组织分离而采取的行动，包括缺勤和离职。

#### 群体层面 / Group Level

**Group Cohesion**

Group cohesion is the degree to which group members support and validate each other. High cohesion leads to better group functioning.

群体凝聚力是群体成员相互支持和验证的程度。高凝聚力带来更好的群体功能。

#### 组织层面 / Organizational Level

**Productivity**

Productivity requires both effectiveness (achieving goals) and efficiency (achieving goals at low cost).

生产力既需要有效性（达到目标），也需要效率（低成本达到目标）。

**Organizational Survival**

An organization's long-term ability to exist and grow is measured by its survival.

组织的长期生存和成长能力通过其生存来衡量。

> **Key Formula**: Productivity = Effectiveness + Efficiency  
> **关键公式**：生产力 = 有效性 + 效率
