## 第一部分：理论基础 / Theory Foundations

### 一、冲突定义与类型 / Definition and Types of Conflict

Conflict is a process that begins when one party perceives that another party has negatively affected, or is about to negatively affect, something that the first party cares about. If no one is aware of a conflict, then generally agreed no conflict exists.

冲突是一个过程，当一方感知到另一方已经或即将对其关心的事物产生负面影响时开始。如果没有人意识到冲突，那么一般认为冲突不存在。

**Functional Conflict (功能冲突)**: Supports the goals of the group and improves its performance; constructive form of conflict. Example: debate about the most efficient way to improve production.
支持群体目标并提高绩效；建设性冲突形式。

**Dysfunctional Conflict (功能失调冲突)**: Hinders group performance; destructive. Example: highly personal struggle for control that distracts from the task.
阻碍群体绩效；破坏性。

**Three Types of Conflict (三种冲突类型)**:

1. **Relationship Conflict (关系冲突)**: Focuses on interpersonal relationships. Almost always dysfunctional in work settings. Increases personality clashes and decreases mutual understanding. Most psychologically exhausting.
   关注人际关系。在工作环境中几乎总是功能失调的。增加人格冲突并减少相互理解。心理消耗最大。

2. **Task Conflict (任务冲突)**: Relates to content and goals of the work. Moderate levels can be beneficial — stimulate discussion, promote critical assessment. Among top management teams: positive association with performance. Among lower levels: negative association. Too low = not engaged; too high = degenerate into relationship conflict. Moderate levels optimal for creativity.
   与工作内容和目标相关。中等水平可能有益——激发讨论、促进批判性评估。高管团队中与绩效正相关；低层级中与绩效负相关。太低=不投入；太高=退化为关系冲突。中等水平对创造力最优。

3. **Process Conflict (过程冲突)**: About how the work gets done. Conflicts over delegation and roles. Often become highly personalized and quickly devolve into relationship conflicts.
   关于工作如何完成。关于授权和角色的冲突。经常变得高度个性化并迅速退化为关系冲突。

### 二、冲突位置 / Loci of Conflict

1. **Dyadic Conflict (二元冲突)**: Between two people.
   两个人之间。

2. **Intragroup Conflict (群体内冲突)**: Within a group or team. Requires supportive climate for positive effects.
   在群体或团队内部。需要支持性氛围才能产生积极效果。

3. **Intergroup Conflict (群体间冲突)**: Between groups or teams. Inevitable when groups compete. Peripheral members may be better at resolving intergroup conflicts.
   在群体或团队之间。当群体竞争时不可避免。边缘成员可能更擅长解决群体间冲突。

### 三、冲突过程五阶段 / The Conflict Process (Five Stages)

**Stage I: Potential Opposition or Incompatibility (潜在对立或不兼容)**

Three categories of conditions:
1. **Communication**: Semantic difficulties, misunderstandings, noise, jargon, insufficient information. Too little OR too much communication increases conflict potential.
   语义困难、误解、噪音、行话、信息不足。太少或太多沟通都增加冲突可能性。

2. **Structure**: Size, specialization, jurisdictional clarity, goal compatibility, leadership styles, reward systems, dependence. Larger groups and more specialization = more conflict. Tenure and conflict are inversely related.
   规模、专业化、管辖清晰度、目标兼容性、领导风格、奖励系统、依赖性。更大的群体和更多专业化=更多冲突。任期与冲突呈反比。

3. **Personal Variables**: Personality (disagreeableness, neuroticism, self-monitoring), emotions, values. Differences in values generate increased conflict.
   人格（不宜人性、神经质、自我监控）、情绪、价值观。价值观差异增加冲突。

**Stage II: Cognition and Personalization (认知和个性化)**

**Perceived Conflict (感知冲突)**: Awareness that antecedent conditions exist.
意识到先决条件存在。

**Felt Conflict (感受冲突)**: Emotional involvement — anxiety, tension, frustration, hostility. This is where conflict issues tend to be defined.
情感卷入——焦虑、紧张、挫败、敌意。这是冲突问题被定义的地方。

Emotions shape perceptions: negative emotions → oversimplify issues, lose trust; positive feelings → see potential relationships, broader view, innovative solutions.
情绪塑造感知：负面情绪→过度简化问题、失去信任；积极情感→看到潜在关系、更广阔的视野、创新解决方案。

**Stage III: Intentions (意图)**

Five conflict-handling intentions based on assertiveness and cooperativeness:
基于果断性和合作性的五种冲突处理意图：

1. **Competing (竞争)**: Assertive + uncooperative. Seek to satisfy own interests regardless of impact on others.
   果断+不合作。追求满足自身利益，不管对他人的影响。

2. **Collaborating (合作)**: Assertive + cooperative. Fully satisfy concerns of all parties; search for mutually beneficial outcome. Openness and collaborating associated with superior performance.
   果断+合作。完全满足各方关注；寻求互利的结果。开放和合作与卓越绩效相关。

3. **Avoiding (回避)**: Unassertive + uncooperative. Withdraw from or suppress conflict. Associated with worse performance.
   不果断+不合作。从冲突中退出或抑制。与较差绩效相关。

4. **Accommodating (迁就)**: Unassertive + cooperative. Place opponent's interests above own; sacrifice to maintain relationship.
   不果断+合作。将对方的利益置于自己之上；牺牲以维持关系。

5. **Compromising (妥协)**: Mid-range on both dimensions. Each party gives up something; no winner or loser.
   两个维度都中等。各方都放弃一些东西；没有赢家或输家。

> **Key Point**: Openness and collaborating = superior performance. Avoiding and competing = significantly worse performance.  
> **记忆要点**：开放和合作=卓越绩效。回避和竞争=显著更差的绩效。

**Stage IV: Behavior (行为)**

Dynamic process of interaction. Conflicts at lower levels are typically functional; conflicts at upper ranges (strikes, riots, wars) are almost always dysfunctional. Functional conflicts confined to lower levels.
互动的动态过程。低级别的冲突通常是功能性的；高级别的冲突（罢工、骚乱、战争）几乎总是功能失调的。功能性冲突局限于较低级别。

**Stage V: Outcomes (结果)**

**Functional Outcomes (功能性结果)**: Improve decision quality, stimulate creativity, encourage curiosity, provide medium for problems to be aired, foster self-evaluation and change. Conflict is an antidote for groupthink.
提高决策质量、激发创造力、鼓励好奇心、提供问题表达的媒介、促进自我评估和变革。冲突是群体思维的解毒剂。

**Dysfunctional Outcomes (功能失调结果)**: Uncontrolled opposition breeds discontent, dissolves common ties, poor communication, reductions in cohesiveness, subordination of group goals to infighting. All forms of conflict reduce member satisfaction and trust.
不受控制的反对滋生不满、解散共同联系、沟通不良、凝聚力降低、群体目标从属于内斗。所有形式的冲突都降低成员满意度和信任。

### 四、谈判 / Negotiation

Negotiation is a process that occurs when two or more parties decide how to allocate scarce resources.

谈判是当两个或更多方决定如何分配稀缺资源时发生的过程。

**Distributive Bargaining (分配式谈判)**:
- Operates under zero-sum conditions — any gain I make is at your expense
- Fixed pie belief
- Focus on positions ("I can't go beyond this point")
- Low information sharing
- Short-term relationship
- **Best strategy**: Make the first offer (aggressive) — anchoring bias favors the person who sets it

在零和条件下运作——我的任何收益都是以你的损失为代价
固定馅饼信念
关注立场（"我不能超越这一点"）
低信息共享
短期关系
**最佳策略**：第一个报价（激进的）——锚定偏差有利于设定锚的人

**Integrative Bargaining (整合式谈判)**:
- Win-win solution possible
- Expand the pie
- Focus on interests ("Can you explain why this is important?")
- High information sharing
- Long-term relationship
- Preferable for intraorganizational behavior — builds long-term relationships

可能实现双赢解决方案
扩大馅饼
关注利益（"你能解释为什么这很重要吗？"）
高信息共享
长期关系
更适合组织内行为——建立长期关系

**The Negotiation Process (谈判过程)**:
1. Preparation and planning (BATNA — Best Alternative To a Negotiated Agreement)
2. Definition of ground rules
3. Clarification and justification
4. Bargaining and problem solving
5. Closure and implementation

准备和计划（BATNA——谈判协议的最佳替代方案）
基本规则定义
澄清和证明
讨价还价和解决问题
结束和实施

### 五、谈判中的个体差异 / Individual Differences in Negotiation

**Personality (人格)**: Self-efficacy consistently relates to negotiation outcomes — those who believe they will be successful perform more effectively. Agreeableness weakly related.
自我效能与谈判结果始终相关——那些相信自己会成功的人表现更有效。宜人性弱相关。

**Moods/Emotions (情绪)**:
- Genuine anger can induce concessions (only if you have at least as much power)
- Disappointment → counterpart concedes more
- Anxiety → more deception, expect lower outcomes, exit quickly
- Emotional unpredictability → extract more concessions

真实愤怒可能促使让步（仅当你至少拥有同等权力时）
失望→对方让步更多
焦虑→更多欺骗、预期更低结果、快速退出
情绪不可预测性→获取更多让步

**Culture (文化)**: People negotiate more effectively within cultures than between them. Cross-cultural negotiators should be high in openness. East Asians showing anger → more concessions (stereotype effect).
人们在文化内部比跨文化谈判更有效。跨文化谈判者应具有高开放性。东亚人表现愤怒→更多让步（刻板印象效应）。

**Gender (性别)**: Men tend to value status/power/recognition; women value compassion/relationship outcomes. Women more reluctant to initiate negotiations, ask for less, make more generous offers. Everyone is better at advocating for others than for themselves.
男性倾向于重视地位/权力/认可；女性重视同情/关系结果。女性更不愿发起谈判、要求更少、提供更慷慨的报价。每个人在为他人辩护时都比为自己辩护更好。

### 六、第三方谈判 / Third-Party Negotiations

1. **Mediator (调解人)**: Neutral third party who facilitates solution through reasoning, persuasion, suggestions. Most effective under moderate conflict levels; must be perceived as neutral and noncoercive.
   通过推理、说服、建议促进解决方案的中立第三方。在中等冲突水平下最有效；必须被视为中立和非强制的。

2. **Arbitrator (仲裁人)**: Has authority to dictate an agreement. Always results in settlement, but may leave one party feeling defeated.
   有权裁定协议。总是导致和解，但可能让一方感到失败。

3. **Conciliator (调停人)**: Trusted third party providing informal communication link between negotiator and opponent. Engages in fact-finding, interpreting messages, persuading.
   在谈判者和对手之间提供非正式沟通链接的可信第三方。参与事实调查、解释信息、说服。

## 第三部分：考点总结 / Key Exam Points

### 🔴 高频考点（必考）

1. **冲突五阶段 / Five Stages of Conflict Process**: Potential opposition → Cognition/personalization → Intentions → Behavior → Outcomes
2. **三种冲突类型 / Three Types of Conflict**: Relationship (dysfunctional), Task (moderate=beneficial), Process (usually dysfunctional)
3. **五种冲突处理意图 / Five Conflict-Handling Intentions**: Based on assertiveness and cooperativeness; collaborating = best, competing/avoiding = worst
4. **分配式vs整合式谈判 / Distributive vs. Integrative Bargaining**: Zero-sum vs. win-win; fixed pie vs. expand pie
5. **BATNA / Best Alternative To Negotiated Agreement**: Determines lowest acceptable value
6. **冲突的功能性vs功能失调结果 / Functional vs. Dysfunctional Outcomes**: Functional improves creativity/decisions; dysfunctional reduces cohesiveness/satisfaction

### 🟡 中频考点（常考）

1. **谈判中的锚定偏差 / Anchoring Bias in Negotiation**: First offer advantage
2. **情绪在谈判中的作用 / Role of Emotions in Negotiation**: Anger, disappointment, anxiety, unpredictability
3. **谈判的性别差异 / Gender Differences in Negotiation**: Men vs. women values and behaviors
4. **第三方谈判角色 / Third-Party Roles**: Mediator, arbitrator, conciliator
5. **跨文化谈判 / Cross-Cultural Negotiation**: Openness important; within-culture more effective

### 🟢 低频考点（了解）

1. **冲突位置 / Loci of Conflict**: Dyadic, intragroup, intergroup
2. **群体间冲突中边缘成员的作用 / Peripheral Members in Intergroup Conflict**
3. **社会声誉和关系在谈判中的作用 / Reputation and Relationships in Negotiation**

**Q1:** Which type of conflict is almost always dysfunctional?
A. Task conflict
B. Process conflict
C. Relationship conflict
D. Intergroup conflict

<summary>查看答案 / Answer</summary>
**Answer:** C — Relationship conflicts are almost always dysfunctional in work settings.
关系冲突在工作环境中几乎总是功能失调的。

**Q2:** Which conflict-handling intention is assertive AND cooperative?
A. Competing
B. Collaborating
C. Avoiding
D. Accommodating

合作在果断性和合作性上都很高。

A. Wait for the other party to make the first offer
B. Make the first aggressive offer

第一个激进的报价利用了锚定偏差。

真实愤怒可能促使让步，但仅当你至少拥有同等权力时。

### 论述题 / Essay Question

**Q8:** Discuss the five stages of the conflict process and explain how managers can manage conflict effectively.

<details>
<summary>查看答案 / Answer Template</summary>

**Standard Answer:**
1. **Five Stages**: (I) Potential opposition — communication, structure, personal variables create conditions. (II) Cognition and personalization — perceived and felt conflict; emotions shape perceptions. (III) Intentions — five handling approaches based on assertiveness/cooperativeness. (IV) Behavior — dynamic interaction; lower levels functional, upper levels dysfunctional. (V) Outcomes — functional (improves creativity) or dysfunctional (reduces cohesiveness).
2. **Management Strategies**: (a) Recognize real disagreements (different verbiage for same issue). (b) Encourage open, frank discussions focused on interests. (c) Emphasize shared interests. (d) Use collaborative or compromising approaches. (e) Match conflict-handling style to situation.
</details>
