1. Digital Systems Case
Top management at Digital Systems was not happy with corporate performance in 2024. So, in January 2025 the CEO, John Anderson created a new incentive pay plan where store managers would get quarterly bonuses if they achieved their performance goals. Following the first quarter (January – March 2025) under the new bonus program, over 75 percent of all store managers qualified for the bonus.
Question 1:
Do you think the store managers were motivated by the incentive plan? Explain your answer utilizing one of the following theories of motivation (expectancy, equity, reinforcement). Be sure to write down which theory you are applying. Relate your answer to the specifics of the case.
