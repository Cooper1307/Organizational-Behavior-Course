### Case Analysis: Digital Systems Incentive Plan

**Question 1:**
Do you think the store managers were motivated by the incentive plan? Explain your answer utilizing one of the theories of motivation (expectancy, equity, reinforcement). Be sure to write down which theory you are applying. Relate your answer to the specifics of the case.

**Response:**
I believe the store managers were definitely motivated by this new plan, and **Expectancy Theory** (Vroom, 1964) helps explain why it worked so well. First, the fact that over 75% of managers hit their targets shows that they felt the goals were actually doable with reasonable effort, which represents high **Expectancy**. The incentive was also very straightforward; the CEO promised a clear quarterly bonus for hitting specific numbers, building strong **Instrumentality** because managers knew exactly what to expect if they performed. Finally, most managers obviously value extra financial pay, giving the bonus positive **Valence**. Basically, the plan was successful in the first quarter because it connected reachable goals directly to a reward that the managers actually wanted.
