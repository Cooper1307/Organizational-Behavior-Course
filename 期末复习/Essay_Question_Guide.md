## 第一部分：论述题通用答题策略 / Universal Essay Strategy

### 五步标准答题框架 / Five-Step Answer Framework

**Step 1: Define Key Concepts / 定义核心概念**
- Start with clear, textbook definitions of all key terms in the question
- 以清晰的教材定义开始，回答所有关键术语
- Example: "Transformational leadership is a style that inspires followers to transcend self-interest for organizational good."

**Step 2: Identify Theory or Model / 识别理论或模型**
- Name the relevant theory, model, or framework
- 命名相关理论、模型或框架
- Include the theorist's name if known (e.g., Herzberg, Maslow, Fiedler)

**Step 3: Compare/Analyze/Explain / 比较/分析/解释**
- Provide the main body: compare theories, explain relationships, analyze effectiveness
- 提供主体内容：比较理论、解释关系、分析有效性
- Use evidence from research (e.g., "Research shows a strong positive correlation...")

**Step 4: Provide Examples or Applications / 提供示例或应用**
- Include practical examples to illustrate your points
- 包含实际示例来说明观点
- Example: "A manager dealing with layoffs should use face-to-face communication (rich channel) rather than email."

**Step 5: Conclude with Managerial Implications / 以管理启示总结**
- End with practical implications for managers
- 以管理者的实践启示结束
- Example: "Therefore, managers should select the leadership style that fits the situation rather than using one universal approach."

### Ch1 OB导论 / OB Introduction

**题目示例 / Sample Question:** Explain why OB matters for managers.

**答题框架 / Answer Framework:**
1. OB helps managers develop **interpersonal skills** to attract and retain high-performing employees.
2. Incorporating OB principles can **transform workplace performance** and improve financial outcomes.
3. OB improves **workplace relationships**, leading to higher job satisfaction and lower turnover.
4. OB helps managers address **contemporary challenges** like diversity, social media, and employee well-being.
5. OB promotes **ethical behavior** and corporate social responsibility awareness.

**得分关键词 / Key Score Words:** interpersonal skills, productivity, OCB, withdrawal behavior, effective vs. successful manager

---

### Ch2 工作态度 / Job Attitudes

**题目示例 / Sample Question:** Explain the relationship between job satisfaction and job performance.

**答题框架 / Answer Framework:**
1. Research shows a **strong positive correlation** between job satisfaction and job performance.
2. A review of **300 studies** confirmed this relationship is robust across many contexts.
3. Organizations with more satisfied employees tend to be **more effective** and productive.
4. This is partly because satisfied employees are more likely to engage in **OCB** and less likely to engage in **withdrawal behavior** (absenteeism, turnover).

**得分关键词 / Key Score Words:** cognitive dissonance, EVLN model, POS, single global rating, job conditions

---

### Ch3 情绪 / Emotions

**题目示例 / Sample Question:** Explain the difference between surface acting and deep acting. Which is more effective and why?

**答题框架 / Answer Framework:**
1. **Define Both:** Surface acting = hiding inner feelings and forgoing emotional expressions. Deep acting = trying to modify true inner feelings based on display rules.
2. **Effectiveness:** Deep acting is more effective and less psychologically costly than surface acting.
3. **Why:** Surface acting creates emotional dissonance → emotional exhaustion → burnout. Deep acting creates authentic displays perceived as genuine.
4. **Organizational Interventions:** Select high-EI employees; provide deep acting training; create supportive environment; use mindfulness programs.

**得分关键词 / Key Score Words:** emotional labor, emotional dissonance, Affective Events Theory, emotional intelligence, surface vs. deep acting

---

### Ch4 人格因素 / Personality

**题目示例 / Sample Question:** Explain how personality affects workplace behavior.

**答题框架 / Answer Framework:**
1. Personality is the sum total of ways an individual reacts and interacts with others, largely determined by heredity.
2. The Big Five model (OCEAN): Openness, Conscientiousness, Extraversion, Agreeableness, Emotional Stability.
3. **Conscientiousness** is the strongest predictor of job performance across all jobs.
4. Person-job fit theory (Holland): matching personality to occupational environment increases satisfaction and reduces turnover.
5. Cultural values (Hofstede) also influence workplace behavior.

**得分关键词 / Key Score Words:** Big Five/OCEAN, MBTI, Dark Triad, core self-evaluation, proactive personality, person-job fit, Hofstede's dimensions

---

### Ch5 知觉与决策 / Perception & Decision Making

**题目示例 / Sample Question:** Discuss the three decision-making models (rational, bounded rationality, and intuitive). When might each be most appropriate?

**答题框架 / Answer Framework:**
1. **Rational:** 6-step, value-maximizing, assumes complete information — idealistic but rarely achievable.
2. **Bounded rationality:** Simplified models, satisficing, "good enough" — realistic and practical.
3. **Intuitive:** Unconscious, fast, affectively charged, from distilled experience — fastest but least tested.
4. **When appropriate:** Rational → important strategic decisions with time/resources. Bounded → everyday decisions, time-constrained. Intuitive → experienced professionals, emergencies, hypothesis generator.

**得分关键词 / Key Score Words:** selective perception, halo effect, anchoring bias, confirmation bias, escalation of commitment, bounded rationality, satisficing

---

### Ch6 多元化 / Diversity

**题目示例 / Sample Question:** Discuss the difference between surface-level and deep-level diversity. Why is it important for managers to understand both?

**答题框架 / Answer Framework:**
1. **Surface-level:** easily perceived characteristics (gender, race, age, disability).
2. **Deep-level:** values, personality, work preferences (require getting to know).
3. **Relationship:** Deep-level similarities can overshadow superficial differences; people less concerned about demographics if they share deep-level values.
4. **Managerial implications:** Don't decide solely on surface-level; look beyond demographics to capabilities; encourage disclosure of hidden disabilities; create positive diversity climate.

**得分关键词 / Key Score Words:** surface-level vs. deep-level, biographical characteristics, diversity climate, ADA, discrimination, tokenism

### Ch7 基础动机 / Motivation Basics

**题目示例 / Sample Question:** Compare and contrast Herzberg's Two-Factor Theory with Maslow's Hierarchy of Needs.

**答题框架 / Answer Framework:**
1. **Maslow:** Five needs hierarchy (physiological → safety → social → esteem → self-actualization); as one satisfied, next becomes dominant.
2. **Herzberg:** Two factors — hygiene (prevent dissatisfaction) and motivators (create satisfaction); dual continuum.
3. **Similarities:** Both focus on needs; both are early 1950s theories; both intuitively logical but research support questionable; both still used by managers.
4. **Differences:** Maslow has hierarchy; Herzberg has dual continuum. Hygiene ≈ lower-order needs; motivators ≈ higher-order needs.
5. **Usefulness:** Herzberg more specific about what managers can do (emphasize motivators, not just hygiene).

**得分关键词 / Key Score Words:** Theory X/Y, hierarchy of needs, two-factor theory, hygiene vs. motivators, dual continuum, McClelland's needs (nAch, nPow, nAff)

---

### Ch8 动机应用 / Motivation Applications

**题目示例 / Sample Question:** Compare and contrast the five types of variable-pay programs. When would each be most appropriate?

**答题框架 / Answer Framework:**
1. **Piece-rate:** fixed sum per unit; appropriate when outputs measurable by quantity (production, sales).
2. **Merit-based:** based on performance appraisal; cumulative; appropriate when appraisal is objective and reliable.
3. **Bonus:** rewards recent performance; not cumulative; appropriate when flexibility needed.
4. **Profit-sharing:** based on company profitability; appropriate when profitable and wants to increase commitment.
5. **ESOP:** employees acquire stock; appropriate when wants to reduce unethical behavior and increase ownership feeling.

**得分关键词 / Key Score Words:** job characteristics model, MBO, empowerment, variable-pay, employee recognition, flextime, telecommuting

---

### Ch9 沟通 / Communication

**题目示例 / Sample Question:** Discuss the barriers to effective communication and explain how managers can overcome them.

**答题框架 / Answer Framework:**
1. **Identify Barriers:** filtering, selective perception, information overload, emotions, language, silence, communication apprehension, lying.
2. **Explain Key Barriers:** Filtering = sender manipulates info; selective perception = receiver interprets based on own frame; information overload = exceeds processing capacity.
3. **Overcoming Strategies:** Use feedback; simplify language; listen actively; constrain emotions; watch nonverbal cues.
4. **Channel Selection:** Routine messages → lean channels (email). Nonroutine messages → rich channels (face-to-face).

**得分关键词 / Key Score Words:** channel richness, high-context vs. low-context culture, filtering, information overload, active listening, automatic vs. controlled processing

---

### Ch10 群体基础 / Group Foundations

**题目示例 / Sample Question:** Discuss the strengths and weaknesses of group decision making. When are groups more effective than individuals?

**答题框架 / Answer Framework:**
1. **Strengths:** More complete information and knowledge; increased diversity of views; increased acceptance of solution; members more likely to support.
2. **Weaknesses:** Time-consuming; conformity pressures; dominated by one/few members; ambiguous responsibility.
3. **Effectiveness vs. Efficiency:** Groups are more accurate but slower; more creative but less efficient; higher acceptance but more conformity pressure.
4. **When effective:** Complex tasks requiring diverse expertise; when acceptance is critical; when creativity is valued.
5. **Preventing groupthink:** Encourage devil's advocate; bring in outsiders; use nominal group technique.

**得分关键词 / Key Score Words:** punctuated-equilibrium model, social loafing, groupthink, groupshift, nominal group technique, brainstorming, cohesiveness × norms

---

### Ch11 团队 / Teams

**题目示例 / Sample Question:** Compare and contrast groups and teams. When should organizations use teams?

**答题框架 / Answer Framework:**
1. **Define Both:** Work group → share information; performance = sum of individual inputs. Work team → coordinated effort; positive synergy; performance > sum of inputs.
2. **Key Difference:** Synergy. Groups = no positive synergy. Teams = positive synergy through purposeful, symbiotic effort.
3. **Three Tests for Using Teams:** (a) Can work be done better by more than one person? (b) Does the work create a common purpose? (c) Are members interdependent?
4. **Effective Team Factors:** Context (resources, trust, leadership, rewards); Composition (abilities, personality, size 5-9, roles, diversity); Process (plan, goals, efficacy, identity, cohesion, moderate task conflict).
5. **Turning Individuals into Team Players:** Selecting (hire for interpersonal skills); Training (problem-solving, communication); Rewarding (hybrid individual + team systems).

**得分关键词 / Key Score Words:** positive synergy, self-managed team, cross-functional team, virtual team, reflexivity, team efficacy, task vs. relationship conflict

### Ch12 领导力 / Leadership

**题目示例 / Sample Question:** Compare transformational leadership with transactional leadership. How do they complement each other?

**答题框架 / Answer Framework:**
1. **Transactional:** Guide followers toward established goals; contingent reward, management by exception, laissez-faire; focus on compliance and status quo.
2. **Transformational:** Inspire followers to transcend self-interests; four I's — idealized influence, inspirational motivation, intellectual stimulation, individualized consideration.
3. **Complementary Nature:** Transformational builds on transactional. Best leaders are both. Transformational produces effort beyond transactional alone. Only transactional = mediocre leader.
4. **Evidence:** Transformational more strongly correlated with lower turnover, higher productivity, higher satisfaction. Contingent reward sometimes works as well.
5. **Conclusion:** Full range of leadership model — use both for maximum effectiveness.

**得分关键词 / Key Score Words:** trait theory, initiating structure vs. consideration, Fiedler LPC, SLT readiness, LMX ingroup/outgroup, charismatic leadership, four I's, trust (integrity, benevolence, ability)

---

### Ch13 权力与政治 / Power & Politics

**题目示例 / Sample Question:** Discuss the five bases of power and explain why personal power is more effective than formal power.

**答题框架 / Answer Framework:**
1. **Five Bases:** Coercive (fear-based); Reward (benefits-based); Legitimate (position-based); Expert (knowledge-based); Referent (admiration-based).
2. **Effectiveness:** Expert and referent (personal) → positively related to satisfaction, commitment, performance. Reward and legitimate → unrelated. Coercive → damaging.
3. **Why Personal Better:** Creates genuine commitment rather than mere compliance. Builds trust and respect. Formal power may create resentment or mere obedience.
4. **Dependence Factors:** Importance (resource value); Scarcity (limited availability); Nonsubstitutability (no alternatives).
5. **Power Tactics:** Rational persuasion most effective; pressure least effective (tends to backfire).

**得分关键词 / Key Score Words:** power vs. leadership, formal vs. personal power, dependence, nine power tactics, impression management, political behavior, defensive behaviors

### Ch14 冲突管理 / Conflict Management

**题目示例 / Sample Question:** Discuss the five stages of the conflict process and explain how managers can manage conflict effectively.

**答题框架 / Answer Framework:**
1. **Five Stages:** (I) Potential opposition — communication, structure, personal variables. (II) Cognition and personalization — perceived and felt conflict. (III) Intentions — five handling approaches. (IV) Behavior — dynamic interaction. (V) Outcomes — functional or dysfunctional.
2. **Conflict Types:** Relationship (almost always dysfunctional); Task (moderate levels beneficial); Process (usually dysfunctional).
3. **Five Intentions:** Competing (assertive + uncooperative); Collaborating (assertive + cooperative — best); Avoiding (unassertive + uncooperative); Accommodating (unassertive + cooperative); Compromising (midrange).
4. **Management Strategies:** Recognize real disagreements; encourage open discussion; emphasize shared interests; use collaborative/compromising approaches.
5. **Negotiation:** Distributive (zero-sum, fixed pie) vs. Integrative (win-win, expand pie). BATNA determines lowest acceptable value.

**得分关键词 / Key Score Words:** conflict process stages, task vs. relationship conflict, five intentions, distributive vs. integrative bargaining, BATNA, anchoring bias, third-party negotiation

### Ch15 组织结构 / Organizational Structure

**题目示例 / Sample Question:** Discuss the six elements that define an organization's structure. How does strategy influence structure?

**答题框架 / Answer Framework:**
1. **Seven Elements:** Work specialization; departmentalization; chain of command; span of control; centralization vs. decentralization; formalization; boundary spanning.
2. **Five Departmentalization Types:** Functional, product, geographic, process, customer.
3. **Common Frameworks:** Simple structure (low departmentalization, wide spans, centralized); Bureaucracy (high specialization, formalization, centralized); Matrix structure (dual chain of command, breaks unity of command).
4. **Strategy-Structure Match:** Innovation strategy → organic structure. Cost-minimization strategy → mechanistic structure. Imitation strategy → mixed (mechanistic for tight control + organic for new ventures).
5. **Environmental Factors:** Capacity (rich vs. lean); Volatility (dynamic vs. stable); Complexity (degree of differentiation).

**得分关键词 / Key Score Words:** mechanistic vs. organic, matrix structure, span of control, centralization, formalization, strategy-structure fit, environment dimensions

---

### Ch16 组织文化 / Organizational Culture

**题目示例 / Sample Question:** Explain how organizational culture is created, maintained, and transmitted to employees.

**答题框架 / Answer Framework:**
1. **Seven Cultural Characteristics:** Innovation, attention to detail, outcome orientation, people orientation, team orientation, aggressiveness, stability.
2. **Culture Creation (Founders):** (a) Hire and keep employees who think and feel the way they do. (b) Socialize employees to think and feel as they do. (c) Own behavior acts as role model.
3. **Socialization Three Stages:** Prearrival (prior learning) → Encounter (see what organization is really like) → Metamorphosis (work out problems, internalize values).
4. **Culture Transmission (Four Forms):** Stories (anchor in present); Rituals (repetitive sequences); Material symbols (office layout, dress); Language (jargon, acronyms).
5. **Maintaining Culture:** Selection (identify those who fit); Top management (behavior establishes norms); Socialization (helps adapt).

**得分关键词 / Key Score Words:** dominant culture vs. subculture, strong vs. weak culture, socialization stages, culture transmission forms, ethical culture, positive organizational culture, spirituality

---

### Ch17 组织变革 / Organizational Change

**题目示例 / Sample Question:** Discuss the sources of resistance to change and evaluate the eight tactics for overcoming resistance.

**答题框架 / Answer Framework:**
1. **Individual Sources (5):** Habit (programmed responses); Security (threatens safety); Economic factors (fear of lowered productivity); Fear of unknown (ambiguity); Selective processing (keep perceptions intact).
2. **Organizational Sources (5):** Structural inertia (built-in stability mechanisms); Limited focus (interdependent subsystems); Group inertia (norms as constraints); Threat to expertise; Threat to established power relationships.
3. **Most Effective Tactics:** Communication; participation; building support and commitment; positive relationships; fair implementation. These build trust and genuine commitment.
4. **Least Effective Tactics:** Manipulation; cooptation; coercion. These are covert and can backfire when discovered.
5. **Best Practice:** Begin with softer tactics; move to harder tactics only if necessary. Select people predisposed to accept change as proactive strategy.
6. **OD Techniques:** Sensitivity training; survey feedback; process consultation; team building; intergroup development; appreciative inquiry (discovery → dreaming → design → destiny).

**得分关键词 / Key Score Words:** Lewin's 3-step model, Kotter's 8 steps, action research, resistance sources (individual + organizational), 8 tactics, OD techniques, challenge vs. hindrance stressors, stress management

## 第三部分：常见论述题类型及应对策略 / Essay Types & Strategies

### 类型一：理论比较 / Theory Comparison

**识别特征 / Identifying Features:** "Compare and contrast...", "Discuss the similarities and differences..."

**答题模板 / Answer Template:**
1. Define Theory A / 定义理论A
2. Define Theory B / 定义理论B
3. Similarities (2-3 points) / 相似之处（2-3点）
4. Differences (2-3 points) / 不同之处（2-3点）
5. Which is more useful/practical and why / 哪个更有用/实用及原因

**适用章节 / Applicable Chapters:** Ch7 (Maslow vs. Herzberg), Ch8 (variable-pay types), Ch11 (groups vs. teams), Ch12 (transformational vs. transactional), Ch14 (distributive vs. integrative)

---

### 类型二：因素分析 / Factor Analysis

**识别特征 / Identifying Features:** "Discuss the factors that...", "What contributes to...", "Explain the sources of..."

**答题模板 / Answer Template:**
1. Define the phenomenon / 定义现象
2. List and explain each factor (4-6 factors) / 列出并解释每个因素（4-6个）
3. Categorize if applicable (e.g., individual vs. organizational) / 如适用则分类
4. Practical implications / 实践启示

**适用章节 / Applicable Chapters:** Ch4 (personality factors), Ch5 (biases), Ch10 (group effectiveness), Ch11 (team effectiveness), Ch13 (power bases), Ch17 (resistance sources, stress sources)

---

### 类型三：过程/阶段描述 / Process/Stage Description

**识别特征 / Identifying Features:** "Describe the stages of...", "Explain the process of...", "Discuss the steps in..."

**答题模板 / Answer Template:**
1. Define the process / 定义过程
2. Describe each stage in order / 按顺序描述每个阶段
3. Explain what happens at each stage / 解释每个阶段发生什么
4. Managerial implications / 管理启示

**适用章节 / Applicable Chapters:** Ch5 (rational decision-making 6 steps), Ch10 (punctuated-equilibrium 6 phases), Ch14 (conflict process 5 stages), Ch16 (socialization 3 stages), Ch17 (Lewin's 3 steps, Kotter's 8 steps, action research 5 steps, appreciative inquiry 4 steps)

### 类型四：管理应用 / Managerial Application

**识别特征 / Identifying Features:** "How should managers...", "What can organizations do to...", "Discuss practical implications..."

**答题模板 / Answer Template:**
1. Define the issue / 定义问题
2. Explain why it matters / 解释为什么重要
3. List specific strategies (4-6 strategies) / 列出具体策略（4-6个）
4. Provide examples for each / 为每个提供示例
5. Conclude with expected outcomes / 以预期结果总结

**适用章节 / Applicable Chapters:** Ch2 (increasing job satisfaction), Ch3 (managing emotional labor), Ch8 (motivating employees), Ch9 (overcoming barriers), Ch11 (turning individuals into team players), Ch17 (managing stress, overcoming resistance)

## 第四部分：答题常见错误及避免方法 / Common Mistakes & How to Avoid

| 错误 / Mistake | 避免方法 / How to Avoid |
|----------------|------------------------|
| 只定义不解释 / Define only, no explanation | Always follow definition with explanation and example |
| 只列理论不提应用 / List theory without application | Always end with managerial implications |
| 混淆相似概念 / Confuse similar concepts | Create comparison tables; use keywords to distinguish |
| 答案没有结构 / No structured answer | Use 5-step framework; number your points |
| 缺少研究证据 / No research evidence | Cite specific studies or numbers (e.g., "300 studies confirmed...") |
| 回答不完整 / Incomplete answer | Check question for multiple parts; address each part |
| 只写英文或只写中文 / Only English or only Chinese | Use bilingual if exam allows; otherwise use the exam language consistently |
