# 个人介绍及测试要求 (Individual Introduction & Test Requirements)

## Part 2 — Individual Introduction (1 slide per person)
## 第二部分：个人介绍 (每人 1 页 PPT)

Each group member should prepare ONE slide including:
每个小组人员应准备 1 页 PPT，包括：

### 1. Basic Introduction 基本介绍
- Name / 姓名
- A clear and recognizable photo (must show your face clearly) / 一张清晰可辨的照片（必须清晰显示面部）
- Interests / hobbies / 兴趣爱好

### 2. Personality (Jung Typology Test) 人格测验结果
- **Testing Link / 测试链接:** [https://www.humanmetrics.com/personality](https://www.humanmetrics.com/personality)
- **Include / 包含内容:**
    - Your personality type (e.g., ESFJ) / 你的性格类型（例如：ESFJ）
    - Your percentages / 各项特征百分比
- **Example / 示例:**
    - ESFJ
    - Extravert (12%) Sensing (28%) Feeling (41%) Judging (12%)

### 3. Motivation Test 动机测验结果
- Based on the "What Motivates You?" test
- **Include your scores / 包含你的得分:**
    - Achievement (成就): ___
    - Power (权力): ___
    - Affiliation (归属): ___

### 4. Reflection 反思
Briefly answer / 简要回答：
- What do your personality and motivation tell about you? / 你的性格和动机反映了你是一个怎样的人？
- What do you think is the most challenging part of this course? / 你认为这门课程最具挑战性的部分是什么？
- Expectations for this course / 对这门课程的期望
