## Sociology (社会学 - 续)

...most importantly, sociologists have studied organizational culture, formal organization theory and structure, organizational technology, communications, power, and conflict.
最重要的是，社会学家研究了组织文化、正式组织理论与结构、组织技术、沟通、权力和冲突。

**术语对照 (Terminology):**

* **Organizational culture**: 组织文化
* **Formal organization theory**: 正式组织理论
* **Organizational technology**: 组织技术
* **Communications**: 沟通
* **Conflict**: 冲突

---

## Anthropology (人类学)

Anthropology is the study of societies in order to learn about human beings and their activities.
人类学是对社会的研究，目的是了解人类及其活动。

**术语对照 (Terminology):**

* **Anthropology**: 人类学
* **Societies**: 社会

Anthropologists’ work on cultures and environments has helped us understand differences in fundamental values, attitudes, and behavior among people in different countries and within different organizations.
人类学家对文化和环境的研究帮助我们理解了不同国家、不同组织中人们在基本价值观、态度和行为方面的差异。

**术语对照 (Terminology):**

* **Fundamental values**: 基本价值观
* **Attitudes**: 态度
* **Behavior**: 行为

Much of our current understanding of organizational culture, organizational climate, and differences among national cultures is a result of the work of anthropologists or those using their methods.
目前我们对组织文化、组织气候以及国家文化差异的理解，很大程度上是人类学家或使用其方法的研究者的工作成果。

**术语对照 (Terminology):**

* **Organizational climate**: 组织气候
* **National cultures**: 国家文化

**Sidebar Definition (侧边栏定义)**:
Anthropology: The study of societies to learn about human beings and their activities.
人类学：研究社会以了解人类及其活动的学科。

## THERE ARE FEW ABSOLUTES IN OB (组织行为学中几乎没有绝对真理)

Laws in the physical sciences—chemistry, astronomy, physics—are consistent and apply in a wide range of situations.
自然科学（如化学、天文学、物理学）的规律是连贯的，并适用于各种情境。

**术语对照 (Terminology):**

* **Physical sciences**: 自然科学/物理科学
* **Consistent**: 连贯的/一致的

They allow scientists to generalize about the pull of gravity or to be confident about sending astronauts into space to repair satellites.
它们允许科学家对重力进行概括，或者对派宇航员进入太空维修卫星充满信心。

Human beings are complex, and few, if any, simple and universal principles explain human behavior.
人类是复杂的，几乎没有任何简单且普遍的原理能够解释人类行为。

**术语对照 (Terminology):**

* **Universal principles**: 普遍原理
* **Human behavior**: 人类行为

Because we are not alike, our ability to make simple, accurate, and sweeping generalizations about ourselves is limited.
因为我们各不相同，我们对自己做出简单、准确且笼统概括的能力是有限的。

**术语对照 (Terminology):**

* **Generalizations**: 概括/归纳
* **Limited**: 有限的
