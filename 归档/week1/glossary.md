# 组织行为学词汇表 (OB Glossary)

本词汇表汇集了教材（第 14 版）及讨论中出现的核心术语与难懂表达。

## A

* **Anthropology (人类学)**: The study of societies in order to learn about human beings and their activities. (对社会的研究，目的是了解人类及其活动。)
* **Attitudes (态度)**: Evaluative statements or judgments concerning objects, people, or events. (对物体、人、或事件的评价性陈述或判断。)
* **Avoidance of Conflict (回避冲突)**: A leader's failure to address disagreements or problematic issues. (领导者未能处理分歧或问题。)

## C

* **Citizenship behavior (公民行为 / 组织公民行为 OCB)**: Discretionary behavior that contributes to the psychological and social environment of the workplace. (有助于工作场所心理和社会环境的自发性行为。)
* **Confidence and Decisiveness (自信与果敢)**: Positively influencing others through self-assurance and firm decision-making. (通过自信和坚定的决策积极影响他人。)
* **Consideration / People-oriented (关怀型/以人为本)**: Showing concern for employees' needs and well-being. (表现出对员工需求和福祉的关注。)
* **Contingency variables (权变变量)**: Situational factors or variables that moderate the relationship between two or more variables. (调节两个或多个变量之间关系的境况因素或变量。)
* **Controlling (控制)**: Monitoring activities to ensure they are being accomplished as planned. (监控活动以确保按计划完成。)

## D

* **Decision making (决策)**: The process of choosing between two or more alternatives. (在两个或两个以上方案中进行选择的过程。)

## E

* **Effective Manager (绩优型管理者)**: A manager who defines excellence based on the quantity and quality of performance and satisfaction/commitment of subordinates. (根据绩效水平及其下属的满意度和承诺度来定义的优秀管理者。)
* **Emotional Intelligence (情感智力/情商 EQ)**: The ability to detect and manage emotional cues and information. (察觉并管理情感线索和信息的能力。)
* **Emotions and moods (情绪与心境)**: Affective states that individuals experience. (个体所经历的情感状态。)
* **Expatriate (外派人员)**: Employees who are sent by their companies to work in another country for a long period. (由公司派往其他国家长期工作的人员。)

## F

* **Flexpatriate (灵活外派人员)**: Employees who travel for brief periods of time for work abroad but do not stay for long periods. (频繁进行简短国际差旅处理全球业务但不长期定居的人员。)
* **Furloughed (停薪留职)**: A temporary leave of employees due to special needs of a company or employer. (由于公司原因导致的暂时性休假，通常保留职位但无薪。)

## G

* **Globalization (全球化)**: The process of organizations developing international influence or starting to operate on an international scale. (组织发展国际影响力或开始在国际规模上运作的过程。)
* **Group cohesion (群体凝聚力)**: The degree to which members of a group are attracted to each other and motivated to stay in the group. (群体成员相互吸引并愿意留在群体中的程度。)
* **Group functioning (群体运行)**: The quantity and quality of a group’s work output. (群体工作产出的数量和质量。)

## I

* **Inputs (输入)**: Variables like personality, group structure, and organizational culture that lead to processes. (导致过程的变量，如人格、群体结构和组织文化。)

## L

* **Laid off (解雇/裁员)**: A temporary or permanent termination of employment by an employer for reasons unrelated to the employee's performance. (非因绩效原因导致的雇佣终止。)

## M

* **Motivation (动机)**: The processes that account for an individual’s intensity, direction, and persistence of effort toward attaining a goal. (解释个体为实现目标而付出的努力强度、方向和持久性的过程。)

## O

* **Networking (网络联系)**: Socializing, politicking, and interacting with outsiders. (社交、政治活动以及与外部人员的互动。)
* **Organizational Behavior (OB, 组织行为学)**: A field of study that investigates the impact that individuals, groups, and structure have on behavior within organizations. (研究个体、群体和结构对组织内行为影响的领域。)
* **Outcomes (产出)**: Key factors that are affected by some other variables. (受其他变量影响的关键因素。)

## P

* **Perception (知觉)**: A process by which individuals organize and interpret their sensory impressions in order to give meaning to their environment. (个体组织和解释感觉印象以赋予环境意义的过程。)
* **Processes (过程)**: Actions that individuals, groups, and organizations engage in as a result of inputs and that lead to certain outcomes. (个体、群体和组织由于输入而进行的、并导致某些产出的行动。)
* **Productivity (生产率)**: The combination of the effectiveness and efficiency of an organization. (组织的效力与效率的结合。)
* **Psychology (心理学)**: The science that seeks to measure, explain, and sometimes change the behavior of humans and other animals. (寻求测量、解释并有时改变人类及其他动物行为的科学。)

## S

* **Social psychology (社会心理学)**: An area of psychology that blends concepts from psychology and sociology to focus on the influence of people on one another. (融合了心理学和社会学概念，专注于人与人之间相互影响的心理学领域。)
* **Sociology (社会学)**: The study of people in relation to their social environment or culture. (研究人与其社会环境或文化关系的学科。)
* **Successful Manager (升职型管理者)**: A manager who defines success by the speed of promotion within an organization. (根据在组织内晋升的速度来定义的成功管理者。)
* **Survival (生存)**: The degree to which an organization is able to exist and grow over the long term. (组织能够长期存在并发展的程度。)

## T

* **Task performance (任务绩效)**: The combination of effectiveness and efficiency at doing core job tasks. (完成核心工作任务时效力与效率的结合。)
* **Transformational leadership (变革型领导)**: Leaders who inspire followers to transcend their own self-interests and who are capable of having a profound and extraordinary effect on followers. (激励下属超越自身利益，并能对下属产生深刻而非凡影响的领导者。)

## U

* **Unit of analysis (分析层面)**: The level (individual, group, or organization) at which behavior is studied. (研究行为的层面，如个体、群体或组织系统。)

## W

* **Withdrawal behavior (退缩行为)**: The set of actions employees take to separate themselves from the organization. (员工为使自己脱离组织而采取的一系列行动。)
* **Workforce demographics (劳动力人口特征)**: Statistical characteristics of the workforce population. (劳动力人口的统计特征。)
* **Workforce diversity (劳动力多元化)**: The concept that organizations are becoming more heterogeneous in terms of employees' gender, age, race, ethnicity, sexual orientation, and other characteristics. (组织在性别、年龄、种族、性取向等维度上变得更加异质化的趋势。)
