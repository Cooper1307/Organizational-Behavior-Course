## EXHIBIT 1-3: A Basic OB Model (一个基础的组织行为学模型)

该模型展示了从输入到过程再到产出的三个层次分析：

| 层面 (Level) | Inputs (输入) | Processes (过程) | Outcomes (产出) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Individual Level** (个体层面) | Diversity (多元性), Personality (人格), Values (价值观) | Emotions and moods (情绪与心境), Motivation (动机), Perception (知觉), Decision making (决策) | Attitudes and stress (态度与压力), Task performance (任务绩效), Citizenship behavior (公民行为), Withdrawal behavior (退缩行为) |
| **Group Level** (群体层面) | Group structure (群体结构), Group roles (群体角色), Team responsibilities (团队职责) | Communication (沟通), Leadership (领导力), Power and politics (权力与政治), Conflict and negotiation (冲突与谈判) | Group cohesion (群体凝聚力), Group functioning (群体运行) |
| **Organizational Level** (组织层面) | Structure (结构), Culture (文化) | Human resource management (人力资源管理), Change practices (变革实践) | Productivity (生产率), Survival (生存) |

### 2. Inputs (输入)

Inputs are the variables like personality, group structure, and organizational culture that lead to processes. These variables set the stage for what will occur in an organization later.
输入是诸如人格、群体结构和组织文化等引导至过程的变量。这些变量为组织中随后将发生的事情奠定了基础。

Many are determined in advance of the employment relationship. For example, individual diversity characteristics, personality, and values are shaped by a combination of an individual's genetic inheritance and childhood environment.
许多变量在雇佣关系建立之前就已经确定了。例如，个人的多元化特征、人格和价值观是由个人的遗传继承和童年环境共同塑造的。

Group structure, roles, and team responsibilities are typically assigned immediately before or after a group is formed. Finally, organizational structure and culture are usually the result of years of development and change as the organization adapts to its environment and builds up customs and norms.
群体结构、角色和团队职责通常在群体形成之前或之后立即分配。最后，组织结构和文化通常是多年发展和改造成的结果，因为组织需要适应其环境并建立习俗和规范。

**术语对照 (Terminology):**

* **Inputs**: 输入
* **Variables**: 变量
* **Set the stage**: 奠定基础
* **Genetic inheritance**: 遗传继承
* **Customs and norms**: 习俗与规范

---

### 3. Processes (过程)

If inputs are like the nouns in OB, processes are like verbs. Processes are actions that individuals, groups, and organizations engage in as a result of inputs and that lead to certain outcomes.
如果说输入像组织行为学中的名词，那么过程就像动词。过程是个体、群体和组织由于输入而进行的、并导致某些产出的行动。

At the individual level, processes include emotions and moods, motivation, perception, and decision making. At the group level, they include communication, leadership, power and politics, and conflict and negotiation.
在个体层面，过程包括情绪与心境、动机、知觉以及决策。在群体层面，它们包括沟通、领导力、权力与政治，以及冲突与谈判。

Finally, at the organizational level, processes include HR management and change practices.
最后，在组织层面，过程包括人力资源管理和变革实践。

**术语对照 (Terminology):**

* **Processes**: 过程
* **Engagement**: 参与/进行
* **HR management**: 人力资源管理
* **Change practices**: 变革实践
* **Nouns and Verbs**: 名词与动词 (生动的比喻)
